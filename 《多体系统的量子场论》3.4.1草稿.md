# 《多体系统的量子场论》3.4.1草稿

注意，整个3.4节中，$\varphi$都不是指“所有玻色子”的湮灭算符，而是指“进入超流状态的玻色子”的湮灭算符，而其他玻色子应理解为热粒子。也正是因为这样，问题3.4.1题干中的某些话才能理解。

按如下标准取傅里叶正负号：

$$\varphi_{\omega_n}(\pmb x)=\beta^{-1}\int_0^\beta d\tau\ \varphi(\pmb x,\tau)\exp[-i\tau\omega_n]$$

$$\varphi(\pmb x,\tau)=\sum_n\varphi_{\omega_n}(\pmb x)\exp[i\tau\omega_n]$$

$$\varphi^*(\pmb x,\tau)=\sum_n\varphi_{\omega_n}^*(\pmb x)\exp[-i\tau\omega_n]$$

$$\partial_\tau\varphi(\pmb x,\tau)=\sum_ni\omega_n\varphi_{\omega_n}(\pmb x)\exp[i\tau\omega_n]$$

$$\partial_\tau\varphi^*(\pmb x,\tau)=\sum_n-i\omega_n\varphi_{\omega_n}^*(\pmb x)\exp[-i\tau\omega_n]$$

$$S_E=\int_0^\beta d\tau\ \int d^d\pmb x\ \left[\frac12(\varphi^*\partial_\tau\varphi-\varphi\partial_\tau\varphi^*)\\+\frac{1}{2m}\partial_{\pmb x}\varphi^*\partial_{\pmb x}\varphi-\mu|\varphi|^2+\frac{V_0}{2}|\varphi|^4\right]\\=\int_0^\beta d\tau\ \int d^d\pmb x\ \sum_{n_1,n_2}\left[\frac12i\omega_{n_2}(\varphi_{\omega_{n_1}}^*\varphi_{\omega_{n_2}}\exp[i\tau(\omega_{n_2}-\omega_{n_1})]\\+\varphi_{\omega_{n_1}}\varphi_{\omega_{n_2}}^*\exp[i\tau(\omega_{n_1}-\omega_{n_2})])\\+\frac{1}{2m}\partial_{\pmb x}\varphi_{\omega_{n_1}}^*\partial_{\pmb x}\varphi_{\omega_{n_2}}\exp[i\tau(\omega_{n_2}-\omega_{n_1})]\\-\mu\varphi_{\omega_{n_1}}^*\varphi_{\omega_{n_2}}\exp[i\tau(\omega_{n_2}-\omega_{n_1})]\\+\frac{V_0}{2}\sum_{n_3,n_4}\varphi_{\omega_{n_1}}^*\varphi_{\omega_{n_2}}\varphi_{\omega_{n_3}}^*\varphi_{\omega_{n_4}}\exp[i\tau(\omega_{n_4}-\omega_{n_3}+\omega_{n_2}-\omega_{n_1})]\right]\\=\int d^d\pmb x\ \sum_{n_1}\left[\beta i\omega_{n_1}|\varphi_{\omega_{n_1}}|^2+\frac{1}{2m}\beta\partial_{\pmb x}\varphi_{\omega_{n_1}}^*\partial_{\pmb x}\varphi_{\omega_{n_1}}-\mu\beta|\varphi_{\omega_{n_1}}|^2\\+\frac{V_0\beta}{2}\sum_{n_2,n_3,n_4}\varphi_{\omega_{n_1}}^*\varphi_{\omega_{n_2}}\varphi_{\omega_{n_3}}^*\varphi_{\omega_{n_4}}\delta(\omega_{n_4}-\omega_{n_3}+\omega_{n_2}-\omega_{n_1})\right]$$

关于从(3.4.2)得出后边那一堆结论的过程：（注意，下方很多推导实际参考问题3.3.6.的解答过程）
要判断是否有长程序，实际就是要在$\beta$计算：
$$\langle\exp[i\theta(\pmb x)]\exp[-i\theta(\pmb0)]\rangle=\langle\exp[i\theta(\pmb x)-i\theta(0)]\rangle$$

由于(3.4.2)是二次型，所以类似上一节，可得$\langle\exp[iA]\rangle=\exp[-\langle A^2\rangle/2]$

所以就是要考虑
$$\exp\left[-\frac12\langle[\theta(\pmb x)-\theta(\pmb 0)]^2\rangle\right]=\exp\left[\mathcal G_\theta^\beta(\pmb x)-\mathcal G_\theta^\beta(\pmb0)\right]$$

而
$$\mathcal G_\theta^\beta(\pmb x)=\frac{\int\mathcal D\theta\ \theta(\pmb x)\theta(\pmb 0)\exp\left[\int d^d\pmb x\ \frac{\eta}{2}\theta\partial_{\pmb x}^2\theta\right]}{\int\mathcal D\theta\ \exp\left[\int d^d\pmb x\ \frac{\eta}{2}\theta\partial_{\pmb x}^2\theta\right]}=-\frac{\eta^{-1}}{\partial_{\pmb x}^2}$$

故$$\mathcal G_\theta^\beta(\pmb k)=\frac{\eta^{-1}}{|\pmb k|^2}$$

故
$$\mathcal G_{\theta}^\beta(\pmb x)=\frac{1}{\eta(2\pi)^d}\int d^d\pmb k\frac{\exp[i\pmb k\pmb x]}{|\pmb k|^2}\\=\frac{1}{\eta(2\pi)^d|\pmb x|^{d-2}}\int d^d\pmb u\ \frac{\exp[i\hat{\pmb n}\pmb u]}{|\pmb u|^2}\propto\frac{1}{\eta(2\pi)^d}|\pmb x|^{2-d}$$

故$$\mathcal G_\theta^\beta(\pmb x)-\mathcal G_\theta^\beta(\pmb 0)\propto\frac{1}{\eta(2\pi)^d}\left(|\pmb x|^{2-d}-l^{2-d}\right)$$

注意在$d<2$，那个重尺度得到的积分是负的。
