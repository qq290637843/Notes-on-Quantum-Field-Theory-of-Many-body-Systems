# 《多体系统的量子场论》6.3.2草稿

## 问题 6.3.3.
1.
首先，虚时拉格朗日密度为
$$\frac{1}{2g^2}(\pmb e^2+b^2)$$

设$V^\mu=(b,e_1,e_2)$，故虚时作用量为
$$\int d^3z\ \frac{1}{2g^2}|V|^2$$

根据正文可知：$V^\mu=\frac{1}{2q}\frac{z^\mu}{|z|^3}$，故
位于$x^\mu$的瞬子场为$\frac{1}{2q}\frac{z-x}{|z-x|^3}$，位于$y^\mu$的瞬子场为$-\frac{1}{2q}\frac{z-y}{|z-y|^3}$

注意$V_{\rm tot}=V_{\rm inst}+V_{\rm anti}$，故
$$S_{\rm tot}=\int d^3z\ \frac{1}{2g^2}|V_{\rm inst}+V_{\rm anti}|^2=S_{\rm inst}+S_{\rm anti}+S_{\rm int}$$

参考3.4.2节的内容可知，两个瞬子自能只需要考虑核部分，可以记为常数，真正重要的只有相互作用部分，即
$$S_{\rm int}=\frac{1}{g^2}\int d^3z\ V_{\rm inst}(z)\cdot V_{\rm anti}(z)$$

注意
$$V_{\rm inst}(z)=-\frac{1}{2q}\nabla_z\left(\frac{1}{|z-x|}\right)$$

故
$$S_{\rm int}=\frac1{g^2}\int d^3z\ \left[-\frac{1}{2q}\nabla_z\left(\frac{1}{|z-x|}\right)\right]\cdot V_{\rm anti}(z)\\=\frac{1}{2qg^2}\int d^3z\ \frac{1}{|z-x|}\nabla_z\cdot V_{\rm anti}(z)\\=\frac{1}{2qg^2}\int d^3z\ \frac{1}{|z-x|}(-1)\frac{2\pi}{q}\delta^{(3)}(z-y)\\=-\frac{\pi}{g^2q^2}\frac{1}{|x-y|}$$

从而
$$Z(x,y)=Z_0\exp[-2S_c]\exp\left[\frac{\pi}{g^2q^2}\frac{1}{|x-y|}\right]$$

2.
参照3.4.2节的推导，可得
$$Z=Z_0\sum_{n}\frac{1}{n!n!}\int\prod_{u=1}^{2n}d^3z_u\ \exp[-2nS_c]\exp\left[-\sum_{u<v}^{2n}\frac{s_us_v\pi}{g^2q^2}\frac{1}{|z_u-z_v|}\right]$$

3.
$$Z(x,y)=\int \mathcal D\theta\ \exp[i\theta(x)]\exp[-i\theta(y)]\exp\left[-\int d^3z\ \frac{\chi}{2}(\partial\theta)^2\right]$$

设
$$J(z)=i\delta^{(3)}(z-x)-i\delta^{(3)}(z-y)$$

那么
$$\exp[i\theta(x)-i\theta(y)]=\exp\left[\int d^3z\ J(z)\theta(z)\right]$$

所以
$$Z(x,y)=\int \mathcal D\theta\ \exp\left[\int d^3z\ \frac{\chi}{2}\theta\partial^2\theta+\int d^3z\ J(z)\theta(z)\right]$$

故
$$Z(x,y)=\exp\left[\frac12\int d^3z\ d^3z'\ J(z)\frac{1}{4\pi\chi|z-z'|}J(z')\right]\\=\exp\left[\frac12\left(-G(x,x)-G(y,y)+G(x,y)+G(y,x)\right)\right]\\=\exp[-G(0)]\exp\left[\frac{1}{4\pi\chi|x-y|}\right]$$

所以$$\frac{1}{4\pi\chi}=\frac{\pi}{g^2q^2}$$

故
$$\chi=\frac{g^2q^2}{4\pi^2}$$

4.
当XY模型带上$K\cos(\theta)$，于是
$$Z_{XY}=\int\mathcal D\theta\ \exp\left[-\int d^3z\frac{\chi}{2}(\partial\theta)^2+\int d^3z\ K\cos(\theta)\right]$$

而
$$\exp\left[\int d^3z\ \frac K2(\exp[i\theta]+\exp[-i\theta])\right]\\=\sum_{N_+=0}^\infty\sum_{N_-=0}^\infty\frac{(K/2)^{N_++N_-}}{N_+!N_-!}\int\left(\prod_{u=1}^{N_+}d^3x_u\prod_{v=1}^{N_-}d^3y_v\right)\exp\left[i\sum_u\theta(x_u)-i\sum_v\theta(y_v)\right]$$

执行路径积分的时候，只有$N_+=N_-$的部分会保留，因为不满足的情况会抵消掉，从而
$$Z_{XY}=Z_0\sum_{n=0}^\infty\frac{(K/2)^{2n}}{n!n!}\int\prod_{u=1}^{2n}d^3z_u\ \exp\left[-nG(0)-\sum_{u<v}^{2n}s_us_v\frac{1}{4\pi\chi|z_u-z_v|}\right]\\=Z_0\sum_{n=0}^\infty\frac{\left(\frac K2\exp[-G(0)/2]\right)^{2n}}{n!n!}\int\prod_{u=1}^{2n}d^3z_u\ \exp\left[-\sum_{u<v}^{2n}s_us_v\frac{1}{4\pi\chi}\frac{1}{|z_u-z_v|}\right]$$
