# 《多体系统的量子场论》5.1.5草稿

## 问题 5.1.7.
$$\mathcal G_C(\tau)=\exp\left[\frac12\int_0^\tau d\tau_1\ d\tau_2\int dx_1\ dx_2\ V(x_1)V(x_2)\langle T_\tau\rho(\tau_1,x_1)\rho(\tau_2,x_2)\rangle\right]\\=\exp\left[\frac12\int_0^\tau d\tau_1\ d\tau_2\ \int dx_1\ dx_2\ V(x_1)V(x_2)\frac{N^2(E_F)}{4}\times\\\left(\frac{v^2}{(v\tau_1-v\tau_2+ix_1-ix_2)^2}+\frac{v^2}{(v\tau_1-v\tau_2-ix_1+ix_2)^2}\right)\\+\frac12\int_0^\tau d\tau_1\ d\tau_2\ \int dx_1\ dx_2\ V(x_1)V(x_2)\frac{N^2(E_F)\cos(2k_F(x_1-x_2))}{2}\frac{v^2}{v^2(\tau_1-\tau_2)^2+(x_1-x_2)^2}\right]$$

而$V(x)\propto |x|^{-\gamma}$，所以$V_q\propto|q|^{\gamma-1}$，注意$0<\gamma<1$，所以$V_{2k_F}$相较于$k$小的$V_{k}$可以忽略不计，所以实际上只需要保留第一项。
这里记
$$V_\gamma=2V\Gamma[1-\gamma]\sin[\gamma\pi/2]$$

于是
$$V_k=V_\gamma|k|^{\gamma-1}$$

转到动量空间得
$$\mathcal G_C(\tau)=\exp\left[\frac12\int_0^\tau\ d\tau_1\ d\tau_2\ \int\frac{dk}{2\pi}V_\gamma^2|k|^{2\gamma-2}\mathcal G_\rho(\tau_1-\tau_2,k)\right]\\=\exp\left[\frac12\int_0^\tau d\tau_1\ d\tau_2\ \int\frac{dk}{2\pi}V_\gamma^2|k|^{2\gamma-2}\frac{\rho_0}{2mv}|k|\exp[-v|k||\tau_1-\tau_2|]\right]\\=\exp\left[\int_0^\tau d\tau'\ (\tau-\tau')\int\frac{dk}{2\pi}V_\gamma^2|k|^{2\gamma-1}\frac{\rho_0}{2mv}\exp[-v|k|\tau']\right]\\=\exp\left[\int\frac{dk}{2\pi}V_\gamma^2|k|^{2\gamma-3}\frac{\rho_0}{2mv^3}\left(v|k|\tau-1+\exp[-v|k|\tau]\right)\right]\\=\exp\left[\frac{\rho_0V_\gamma^2}{4\pi mv^3}\int dk\ |k|^{2\gamma-3}(v|k|\tau-1+\exp[-v|k|\tau])\right]\\=\begin{cases}\exp\left[\frac{\rho_0V_\gamma^2\Gamma(2\gamma-2)}{2\pi mv^{1+2\gamma}}\tau^{2-2\gamma}\right]\exp\left[\tau\frac{\rho_0V_\gamma^2}{4\pi mv^2}\int dk\ |k|^{2\gamma-2}\right],\frac12<\gamma<1\\\exp\left[\frac{\rho_0V_\gamma^2\Gamma(2\gamma-2)}{2\pi mv^{1+2\gamma}}\tau^{2-2\gamma}\right],0<\gamma<\frac12\end{cases}$$

对$\frac12<\gamma<1$的情况，把右边部分吸收到$\exp[E_C\tau]$中去，于是
$$\mathcal G_C(\tau)=\exp\left[\frac{\rho_0V_\gamma^2\Gamma(2\gamma-2)}{2\pi mv^{1+2\gamma}}\tau^{2-2\gamma}\right]\exp[E_C\tau]$$

从而当$t>0$，有
$$iG_I(t)=-iG_c(t)iG_C(-t)=i\frac{1}{vt}\exp\left[\frac{\rho_0V_\gamma^2\Gamma(2\gamma-2)}{2\pi mv^{1+2\gamma}}t^{2-2\gamma}\exp[-i(1-\gamma)\pi]\right]\exp[-iE_Ct]$$

考虑记$$-\lambda:=\frac{\rho_0V_\gamma^2\Gamma(2\gamma-2)}{2\pi mv^{1+2\gamma}}\exp[-i(1-\gamma)\pi]$$

于是可知$\lambda$的实部是正的。

从而
$$A_{em}(\omega)=-\frac1\pi{\rm Im}\int_{0}^\infty dt\ \exp[i\omega t]G_I(t)\\=-\frac{1}{\pi}{\rm Im}\int_{t_l}^\infty dt\ \exp[i(\omega-E_C)t]\frac{1}{vt}\exp\left[-\lambda t^{2-2\gamma}\right]\\=\frac{1}{\pi}\int_{t_l}^\infty dt\ \frac{1}{vt}\exp[-\lambda_Rt^{2-2\gamma}]\sin[\lambda_It^{2-2\gamma}+(E_C-\omega)t]$$

## 问题 5.1.8.
（注意，只要过程中出现能被$E_C$吸收的部分，我会不说明直接吸收掉）
$$\mathcal G_C(\tau)=\exp\left[\frac{V_C^2}{2}\int_0^\tau d\tau_1 d\tau_2\ \langle T_\tau\rho(\tau_1,0)\rho(\tau_2,0)\rangle\right]\exp[E_C\tau]\\=\exp\left[\frac{V_C^2}{2}\int_0^\tau d\tau_1\ d\tau_2\ \frac{\chi v/2\pi}{v^2(\tau_1-\tau_2)^2}\\+\frac{V_C^2}{2}\int_0^\tau d\tau_1\ d\tau_2\sum_n|C_n|^2\left(\frac{l^2}{v^2(\tau_1-\tau_2)^2}\right)^{n^2\pi\chi v}\right]\exp[E_C\tau]\\=\exp\left[V_C^2\int_{l/v}^\tau d\tau'\ (\tau-\tau')\frac{\chi v/2\pi}{v^2\tau'^2}\\+V_C^2\sum_n|C_n|^2\int_{l/v}^\tau d\tau'\ (\tau-\tau')\left(\frac{l^2}{v^2\tau'^2}\right)^{n^2\pi\chi v}\right]\exp[E_C\tau]\\=\exp\left[\frac{V_C^2\chi}{2\pi v}\log[l/\tau v]-\sum_n\frac{|C_n|^2V_C^2l^{2n^2\pi\chi v}}{v^{2n^2\pi\chi v}}\int_{l/v}^\tau d\tau'\ \tau'^{1-2n^2\pi\chi v}\right]\exp[E_C\tau]$$

所以$n^2\pi\chi v>2$时，后者随$\tau$增大趋于常数，如果$n^2\pi\chi v<2$，那么后者随$\tau$增大趋于负无穷。重点在于$n=1$，所以当$\pi\chi v>1$，有$\mathcal G_C(\tau)\sim\tau^{-V_C^2\chi/2\pi v}\exp[E_C\tau]$，当$\pi\chi v<1$，有$\mathcal G_C(\tau)\sim\exp[-{\rm const}\cdot(\tau/\tau_0)^{2-2\pi\chi v}]\exp[E_C\tau]$
