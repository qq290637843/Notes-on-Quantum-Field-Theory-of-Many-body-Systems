# 《多体系统的量子场论》5.3.3草稿

## 问题 5.3.3.
$$H=E_g+\int d^2\pmb x\ \frac{d^2\pmb k}{(2\pi)^2}\xi_{\pmb k}\delta n_{\pmb k}+\frac12\int d^2\pmb x\ \frac{d^2\pmb k}{(2\pi)^2}\frac{d^2\pmb k'}{(2\pi)^2}f(\pmb k,\pmb k')\delta n_{\pmb k}\delta n_{\pmb k'}$$

注意$f(\pmb k,\pmb k')$只依赖于费米面上的角度，即$f(\theta,\theta')$，而动量积分$\frac{d^2\pmb k}{(2\pi)^2}$分解为$\frac{kdk\ d\theta}{(2\pi)^2}$

于是
$$H_{int}=\frac12\int d^2\pmb x\ d\theta\ d\theta'\ f(\theta,\theta')\left[\int\frac{kdk}{(2\pi)^2}\delta n_{\pmb k}\right]\left[\int\frac{k'dk'}{(2\pi)^2}\delta n_{\pmb k'}\right]\\=\frac{1}{2}\int d^2\pmb x\ d\theta\ d\theta'\ f(\theta,\theta')\tilde\rho(\theta,\pmb x)\tilde\rho(\theta',\pmb x)$$

$$H_{kin}=\int d^2\pmb x\ d\theta\ \left[\int_{k_F}^{k_F+h(\pmb x,\theta)}\frac{kdk}{(2\pi)^2}\xi_{\pmb k}\right]\\\simeq\int d^2\pmb x\ d\theta\ \left[\int_{k_F}^{k_F+h(\pmb x,\theta)}\frac{kdk}{(2\pi)^2}\pmb v_F^*(\theta)\cdot\hat{\pmb k}(k-k_F(\theta))\right]\\\simeq\frac12\int d^2\pmb x\ d\theta\ h^2(\pmb x,\theta)\frac{k_F(\theta)}{(2\pi)^2}\pmb v_F^*(\theta)\cdot\hat{\pmb k}\\=\int d^2\pmb x\ d\theta\ \frac{2\pi^2\pmb v_F^*(\theta)\cdot\hat{\pmb k}}{k_F(\theta)}\tilde\rho^2(\theta,\pmb x)$$

接着处理方程，首先方程一开始为
$$\partial_tn_{\pmb k}+\partial_{\pmb x}n_{\pmb k}\cdot\partial_{\pmb k}\tilde\epsilon-\partial_{\pmb k}n_{\pmb k}\cdot\partial_{\pmb x}\tilde\epsilon=0$$

同时执行$\int\frac{kdk}{(2\pi)^2}$。

$$\int\frac{kdk}{(2\pi)^2}\partial_tn_{\pmb k}=\partial_t\tilde\rho(\theta,\pmb x)$$

$$\int\frac{kdk}{(2\pi)^2}\partial_{\pmb x}n_{\pmb k}\cdot\partial_{\pmb k}\tilde\epsilon=\partial_{\pmb x}\tilde\rho(\theta,\pmb x)\cdot\pmb v_F^*(\theta)$$

$$-\int\frac{kdk}{(2\pi)^2}\partial_{\pmb k}n_{\pmb k}\cdot\partial_{\pmb x}\tilde\epsilon\\=-\int\frac{kdk}{(2\pi)^2}(\partial_{\pmb k}n_{0,\pmb k}+\partial_{\pmb k}\delta n_{\pmb k})\cdot\int\frac{d^2\pmb k'}{(2\pi)^2}f(\pmb k,\pmb k')\partial_{\pmb x}\delta n_{\pmb k'}\\\simeq-\int\frac{kdk}{(2\pi)^2}\frac{d^2\pmb k'}{(2\pi)^2}f(\pmb k,\pmb k')\partial_{\pmb k}n_{0,\pmb k}\cdot\partial_{\pmb x}\delta n_{\pmb k'}\\=\int\frac{kdk}{(2\pi)^2}\frac{d^2\pmb k'}{(2\pi)^2}f(\pmb k,\pmb k')\delta(k-k_F)\frac{{\pmb v}^*(\pmb k)}{\pmb v^*(\pmb k)\cdot\hat{\pmb k}}\cdot\partial_{\pmb x}\delta n_{\pmb k'}\\=\frac{k_F(\theta)}{(2\pi)^2}\frac{\pmb v_F^*(\theta)}{\pmb v_F^*(\theta)\cdot\hat{\pmb k}}\cdot\int d\theta'\ f(\theta,\theta')\partial_{\pmb x}\tilde\rho(\theta',\pmb x)$$

所以方程为
$$\partial_t\tilde\rho+\pmb v_F^*(\theta)\cdot\partial_{\pmb x}\tilde\rho+\frac{k_F(\theta)}{(2\pi)^2}\frac{\pmb v_F^*(\theta)}{\pmb v_F^*(\theta)\cdot\hat{\pmb k}}\cdot\int d\theta'\ f(\theta,\theta')\partial_{\pmb x}\tilde\rho=0$$

## 问题 5.3.4.
从方程入手，一开始方程为
$$\partial_tn_k+\partial_xn_k\partial_k\tilde\epsilon-\partial_kn_k\partial_x\tilde\epsilon=0$$

设$$\tilde\rho^+=\int_0^{+\infty}\frac{dk}{2\pi}\delta n_{k},\tilde\rho^-=\int_0^{+\infty}\frac{dk}{2\pi}\delta n_{-k}$$

将$\int_0^{+\infty}\frac{dk}{2\pi}$作用在方程上得
$$\partial_t\tilde\rho^++v_F\partial_x\tilde\rho^++\frac1{2\pi}f^+\partial_x\tilde\rho^++\frac1{2\pi}f^-\partial_x\tilde\rho^-=0$$

另一边则为
$$\partial_t\tilde\rho^--v_F\partial_x\tilde\rho^--\frac{1}{2\pi}f^-\partial_x\tilde\rho^+-\frac{1}{2\pi}f^+\partial_x\tilde\rho^-=0$$

设$$\tilde\rho^+=\frac{1}{2\pi}\partial_x\tilde\varphi^+,\tilde\rho^-=-\frac1{2\pi}\partial_x\tilde\varphi^-$$

于是方程为
$$\partial_t\partial_x\tilde\varphi^++v_F\partial_x^2\tilde\varphi^++\frac{1}{2\pi}f^+\partial_x^2\tilde\varphi^+-\frac{1}{2\pi}f^-\partial_x^2\tilde\varphi^-=0$$

$$\partial_t\partial_x\tilde\varphi^--v_F\partial_x^2\tilde\varphi^--\frac{1}{2\pi}f^+\partial_x^2\tilde\varphi^-+\frac{1}{2\pi}f^-\partial_x^2\tilde\varphi^+=0$$

故
$$L=\int dx\left(-\frac1{4\pi}\partial_x\tilde\varphi^+\partial_t\tilde\varphi^++\frac1{4\pi}\partial_x\tilde\varphi^-\partial_t\tilde\varphi^-\\-\frac{v_F}{4\pi}[(\partial_x\tilde\varphi^+)^2+(\partial_x\tilde\varphi^-)^2]-\frac{f^+}{8\pi^2}[(\partial_x\tilde\varphi^+)^2+(\partial_x\tilde\varphi^-)^2]\\+\frac{f^-}{4\pi^2}\partial_x\tilde\varphi^+\partial_x\tilde\varphi^-\right)$$

(b)
$$L=\int dx\ \left(\frac{m}{8\pi^2\rho_0}\partial_t\phi\partial_t\phi-\frac{1}{8\pi^2\chi}\partial_x\phi\partial_x\phi\right)$$

于是正则动量为
$$\tilde\phi=\frac{m}{4\pi^2\rho_0}\dot\phi$$

从而
$$\dot\phi=\frac{4\pi^2\rho_0}{m}\tilde\phi$$

所以
$$H=\int dx\ \left(\frac{2\pi^2\rho_0}{m}\tilde\phi^2+\frac{1}{8\pi^2\chi}(\partial_x\phi)^2\right)$$

现在设$$\phi=\tilde\varphi^+-\tilde\varphi^-,\theta=\tilde\varphi^++\tilde\varphi^-$$

于是
$$L_a=\int dx\ \left(-\frac{1}{4\pi}\partial_x\theta\partial_t\phi-\frac{v_F}{8\pi}[(\partial_x\phi)^2+(\partial_x\theta)^2]\\-\frac{f^+}{16\pi^2}[(\partial_x\phi)^2+(\partial_x\theta)^2]+\frac{f^-}{16\pi^2}[(\partial_x\theta)^2-(\partial_x\phi)^2]\right)$$

于是在$L_a$中$\phi$的正则动量为$-\frac{1}{4\pi}\partial_x\theta$，将其替换为$\tilde\phi$得

故
$$H_a=\int dx\ \left(\frac{v_F}{8\pi}[(\partial_x\phi)^2+16\pi^2\tilde\phi^2]+\frac{f^+}{16\pi^2}[(\partial_x\phi)^2+16\pi^2\tilde\phi^2]\\-\frac{f^-}{16\pi^2}[16\pi^2\tilde\phi^2-(\partial_x\phi)^2]\right)\\=\int dx\ \left((2\pi v_F+f^+-f^-)\tilde\phi^2+\frac{2\pi v_F+f^++f^-}{16\pi^2}(\partial_x\phi)^2\right)$$

故
$$\frac{2\pi^2\rho_0}{m}=2\pi v_F+f^+-f^-\\\frac{1}{8\pi^2\chi}=\frac{2\pi v_F+f^+-f^-}{16\pi^2}$$
