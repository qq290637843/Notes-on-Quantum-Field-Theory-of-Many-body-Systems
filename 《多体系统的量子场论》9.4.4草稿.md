# 《多体系统的量子场论》9.4.4草稿

## 问题 9.4.1.
首先我们知道
$$G_y(\pmb i)=\Theta(\pmb i)\tau^0$$，而变换规则是
$$G_U(\pmb i)\to W(\pmb i)G_U(\pmb i)W^\dagger(U(\pmb i))$$

所以我们的目的是找到合适的$W(\pmb i)$满足
$$W(\pmb i)\Theta(\pmb i)\tau^0W^\dagger(\pmb i-\pmb y)=\tau^0,W(\pmb i)=\pm\tau^0$$

故
$$W(\pmb i)=\Theta(\pmb i)W(\pmb i-\pmb y)$$

显然这就是一个递推式，而且总是能满足，只要确定$i_x=0$那条线上$W(\pmb i)$的值（比如直接取$\tau^0$），然后向$+y,-y$方向递推出别处的值就行

## 问题 9.4.2.
(a)
$$G_x(\pmb i)=\tau^0,G_y(\pmb i)=\tau^0\\G_{P_x}(\pmb i)=\eta_{xpx}^{i_x}\eta_{xpy}^{i_y}g_{px},G_{P_y}(\pmb i)=\eta_{xpy}^{i_x}\eta_{xpx}^{i_y}g_{py}\\G_{P_{xy}}(\pmb i)=g_{pxy},G_T(\pmb i)=\eta_t^{\pmb i}g_t$$

而$Z2Azz13$就是$Z2A(\tau_-^3)(\tau_-^3)(\tau^1)(\tau_+^3)$，故
$$g_{px}=i\tau^3,\eta_{xpx}=-1,g_{py}=i\tau^3,\eta_{xpy}=-1,g_{pxy}=i\tau^1,g_t=i\tau^3,\eta_t=1$$

故
$$G_x(\pmb i)=\tau^0,G_y(\pmb i)=\tau^0\\G_{P_x}(\pmb i)=(-)^{i_x}(-)^{i_y}i\tau^3,G_{P_y}(\pmb i)=(-)^{i_x}(-)^{i_y}i\tau^3\\G_{P_{xy}}(\pmb i)=i\tau^1,G_T(\pmb i)=i\tau^3$$

(b)
首先根据$G_x(\pmb i)=\tau^0,G_y(\pmb i)=\tau^0$可知
$$u_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb m}=u_{\pmb m}=iu_{\pmb m}^0\tau^0+u_{\pmb m}^1\tau^1+u_{\pmb m}^2\tau^2+u_{\pmb m}^3\tau^3$$

根据$G_T(\pmb i)=i\tau^3$可知
$$(i\tau^3)(-u_{\pmb m})(-i\tau^3)=u_{\pmb m}$$

故
$$u_{\pmb m}^0=u_{\pmb m}^3=0$$

根据$G_{P_{xy}}(\pmb i)=i\tau^1$可知
$$(i\tau^1)(u_{P_{xy}\pmb m}^1\tau^1+u_{P_{xy}\pmb m}^2\tau^2)(-i\tau^1)=u_{\pmb m}^1\tau^1+u_{\pmb m}^2\tau^2$$

故
$$u_{P_{xy}\pmb m}^1=u_{\pmb m}^1,u_{P_{xy}\pmb m}^2=-u_{\pmb m}^2$$

根据$G_{P_x}(\pmb i)=(-)^{i_x+i_y}i\tau^3$可知
$$(-)^{i_x+i_y}i\tau^3u_{P_x\pmb m}(-)^{i_x+m_x+i_y+m_y}(-i\tau^3)=u_{\pmb m}$$

故
$$u_{P_x\pmb m}=(-)^{m_x+m_y+1}u_{\pmb m}$$

根据$G_{P_y}(\pmb i)=(-)^{i_x+i_y}i\tau^3$可知
$$(-)^{i_x+i_y}i\tau^3u_{P_y\pmb m}(-)^{i_x+m_x+i_y+m_y}(-i\tau^3)=u_{\pmb m}$$

故
$$u_{P_y\pmb m}=(-)^{m_x+m_y+1}u_{\pmb m}$$

总上得到
$$\begin{cases}u_{\pmb m}=u_{\pmb m}^1\tau^1+u_{\pmb m}^2\tau^2\\u_{P_{xy}\pmb m}^1=u_{\pmb m}^1,u_{P_{xy}\pmb m}^2=-u_{\pmb m}^2\\u_{P_x{\pmb m}}=(-)^{m_x+m_y+1}u_{\pmb m}\\u_{P_y\pmb m}=(-)^{m_x+m_y+1}u_{\pmb m}\end{cases}$$

## 问题 9.4.3.
(9.4.3)为
$$G_U(\pmb i)\to W(\pmb i)G_U(\pmb i)W^\dagger(U(\pmb i))$$

注意$W(\pmb i)=(i\tau^1)^{\pmb i},W_{\pmb i}^\dagger=(-i\tau^1)^{\pmb i}$

注意(9.4.19)为
$$G_x=g_3(\theta_x)i\tau^1,G_y=g_3(\theta_y)i\tau^1\\G_{P_x}=(-)^{i_x}g_3(\theta_{px}),G_{P_y}=(-)^{i_y}g_3(\theta_{py})\\G_{P_{xy}}=g_3(\theta_{pxy})i\tau^1,G_T=(-)^{\pmb i}g_3(\theta_t)$$

注意
$$(i\tau^1)^{u}g_3(\theta)(-i\tau^1)^{u}=g_3((-)^u\theta)$$

从而
$$G_{P_x}'(\pmb i)=(i\tau^1)^{i_x+i_y}(-)^{i_x}g_3(\theta_{px})(-i\tau^1)^{-i_x+i_y}\\=(i\tau^1)^{i_x+i_y}(-)^{i_x}g_3(\theta_{px})(-i\tau^1)^{i_x+i_y}((-i\tau^1)^2)^{i_x}\\=g_3((-)^{i_x+i_y}\theta_{px})$$

$$G_{P_y}'(\pmb i)=(i\tau^1)^{i_x+i_y}(-)^{i_y}g_3(\theta_{py})(-i\tau^1)^{i_x-i_y}\\=(i\tau^1)^{i_x+i_y}(-)^{i_y}g_3(\theta_{py})(-i\tau^1)^{i_x+i_y}((-i\tau^1)^2)^{i_y}\\=g_3((-)^{i_x+i_y}\theta_{py})$$

$$G_{P_{xy}}'(\pmb i)=(i\tau^1)^{i_x+i_y}g_3(\theta_{pxy})i\tau^1(-i\tau^1)^{i_x+i_y}\\=g_3((-)^{i_x+i_y}\theta_{pxy})i\tau^1$$

$$G_T'(\pmb i)=(i\tau^1)^{i_x+i_y}(-)^{i_x+i_y}g_3(\theta_t)(-i\tau^1)^{i_x+i_y}\\=(-)^{i_x+i_y}g_3((-)^{i_x+i_y}\theta_t)$$

$$G_x'(\pmb i)=(i\tau^1)^{i_x+i_y}g_3(\theta_x)i\tau^1(-i\tau^1)^{i_x-1+i_y}\\=g_3((-)^{i_x+i_y}\theta_x)(i\tau^1)^2=-g_3((-)^{i_x+i_y}\theta_x)$$

$$G_y'(\pmb i)=(i\tau^1)^{i_x+i_y}g_3(\theta_y)i\tau^1(-i\tau^1)^{i_x+i_y-1}\\=g_3((-)^{i_x+i_y}\theta_y)(i\tau^1)^2=-g_3((-)^{i_x+i_y}\theta_y)$$

注意$G(\pmb i)$的使用方式决定了其乘上一个整体相位结果是一样的，所以最后关于平移的补偿$G$并没有负号的问题。
