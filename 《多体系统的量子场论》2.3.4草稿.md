## 问题2.3.4.
$$iz^\dagger dz=i\exp[i\phi]\cos[\theta/2] (-i\exp[-i\phi]\cos[\theta/2]d\phi-\exp[-i\phi]\sin[\theta/2]d\theta/2)\\+i\sin[\theta/2]\cos[\theta/2]d\theta/2=\cos^2[\theta/2]d\phi$$

故沿着题干中走一圈的总积分量为（注意只需要保留到二阶）：
$$\cos^2[(\theta+\delta\theta)/2]\delta\phi-\cos^2[\theta/2]\delta\phi=-\sin[\theta]\delta\theta\delta\phi/2=-\delta\Omega/2$$

显然，积分式结构决定了其沿着同一路径按相反方向积分的结果互相抵消，故可以拼接。

## 问题2.3.5.
1.
$$\int dt\ [2Siz^\dagger\dot z]=\int\ dt\ \left[2S\cos^2[\theta/2]\dot\phi\right]$$

故$A_\phi=2S\cos^2[\theta/2],A_\theta=0$
2.
这里用$\hat{\pmb\theta}$和$\hat{\pmb\phi}$表示$\theta$和$\phi$增大方向的单位向量。于是矢势为（这里默认电荷量为$1$）$$\pmb A|_{r=1}=A_\theta\hat{\pmb\theta}+A_\phi\hat{\pmb\phi}/\sin[\theta]=S\cot[\theta/2]\hat{\pmb\phi}$$，将其补充到$r\neq1$的区域，让其为
$$\pmb A=\frac Sr\cot[\theta/2]\hat{\pmb\phi}$$

于是对应的磁场为
$$(\pmb\nabla\times\pmb A)_r=\frac1{r\sin\theta}\partial_\theta(S\cot[\theta/2]\sin[\theta]/r)=-S/r^2$$

$$(\pmb\nabla\times\pmb A)_\theta=\frac1r\partial_r(rS\cot[\theta/2]/r)=0$$

$$(\pmb\nabla\times\pmb A)_\phi=0$$

故实际磁场就是$-S\pmb n$。所以这意味着总磁通量为$-4\pi S$，再注意到“一个磁通量量子”是指$2\pi$（默认电荷量为$1$），总共有$-2S$个磁通量量子。
3.
随着$m\to0$，粒子的作用量和自旋为$N_\phi/2$的角动量系统一致，从而态总数就是$2S+1=N_\phi+1$，而根据题干所说，在$m\to0$时，粒子所有态都在最低朗道能级，故答案就是$N_\phi+1$。

## 问题2.3.6.
本题默认没有边界

1.
$$S[(\pmb n_j(t))_j]=\int dt\left[i\sum_j2Sz_j^\dagger z_j-S^2\sum_jJ\pmb n_j\cdot\pmb n_{j+1}\right]$$

2.
$$\delta S[...]=\int dt\ \left[S\sum_j(\pmb n_j\times\dot{\pmb n}_j)\cdot\delta\pmb n_j-S^2J\sum_j\pmb n_{j-1}\cdot\delta\pmb n_j-S^2J\sum_j\pmb n_{j+1}\cdot\delta\pmb n_j\right]$$

故运动方程为
$$\pmb n_j\times\dot{\pmb n}_j=SJ(\pmb n_{j-1}+\pmb n_{j+1})-SJ\pmb n_j\pmb n_j\cdot(\pmb n_{j-1}+\pmb n_{j+1})$$

也可写为
$$\dot{\pmb n}_j=SJ(\pmb n_{j-1}+\pmb n_{j+1})\times\pmb n_j$$

也可写为
$$\dot{\pmb S}_j=J(\pmb S_{j-1}+\pmb S_{j+1})\times\pmb S_j$$

考虑$\pmb S_j\simeq(s_j^x,s_j^y,S)$，且$s_j^x,s_j^y\ll S$，从而忽略二阶小量之后得到方程：
$$\dot s_j^x=JS(s_{j-1}^y+s_{j+1}^y-2s_j^y),\dot s_j^y=-JS(s_{j-1}^x+s_{j+1}^x-2s_j^x)$$

考虑设$s_j^+=s_j^x+is_j^y$，于是得
$$\dot s_j^+=-iJS(s_{j-1}^++s_{j+1}^+-2s_j^+)$$

接着考虑傅里叶：
$$\tilde s_k(\omega)=\sum_j\int dt\ s_j^+(t)\exp[i\omega t-ikj]$$

故
$$\sum_j\int dt\ \dot s_j^+(t)\exp[i\omega t-ikj]=-i\omega\sum_j\int dt\ s_j^+(t)\exp[i\omega t-ikj]$$

$$\sum_j\int dt\ s_{j+1}^+(t)\exp[i\omega t-ikj]=\exp[ik]\sum_j\int dt\ s_j^+(t)\exp[i\omega t-ikj]$$

$$\sum_j\int dt\ s_{j-1}^+(t)\exp[i\omega t-ikj]=\exp[-ik]\sum_j\int dt\ s_j^+(t)\exp[i\omega t-ikj]$$

故
$$(-i\omega+iJS\exp[-ik]+iJS\exp[ik]-2iJS)\tilde s_k(\omega)=0$$

故
$$\omega=JS(\exp[-ik]+\exp[ik]-2)=-2JS(1-\cos k)=2|J|S(1-\cos k)$$

当$k$小，可知$$\omega\simeq|J|Sk^2$$

3.
$$\dot{\pmb S}_j=J(\pmb S_{j-1}+\pmb S_{j+1})\times\pmb S_j$$

考虑$\pmb S_j\simeq(s_j^x,s_j^y,(-)^jS)$，且$s_j^x,s_j^y\ll S$，从而忽略二阶小量之后得到方程：
$$\dot s_j^x=(-)^jJS(s_{j-1}^y+s_{j+1}^y+2s_j^y),\dot s_j^y=-(-)^jJS(s_{j-1}^x+s_{j+1}^x+2s_j^x)$$

考虑设$s_j^+=s_j^x+is_j^y$，于是得
$$(-)^j\dot s_j^+=-iJS(s_{j-1}^++s_{j+1}^++2s_j^+)$$

接着考虑傅里叶：
$$\tilde s_k(\omega)=\sum_j\int dt\ s_j^+(t)\exp[i\omega t-ikj]$$

故
$$\sum_j\int dt\ (-)^j\dot s_j^+(t)\exp[i\omega t-ikj]=-i\omega\sum_j\int dt\ s_j^+(t)\exp[i\omega t-i(k+\pi)j]$$

故
$$-i\omega\tilde s_{k+\pi}(\omega)+(iJS\exp[-ik]+iJS\exp[ik]+2iJS)\tilde s_k(\omega)=0$$

$$-i\omega\tilde s_{k+2\pi}(\omega)+(-iJS\exp[-ik]-iJS\exp[ik]+2iJS)\tilde s_{k+\pi}(\omega)=0$$

再注意到$\tilde s_{k+2\pi}(\omega)=\tilde s_k(\omega)$，故
$$\det\left[\begin{matrix}JS(\exp[-ik]+\exp[ik]+2)&-\omega\\-\omega&JS(2-\exp[-ik]-\exp[ik])\end{matrix}\right]=0$$

即
$$\omega^2=4J^2S^2\sin^2[k]$$

故
$$\omega=\pm2JS\sin[k]$$

当$k$小，可知
$$\omega\simeq\pm2JSk$$
