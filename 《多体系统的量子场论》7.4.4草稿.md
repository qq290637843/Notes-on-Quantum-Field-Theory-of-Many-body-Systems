# 《多体系统的量子场论》7.4.4草稿

## 问题 7.4.6.
1.
同7.4.2节，这里同样改为
$$\rho_{I,k}=\frac{1}{\sqrt L}\int dx\ \exp[-ikx]\rho_I(x),\rho_{I,k}=\frac{1}{\sqrt L}\sum_{q\sim...}':c_q^\dagger c_{q+k}:$$

基于和问题7.4.5.2.同样的理由，可得
$$[\rho_{1,k},\rho_{1,k'}]=\frac1L\delta_{k+k',0}\sum_{q\sim k_F}'(n_q-n_{q+k})=\frac1L\delta_{k+k',0}\frac{kL}{2\pi}=\frac{1}{2\pi}k\delta_{k+k',0}$$

$$[\rho_{2,k},\rho_{2,k'}]=\frac1L\delta_{k+k',0}\sum_{q\sim-k_F}'(n_q-n_{q+k})=-\frac1L\delta_{k+k',0}\frac{kL}{2\pi}=-\frac{1}{2\pi}k\delta_{k+k',0}$$

注意在$k_F$附近，$\epsilon_k\simeq v_F(k-k_F)$，而在$-k_F$附近，$\epsilon_k\simeq-v_F(k+k_F)$

再次基于和问题7.4.5.3.同样的理由，可得
$$2\pi v_F\sum_{k>0}\rho_{1,-k}\rho_{1,k}=\sum_{p\sim k_F}v_F(p-k_F):c_p^\dagger c_p:$$

$$2\pi v_F\sum_{k>0}\rho_{2,k}\rho_{2,-k}=\sum_{p\sim-k_F}-v_F(p+k_F):c_p^\dagger c_p:$$

所以
$$H_0=2\pi v_F\sum_{k>0}(\rho_{1,-k}\rho_{1,k}+\rho_{2,k}\rho_{2,-k})$$

所以书中题干中不止$k,-k$顺序错了，中间还错误放了个负号。

2.
同7.4.2节，这里同样改为
$$\psi_1(x)=\frac{1}{\sqrt L}\sum_{k\sim k_F}\exp[ikx]c_k,\psi_2(x)=\frac{1}{\sqrt L}\sum_{k\sim-k_F}\exp[ikx]c_k$$

故
$$\psi_1(x)=\frac{1}{\sqrt L}\exp[ik_Fx]\sum_{q\sim0}\exp[iqx]c_{q+k_F}$$

$$\psi_2(x)=\frac{1}{\sqrt L}\exp[-ik_Fx]\sum_{q\sim0}\exp[iqx]c_{q-k_F}$$

注意（注意对于两项的正规积和普通积之间的差是c-数）
$$[\rho_{1,k},c_{p+k_F}]=\frac{1}{\sqrt L}\sum_{q\sim k_F}[c_q^\dagger c_{q+k},c_{p+k_F}]\\=-\frac{1}{\sqrt L}\sum_{q\sim k_F}\delta_{q,p+k_F}c_{q+k}=-\frac{1}{\sqrt L}c_{p+k_F+k}$$

故
$$[\rho_1(x),\psi_1(y)]=\frac1L\sum_{k,p\sim0}\exp[ikx+ik_Fy+ipy] [\rho_{1,k},c_{p+k_F}]\\=-\frac{1}{L\sqrt L}\sum_{k,p\sim0}\exp[ikx+ik_Fy+ipy]c_{p+k_F+k}\\=-\frac{1}{L\sqrt L}\exp[ik_Fy]\sum_{k,q\sim0}\exp[ik(x-y)+iqy]c_{q+k_F}\\=-\frac{1}{\sqrt L}\exp[ik_Fy]\delta(x-y)\sum_{q\sim0}\exp[iqy]c_{q+k_F}\\=-\delta(x-y)\psi_1(y)$$

以及
$$[\rho_{2,k},c_{p-k_F}]=\frac{1}{\sqrt L}\sum_{q\sim-k_F}[c_q^\dagger c_{q+k},c_{p-k_F}]\\=-\frac{1}{\sqrt L}\sum_{q\sim-k_F}\delta_{q,p-k_F}c_{q+k}=-\frac{1}{\sqrt L}c_{p-k_F+k}$$

故
$$[\rho_2(x),\psi_2(y)]=\frac1L\sum_{k,p\sim0}\exp[ikx-ik_Fy+ipy] [\rho_{2,k},c_{p-k_F}]\\=-\frac{1}{L\sqrt L}\sum_{k,p\sim0}\exp[ikx-ik_Fy+ipy]c_{p-k_F+k}\\=-\frac{1}{L\sqrt L}\exp[-ik_Fy]\sum_{k,q\sim0}\exp[ik(x-y)+iqy]c_{q-k_F}\\=-\frac1{\sqrt L}\exp[-ik_Fy]\delta(x-y)\sum_{q\sim0}\exp[iqy]c_{q-k_F}\\=-\delta(x-y)\psi_2(y)$$

现在注意定义$\partial_x\phi_I(x)=2\pi\rho_I(x)$，故
$$\phi_I(x)=\frac{2\pi}{i\sqrt L}\sum_{k\neq0}\frac{\exp[ikx]}{k}\rho_{I,k}$$

从而
$$[\rho_1(x),\phi_1(y)]=\frac{2\pi}{iL}\sum_{k,k'}\frac{\exp[ikx+ik'y]}{k'}\frac{k}{2\pi}\delta_{k+k',0}=i\delta(x-y)$$

$$[\rho_2(x),\phi_2(y)]=\frac{2\pi}{iL}\sum_{k,k'}\frac{\exp[ikx+ik'y]}{k'}\frac{-k}{2\pi}\delta_{k+k',0}=-i\delta(x-y)$$

从而
$$[\rho_1(x),\exp[i\alpha_1\phi_1(y)]]=i\alpha_1(i\delta(x-y))\exp[i\alpha_1\phi_1(y)]$$

$$[\rho_2(x),\exp[i\alpha_2\phi_2(y)]]=i\alpha_2(-i\delta(x-y))\exp[i\alpha_2\phi_2(y)]$$

注意我们希望$\phi_I(x)$的波形平缓（即$k\sim0$），而$\psi_I(x)$的波形并不平缓（例如$\psi_1$的$p\sim k_F$），为了让波形平缓部波矢对齐，于是

$$\psi_1(x)\propto\exp[i\phi_1(x)+ik_Fx],\psi_2(x)\propto\exp[-i\phi_2(x)-ik_Fx]$$

3.
题干里的相互作用项显然写错了，动量甚至不守恒，相互作用项应为
$$H_V=\sum_{q,k_1,k_2}V_qc_{k_1}^\dagger c_{k_1+q}c_{k_2}^\dagger c_{k_2-q}$$

注意，题干要求动量必须落在$\pm k_F$附近，故$k_1,k_1+q,k_2,k_2-q$也必须在$\pm k_F$附近。从而实际只有可能：$q\sim0$意味着$k_1$和$k_1+q$在同一分支，$k_2$和$k_2-q$在同一分支，而$q\sim\pm 2k_F$意味着$k_1$和$k_1+q$在相反分支。

所以考虑设$H_V=H_V^{(0)}+H_V^{(2k_F)}+H_V^{(-2k_F)}$。

对于$H_V^{(0)}$：
$$\sum_{k_1}c_{k_1}^\dagger c_{k_1+q}=\sqrt L(\rho_{1,q}+\rho_{2,q})+2\left(\frac{\Lambda L}{2\pi}+1\right)$$

$$\sum_{k_2}c_{k_2}^\dagger c_{k_2-q}=\sqrt L(\rho_{1,-q}+\rho_{2,-q})+2\left(\frac{\Lambda L}{2\pi}+1\right)$$

故
$$H_V^{(0)}=L\sum_{q\sim0}V_0\left(\rho_{1,q}\rho_{1,-q}+\rho_{1,q}\rho_{2,-q}+\rho_{2,q}\rho_{1,-q}+\rho_{2,q}\rho_{2,-q}\right)+...$$

对于$H_V^{(2k_F)}$：
$$\sum_{k_1}c_{k_1}^\dagger c_{k_1+2k_F+p}=\sum_{k\sim0}c_{2,k}^\dagger c_{1,k+p}$$

$$\sum_{k_2}c_{k_2}^\dagger c_{k_2-2k_F-p}=\sum_{k\sim0}c_{1,k}^\dagger c_{2,k-p}$$

故
$$H_V^{(2k_F)}=\sum_{p\sim0}V_{2k_F}\sum_{k\sim0,k'\sim0}c_{2,k}^\dagger c_{1,k+p}c_{1,k'}^\dagger c_{2,k'-p}\\=\sum_{p\sim0}V_{2k_F}\sum_{k\sim0,k'\sim0}c_{1,k+p}c_{1,k'}^\dagger c_{2,k}^\dagger c_{2,k'-p}\\=\sum_{p\sim0}V_{2k_F}\sum_{k\sim0,k'\sim0}(\delta_{k+p,k'}-c_{1,k'}^\dagger c_{1,k+p})c_{2,k}^\dagger c_{2,k'-p}\\=-\sum_{q\sim0}V_{2k_F}\sum_{k\sim0,k'\sim0}c_{1,k'}^\dagger c_{1,q+k'}c_{2,k}^\dagger c_{2,k-q}+...\\=-L\sum_{q\sim0}V_{2k_F}\rho_{1,q}\rho_{2,-q}+...$$

对于$H_V^{(-2k_F)}$：
$$\sum_{k_1}c_{k_1}^\dagger c_{k_1-2k_F+p}=\sum_{k\sim0}c_{1,k}^\dagger c_{2,k+p}$$

$$\sum_{k_2}c_{k_2}^\dagger c_{k_2+2k_F-p}=\sum_{k\sim0}c_{2,k}^\dagger c_{1,k-p}$$

故
$$H_V^{(-2k_F)}=\sum_{p\sim0}V_{-2k_F}\sum_{k\sim0,k'\sim0}c_{1,k}^\dagger c_{2,k+p}c_{2,k'}^\dagger c_{1,k'-p}\\=-\sum_{q\sim0}V_{-2k_F}\sum_{k\sim0,k'\sim0}c_{1,k}^\dagger c_{1,-q+k}c_{2,k'}^\dagger c_{2,q+k'}+...\\=-L\sum_{q\sim0}V_{-2k_F}\rho_{1,-q}\rho_{2,q}+...$$

所以
$$H_V=L\sum_{q\sim0}\left(V_0\rho_{1,q}\rho_{1,-q}+V_0\rho_{2,q}\rho_{2,-q}+(V_0-V_{2k_F})\rho_{1,q}\rho_{2,-q}+(V_0-V_{2k_F})\rho_{2,q}\rho_{1,-q}\right)\\+...$$

故
$$V_{IJ}=\frac{L}{2\pi}\left[\begin{matrix}V_0&V_0-V_{2k_F}\\V_0-V_{2k_F}&V_0\end{matrix}\right]$$

4.
适当扔掉常数项
$$H_0=2\pi v_F\sum_{k>0}(\rho_{1,-k}\rho_{1,k}+\rho_{2,k}\rho_{2,-k})$$

$$H_V=2\pi\sum_{k>0}\left(\frac{V_0}{\pi}(\rho_{1,k}^\dagger\rho_{1,k}+\rho_{2,-k}^\dagger\rho_{2,-k})+\frac{V_0-V_{2k_F}}{\pi}(\rho_{1,k}^\dagger\rho_{2,-k}^\dagger+\rho_{2,-k}\rho_{1,k})\right)$$
