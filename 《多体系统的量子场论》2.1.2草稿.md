# 《多体系统的量子场论》2.1.2草稿

## 问题 2.1.3.
先说明相干态确实具有单位分解性：
$$|a\rangle=\exp[-|a|^2/2]\sum_{n=0}^\infty\frac{a^n}{\sqrt{n!}}|n\rangle\\\langle a|=\exp[-|a|^2/2]\sum_{n=0}^\infty\frac{(a^*)^n}{\sqrt{n!}}\langle n|$$于是
$$\int\frac{d^2a}{\pi}\ |a\rangle\langle a|=\int\frac{d^2a}{\pi}\ \exp[-|a|^2]\sum_{n=0}^\infty\sum_{m=0}^\infty\frac{a^n(a^*)^m}{\sqrt{n!m!}}|n\rangle\langle m|\\=\frac{1}{\pi}\int_0^\infty rdr\ \int_0^{2\pi}d\theta\ \exp[-r^2]\sum_{n=0}^\infty\sum_{m=0}^\infty\frac{r^{n+m}\exp[in\theta-im\theta]}{\sqrt{n!m!}}|n\rangle\langle m|\\=\int_0^\infty dr\ r\exp[-r^2]\sum_{n=0}^\infty\frac{2r^{2n}}{n!}|n\rangle\langle n|\\=\sum_{n=0}^\infty\frac{\Gamma[n+1]}{n!}|n\rangle\langle n|=I$$

现在处理传播子，将$t_a$到$t_b$这段时间等分为$N$段，于是
$$\langle a|U(\Delta t)|a'\rangle=\langle a|\exp[-i\omega\Delta t\hat a^\dagger\hat a]|a'\rangle\\=\langle a|(1-i\omega\Delta t\hat a^\dagger\hat a)|a'\rangle+O(\Delta t^2)\\=\langle a|a'\rangle(1-i\omega\Delta t a^*a')+O(\Delta t^2)\\=\exp[-|a|^2/2-|a'|^2/2+a^*a'-i\omega\Delta ta^*a']+O(\Delta t^2)\\=\exp[-a^*a/2+a^*a'/2+a^*a'/2-a'^*a'/2-i\omega\Delta ta^*a']\\+O(\Delta t^2)\\=\exp[-a^*(a-a')/2+a'(a-a')^*/2-i\omega\Delta ta^*a']+O(\Delta t^2)$$

取$a=a_{i+1},a'=a_i$得：
$$\exp[(-a^*\dot a/2+a\dot a^*/2-i\omega a^*a)\Delta t]+O(\Delta t^2)$$

故
$$iG(a_b,t_b,a_a,t_a)=\langle a_b|U(t_b-t_a)|a_a\rangle\\=\int\pi^{1-N}\mathcal D^2[a(t)]\langle a_b|U(\Delta t)|a_{N-1}\rangle...\langle a_1|U(\Delta t)|a_a\rangle\\=\int\pi^{1-N}\mathcal D^2[a(t)]\exp\left[i\int_{t_a}^{t_b}dt\ [i(a^*\dot a-a\dot a^*)-\omega a^*a]\right]$$

接着是关于拉格朗日量：
$$p=i\sqrt{m\omega/2}(a^*-a),x=\sqrt{1/2m\omega}(a^*+a)$$

从而
$$p\dot x-H=i(a^*-a)(\dot a^*+\dot a)/2-\omega a^*a\\=ia^*\dot a^*/2+ia^*\dot a/2-ia\dot a^*/2-ia\dot a/2-\omega a^*a\\=i\partial_t(a^{*2})/4-i\partial_t(a^2)/4+i(a^*\dot a-a\dot a^*)/2-\omega a^*a$$
