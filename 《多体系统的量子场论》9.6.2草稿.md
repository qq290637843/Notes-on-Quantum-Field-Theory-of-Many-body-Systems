## 问题 9.6.1.
1.
$Z2A001n$即$Z2A(\tau^0_+)(\tau^0_+)(\tau^1)(\tau^0_-)$
故
$$G_x(\pmb i)=\tau^0,G_y(\pmb i)=\tau^0\\G_{P_x}(\pmb i)=\tau^0,G_{P_y}(\pmb i)=\tau^0\\G_{P_{xy}}(\pmb i)=i\tau^1,G_T(\pmb i)=(-)^{i_x+i_y}\tau^0$$

2.
拟设(9.6.3)为
$$a_0^l=0\\u_{\pmb x}=\chi\tau^1+\eta\tau^2,u_{\pmb y}=\chi\tau^1-\eta\tau^2\\u_{2\pmb x+\pmb y}=\chi_1\tau^1+\eta_1\tau^2+\lambda_1\tau^3,u_{-\pmb x+2\pmb y}=\chi_1\tau^1-\eta_1\tau^2-\lambda_1\tau^3\\u_{2\pmb x-\pmb y}=\chi_1\tau^1+\eta_1\tau^2+\lambda_1\tau^3,u_{\pmb x+2\pmb y}=\chi_1\tau^1-\eta_1\tau^2-\lambda_1\tau^3$$

平移部分显然，$P_x$和$P_y$显然。接着处理$P_{xy}$：
$$G_{P_{xy}}u_{\pmb y}G_{P_{xy}}^\dagger=(i\tau^1)(\chi\tau^1-\eta\tau^2)(-i\tau^1)\\=\chi\tau^1+\eta\tau^2=u_{\pmb x}$$

$$G_{P_{xy}}u_{\pmb x}G_{P_{xy}}^\dagger=(i\tau^1)(\chi\tau^1+\eta\tau^2)(-i\tau^1)\\=\chi\tau^1-\eta\tau^2=u_{\pmb y}$$

$$G_{P_{xy}}u_{2\pmb y+\pmb x}G_{P_{xy}}^\dagger=(i\tau^1)(\chi_1\tau^1-\eta_1\tau^2-\lambda_1\tau^3)(-i\tau^1)\\=\chi_1\tau^1+\eta_1\tau^2+\lambda_1\tau^3=u_{2\pmb x+\pmb y}$$

$$G_{P_{xy}}u_{-\pmb y+2\pmb x}G_{P_{xy}}^\dagger=(i\tau^1)(\chi_1\tau^1+\eta_1\tau^2+\lambda_1\tau^3)(-i\tau^1)\\=\chi_1\tau^1-\eta_1\tau^2-\lambda_1\tau^3=u_{-\pmb x+2\pmb y}$$

$$G_{P_{xy}}u_{2\pmb y-\pmb x}G_{P_{xy}}^\dagger=(i\tau^1)(\chi_1\tau^1-\eta_1\tau^2-\lambda_1\tau^3)(-i\tau^1)\\=\chi_1\tau^1+\eta_1\tau^2+\lambda_1\tau^3=u_{2\pmb x-\pmb y}$$

$$G_{P_{xy}}u_{\pmb y+2\pmb x}G_{P_{xy}}^\dagger=(i\tau^1)(\chi_1\tau^1+\eta_1\tau^2+\lambda_1\tau^3)(-i\tau^1)\\=\chi_1\tau^1-\eta_1\tau^2-\lambda_1\tau^3=u_{\pmb x+2\pmb y}$$

关于时间反演，就是要说明：
$$G_T(\pmb i)(-u_{\pmb m})G_T^\dagger(\pmb i+\pmb m)=u_{\pmb m}$$

即
$$(-)^{m_x+m_y}u_{\pmb m}=-u_{\pmb m}$$

这个显然。

3.
$Z2A003n$即$Z2A(\tau^0_+)(\tau^0_+)(\tau^3)(\tau^0_-)$
故
$$G_x'(\pmb i)=\tau^0,G_y'(\pmb i)=\tau^0\\G_{P_x}'(\pmb i)=\tau^0,G_{P_y}'(\pmb i)=\tau^0\\G_{P_{xy}}'(\pmb i)=i\tau^3,G_T'(\pmb i)=(-)^{i_x+i_y}\tau^0$$

根据变换法则：$G_U'(\pmb i)=W(\pmb i)G_U(\pmb i)W^\dagger(U(\pmb i))$

我们需要
$$\tau^0=W(\pmb i)\tau^0W^\dagger(\pmb i-\pmb x),\tau^0=W(\pmb i)\tau^0W^\dagger(\pmb i-\pmb y)\\\tau^0=W(\pmb i)\tau^0W^\dagger(P_x\pmb i),\tau^0=W(\pmb i)\tau^0W^\dagger(P_y\pmb i)\\i\tau^3=W(\pmb i)i\tau^1W^\dagger(P_{xy}\pmb i),(-)^{i_x+i_y}\tau^0=W(\pmb i)(-)^{i_x+i_y}\tau^0W^\dagger(\pmb i)$$

根据前两条可知$W(\pmb i)=W$，所以
$$\tau^3=W\tau^1W^\dagger$$

取$W=\frac{1}{\sqrt2}(\tau^0+i\tau^2)$即可

$$\frac{1}{\sqrt2}(\tau^0+i\tau^2)\tau^1\frac{1}{\sqrt2}(\tau^0-i\tau^2)\\=\frac12(\tau^1+\tau^3)(\tau^0-i\tau^2)=\frac12(\tau^1+\tau^3+\tau^3-\tau^1)=\tau^3$$

## 问题 9.6.2.
拟设(9.6.10)为
$$u_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}=i\chi\tau^0+\eta\tau^1,u_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}=(-)^{i_x}(i\chi\tau^0+\eta\tau^2),a_0^{1,2,3}=0$$

从而：
$$P_{C_1}=(i\chi\tau^0+\eta\tau^1)(-)^{i_x+1}(i\chi\tau^0+\eta\tau^2)(-i\chi\tau^0+\eta\tau^1)(-)^{i_x}(-i\chi\tau^0+\eta\tau^2)\\=-(-\chi^2\tau^0+i\chi\eta\tau^2+i\chi\eta\tau^1+i\eta^2\tau^3)(-\chi^2\tau^0-i\chi\eta\tau^2-i\chi\eta\tau^1+i\eta^2\tau^3)\\=(\eta^4-\chi^4-2\chi^2\eta^2)\tau^0+2i\chi\eta^3\tau^1-2i\chi\eta^3\tau^2+2i\chi^2\eta^2\tau^3$$

$$P_{C_2}=(-)^{i_x}(i\chi\tau^0+\eta\tau^2)(-i\chi\tau^0+\eta\tau^1)(-)^{i_x-1}(-i\chi\tau^0+\eta\tau^2)(i\chi\tau^0+\eta\tau^1)\\=-(\chi^2\tau^0+i\chi\eta\tau^1-i\chi\eta\tau^2-i\eta^2\tau^3)(\chi^2\tau^0-i\chi\eta\tau^1+i\chi\eta\tau^2-i\eta^2\tau^3)\\=(\eta^4-\chi^4-2\chi^2\eta^2)\tau^0+2i\chi\eta^3\tau^1+2i\chi\eta^3\tau^2+2i\chi^2\eta^2\tau^3$$

所以不共线，故为$Z_2$规范结构

(b)
首先哈密顿量为
$$H=-2\chi\sin[k_x]\Gamma_0+2\eta\cos[k_x]\Gamma_2-2\chi\sin[k_y]\Gamma_1+2\eta\cos[k_y]\Gamma_3$$

并且
$$\Gamma_0=\tau^0\otimes\tau^3,\Gamma_2=\tau^1\otimes\tau^3,\Gamma_1=\tau^0\otimes\tau^1,\Gamma_3=\tau^2\otimes\tau^1$$

从而
$$H=(-2\chi\sin[k_x]\tau^0+2\eta\cos[k_x]\tau^1)\otimes\tau^3+(-2\chi\sin[k_y]\tau^0+2\eta\cos[k_y]\tau^2)\otimes\tau^1\\=\left[\begin{matrix}A&B\\B&-A\end{matrix}\right]$$

其中$A=-2\chi\sin[k_x]\tau^0+2\eta\cos[k_x]\tau^1,B=-2\chi\sin[k_y]\tau^0+2\eta\cos[k_y]\tau^2$

从而
$$H^2=\left[\begin{matrix}A^2+B^2& [A,B]\\ [B,A]&A^2+B^2\end{matrix}\right]$$

而
$$A^2=(4\chi^2\sin^2[k_x]+4\eta^2\cos^2[k_x])\tau^0-8\chi\eta\sin[k_x]\cos[k_x]\tau^1$$

$$B^2=(4\chi^2\sin^2[k_y]+4\eta^2\cos^2[k_y])\tau^0-8\chi\eta\sin[k_y]\cos[k_y]\tau^2$$

$$[A,B]=4\eta^2\cos[k_x]\cos[k_y] [\tau^1,\tau^2]=8i\eta^2\cos[k_x]\cos[k_y]\tau^3$$

记$$C=4\chi^2(\sin^2[k_x]+\sin^2[k_y])+4\eta^2(\cos^2[k_x]+\cos^2[k_y])\\V_x=-4\chi\eta\sin[2k_x],V_y=-4\chi\eta\sin[2k_y]\\D=8\eta^2\cos[k_x]\cos[k_y]$$

故
$$H^2=\left[\begin{matrix}C\tau^0+V_x\tau^1+V_y\tau^2&iD\tau^3\\-iD\tau^3&C\tau^0+V_x\tau^1+V_y\tau^2\end{matrix}\right]\\=C\tau^0\otimes\tau^0+V_x\tau^1\otimes\tau^0+V_y\tau^2\otimes\tau^0-D\tau^3\otimes\tau^2$$

现在设$\Lambda_1=\tau^1\otimes\tau^0,\Lambda_2=\tau^2\otimes\tau^0,\Lambda_3=-\tau^3\otimes\tau^2$

于是
$$\{\Lambda_1,\Lambda_2\}=0,\{\Lambda_2,\Lambda_3\}=0,\{\Lambda_1,\Lambda_3\}=0\\\Lambda_1^2=\tau^0\otimes\tau^0,\Lambda_2^2=\tau^0\otimes\tau^0,\Lambda_3^2=\tau^0\otimes\tau^0$$

故
$$(V_x\Lambda_1+V_y\Lambda_2+D\Lambda_3)^2=(V_x^2+V_y^2+D^2)\tau^0\otimes\tau^0$$

所以$V_x\Lambda_1+V_y\Lambda_2+D\Lambda_3$的特征值就是$\pm\sqrt{V_x^2+V_y^2+D^2}$

于是$H$的特征值就是$\pm\sqrt{C\pm\sqrt{V_x^2+V_y^2+D^2}}$
