# 《多体系统的量子场论》5.3.2草稿

## 问题 5.3.2.
先说明(5.3.5)：
根据
$$\partial_tn_{\pmb k,\alpha}+\partial_{\pmb x}n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\cdot\partial_{\pmb k}\tilde\epsilon-\partial_{\pmb k}n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\cdot\partial_{\pmb x}\tilde\epsilon=I[n_{\pmb k,\alpha}]$$

求和得
$$\sum_{\pmb k}\partial_tn_{\pmb k,\alpha}+\sum_{\pmb k}\left(\partial_{\pmb x}n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\cdot\partial_{\pmb k}\tilde\epsilon-\partial_{\pmb k}n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\cdot\partial_{\pmb x}\tilde\epsilon\right)=\sum_{\pmb k}I[n_{\pmb k,\alpha}]$$

故
$$\sum_{\pmb k}\partial_tn_{\pmb k,\alpha}+\sum_{\pmb k}\left(\partial_{\pmb x}n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\cdot\partial_{\pmb k}\tilde\epsilon-\partial_{\pmb k}n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\cdot\partial_{\pmb x}\tilde\epsilon\right)=0$$

从而
$$\mathcal V\partial_t\rho(\pmb x,t)+\sum_{\pmb k}\left(\partial_{\pmb x}n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\cdot\partial_{\pmb k}\tilde\epsilon-\partial_{\pmb k}n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\cdot\partial_{\pmb x}\tilde\epsilon\right)=0$$

所以实际就是要说明
$$\sum_{\pmb k}\left(\partial_{\pmb x}n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\cdot\partial_{\pmb k}\tilde\epsilon-\partial_{\pmb k}n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\cdot\partial_{\pmb x}\tilde\epsilon\right)=\sum_{\pmb k}\partial_{\pmb x}\cdot(n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\partial_{\pmb k}\tilde\epsilon)$$

而
$$\partial_{\pmb x}\cdot(n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\partial_{\pmb k}\tilde\epsilon)-\left(\partial_{\pmb x}n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\cdot\partial_{\pmb k}\tilde\epsilon-\partial_{\pmb k}n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\cdot\partial_{\pmb x}\tilde\epsilon\right)\\=\partial_{\pmb x}n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\cdot\partial_{\pmb k}\tilde\epsilon+n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\partial_{\pmb x}\cdot\partial_{\pmb k}\tilde\epsilon-\left(\partial_{\pmb x}n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\cdot\partial_{\pmb k}\tilde\epsilon-\partial_{\pmb k}n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\cdot\partial_{\pmb x}\tilde\epsilon\right)\\=\partial_{\pmb k}\cdot(n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\partial_{\pmb x}\tilde\epsilon)$$

这一项在对$\pmb k$积分后为零。

接着说明(5.3.6)：
$$\pmb J=\mathcal V^{-1}\sum_{\pmb k}(n_{0,\pmb k,\alpha}+\delta n_{\pmb k,\alpha})\partial_{\pmb k}\left(\xi_{\pmb k,\alpha}^*+\mathcal V^{-1}\sum_{\pmb k',\beta}f(\pmb k,\alpha;\pmb k',\beta)\delta n_{\pmb k',\beta}\right)\\\simeq\mathcal V^{-1}\sum_{\pmb k}n_{0,\pmb k,\alpha}\pmb v^*(\pmb k)+\mathcal V^{-1}\sum_{\pmb k}\delta n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\pmb v^*(\pmb k)\\+\mathcal V^{-2}\sum_{\pmb k,\pmb k',\beta}n_{0,\pmb k,\alpha}\delta n_{\pmb k',\beta}\partial_{\pmb k}f(\pmb k,\alpha;\pmb k',\beta)\\=\mathcal V^{-1}\sum_{\pmb k}n_{0,\pmb k,\alpha}\pmb v^*(\pmb k)+\mathcal V^{-1}\sum_{\pmb k}\delta n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\pmb v^*(\pmb k)\\-\mathcal V^{-2}\sum_{\pmb k,\pmb k',\beta}\partial_{\pmb k}n_{0,\pmb k,\alpha}\delta n_{\pmb k',\beta}f(\pmb k,\alpha;\pmb k',\beta)\\=\mathcal V^{-1}\sum_{\pmb k}n_{0,\pmb k,\alpha}\pmb v^*(\pmb k)+\mathcal V^{-1}\sum_{\pmb k}\delta n_{\pmb k,\alpha}\pmb v^*(\pmb k)\\+\mathcal V^{-2}\sum_{\pmb k,\pmb k',\beta}\pmb v^*(\pmb k)\delta(\xi^*)\delta n_{\pmb k',\beta}f(\pmb k,\alpha;\pmb k',\beta)$$

而注意$n_{0,\pmb k,\alpha}$关于$\pmb k$堆成，而$\pmb v^*(\pmb k)$关于$\pmb k$反对称，故第一项为零。
