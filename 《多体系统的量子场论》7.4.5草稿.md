# 《多体系统的量子场论》7.4.5草稿

## 问题 7.4.7.
首先
$$S=-\frac{m}{4\pi}\int dtdx\ (\partial_t\phi\partial_x\phi+v\partial_x\phi\partial_x\phi)$$

考虑
$$\phi(x)=\frac{1}{\sqrt L}\sum_k\phi_k\exp[ikx]$$

由于$\phi(x)$是实场，故$\phi_{-k}=\phi_k^\dagger$

于是
$$\partial_x\phi(x)=\frac{1}{\sqrt L}\sum_kik\phi_k\exp[ikx]$$

从而
$$S=-\frac{m}{4\pi}\int dtdx\ \frac{1}{L}\sum_{k_1,k_2}\exp[ik_1x+ik_2x](\partial_t\phi_{k_1}ik_2\phi_{k_2}-vk_1k_2\phi_{k_1}\phi_{k_2})\\=-\frac{m}{4\pi}\int dt\ \sum_{k_1,k_2}\delta(k_1+k_2)(\partial_t\phi_{k_1}ik_2\phi_{k_2}-vk_1k_2\phi_{k_1}\phi_{k_2})\\=-\frac{m}{4\pi}\int dt\ \sum_k(-ik\dot\phi_k\phi_{-k}+vk^2\phi_k\phi_{-k})\\=-\frac{m}{2\pi}\int dt\ \sum_{k>0}(-ik\dot\phi_k\phi_{-k}+vk^2\phi_k\phi_{-k})$$

所以
$$L=\sum_{k>0}\left(\frac{imk}{2\pi}\dot\phi_k\phi_{-k}-\frac{mvk^2}{2\pi}\phi_k\phi_{-k}\right)$$

从而
$$\pi_k=\frac{\partial L}{\partial\dot\phi_k}=\frac{imk}{2\pi}\phi_{-k}$$

故
$$[\phi_k,\phi_{-k'}]=i\delta_{k,k'}/\frac{imk'}{2\pi}=\frac{2\pi}{mk}\delta_{k,k'}$$

接着注意$\rho(x)=\frac{1}{2\pi}\partial_x\phi(x)$，故
$$\rho_k=\frac{ik}{2\pi}\phi_k,\rho_{-k}=\frac{-ik}{2\pi}\phi_{-k}$$

故
$$[\rho_k,\rho_{-k'}]=\frac{kk'}{4\pi^2}\frac{2\pi}{mk}\delta_{k,k'}=\frac{k}{2\pi m}\delta_{k,k'}$$

故
$$[\rho_k,\rho_{k'}]=\frac{k\delta_{k+k',0}}{2\pi m}$$

接着
$$H=\sum_{k>0}\dot\phi_k\pi_k-L=\sum_{k>0}\frac{mvk^2}{2\pi}\phi_k\phi_{-k}=\sum_{k>0}2\pi mv\rho_k\rho_{-k}\\=2\pi mv\sum_{k>0}\rho_k^\dagger\rho_k+{\rm const}$$
