# 《多体系统的量子场论》3.5.4草稿

$S$的第一个表达式里，$\partial_{\pmb x}\theta_\lambda\partial_{\pmb x}\delta\theta$不存在是因为这两部分在动量空间是互斥的，而$ng_l\sin$项的符号错了，且$\sin$的括号内少了$n$。

关于$S$的第二个表达式的得出：首先要用到累积量展开定理：
$$\langle\exp[-V]\rangle=\exp\sum_{k=1}^\infty\frac{(-1)^k}{k!}\langle V^k\rangle_{\rm connected}\\\simeq\exp[-\langle V\rangle+\langle V^2\rangle/2-\langle V\rangle^2/2]$$

故
$$\langle\exp[A(\pmb x)\delta\theta(\pmb x)+B(\pmb x)\delta\theta^2(\pmb x)]\rangle\\\simeq\exp[A(\pmb x)\langle\delta\theta(\pmb x)\rangle+B(\pmb x)\langle\delta\theta^2(\pmb x)\rangle+A(\pmb x)A(\pmb y)\langle \delta\theta(\pmb x)\delta\theta(\pmb y)\rangle/2\\+A(\pmb x)B(\pmb y)\langle\delta\theta(\pmb x)\delta\theta^2(\pmb y)\rangle+B(\pmb x)B(\pmb y)\langle\delta\theta^2(\pmb x)\delta\theta^2(\pmb y)\rangle/2\\-A(\pmb x)A(\pmb y)\langle\delta\theta(\pmb x)\rangle\langle\delta\theta(\pmb y)\rangle/2-A(\pmb x)B(\pmb y)\langle\delta\theta(\pmb x)\rangle\langle\delta\theta^2(\pmb y)\rangle\\-B(\pmb x)B(\pmb y)\langle\delta\theta^2(\pmb x)\rangle\langle\delta\theta^2(\pmb y)\rangle/2]\\\simeq\exp[A(\pmb x)\langle\delta\theta(\pmb x)\rangle+B(\pmb x)\langle\delta\theta^2(\pmb x)\rangle+A(\pmb x)A(\pmb y)\langle\delta\theta(\pmb x)\delta\theta(\pmb y)\rangle/2\\-A(\pmb x)A(\pmb y)\langle\delta\theta(\pmb x)\rangle\langle\delta\theta(\pmb y)\rangle/2]$$

再下来一个式子，那个等号成立是因为$K(\pmb x-\pmb y)$是一个短程衰减函数，所以可以认为$\pmb x-\pmb y$非常小。这里$K(\pmb x-\pmb y)$短程衰减是因为$K$是短波部分傅里叶出来的结果。多出来的$1/2$是因为，直接泰勒展开到一阶后，应该形如$(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta_\lambda(\pmb x)\cdot(\pmb x-\pmb y))^2$，我们考虑$\pmb y-\pmb x$在各个方向的取值之后，相当于把$({\rm Re}\exp[i\phi])^2$积分一圈，故结果等于把$1/2$积分一圈。

注意$K_2$的定义，意味着$\cos^2(n\theta_\lambda(\pmb x))$被视为了$1/2$，这是因为其和$1/2$的差是高阶谐波，为无关项。
而最后那一行被省略，是因为其只提供一个高阶谐波以及一个常数偏移。高阶谐波不在$\kappa_\lambda$和$g_\lambda$的管辖范围内。
