# 《多体系统的量子场论》3.3.8草稿

关于$\frac{Z[f(t,\pmb x)]}{Z[0]}$：
首先转成$$\int d\pmb v\ \exp\left[-\pmb v^TA\pmb v+\pmb b\cdot\pmb v\right]$$的形式

此时，$A=i\frac{\chi}{2}(\partial_t^2-v^2\partial_{\pmb x}^2),\pmb b=if$

故结果为
$$\exp\left(\frac14if\frac{1}{i\frac\chi2(\partial_t^2-v^2\partial_{\pmb x}^2)}if\right)=\exp\left(-\frac i2f\frac{1}{\chi(-\partial_t^2+v^2\partial_{\pmb x}^2)}f\right)$$

根据问题3.3.3.可知
$$\chi\left(\partial_t^2-v^2\partial_{\pmb x}^2\right)\langle T\theta(\pmb x,t)\theta(\pmb0,0)\rangle=-i\delta(t)\delta^{(d)}(\pmb x)$$

于是
$$G_{\theta}=\frac{-i}{i\chi(\partial_t^2-v^2\partial_{\pmb x}^2)}=\frac{1}{\chi(-\partial_t^2+v^2\partial_{\pmb x}^2)}$$

$$-i\frac12\int dt_1\ d\pmb x_1\ dt_2\ d\pmb x_2\ \\(-\delta(t_1-t_0,\pmb x_1-\pmb x_0)+\delta(t_1,\pmb x_1))G_\theta(t_1-t_2,\pmb x_1-\pmb x_2)\times\\(-\delta(t_2-t_0,\pmb x_2-\pmb x_0)+\delta(t_2,\pmb x_2))\\=-i\frac12\left[G_{\theta}(0,\pmb0)-G_\theta(t_0,\pmb x_0)-G_{\theta}(-t_0,-\pmb x_0)+G_\theta(0,\pmb0)\right]\\=i[G_\theta(t_0,\pmb x_0)-G_{\theta}(0,\pmb0)]$$

关于级数计算中的$0^+$：
$$\sum_{n=1}^\infty\frac{z^n}{n}$$只在$|z|<1$时绝对收敛，而$e^{i(...)}$代入$z$会条件收敛，所以需要补上$-0^+$，让其绝对收敛。

关于(3.3.24)：
$$\left(-2\pi i\frac{x-v|t|}{L}+0^+\right)\left(-2\pi i\frac{-x-v|t|}{L}+0^+\right)\\=4\pi^2\frac{v^2t^2-x^2}{L^2}+2\pi i\frac{v|t|0^+}{L}$$

关于(3.3.24)后表达式中$i0^+$是怎么提出的：
实际是要处理$(x^2-v^2t^2+i0^+)^{-1/4\pi v\chi}$：
如果$x^2-v^2t^2>0$，那么这个开根过程中，虚部没有行为；
如果$x^2-v^2t^2<0$，那么开根过程中，相较$|x^2-v^2t^2|$，会多出相位因子$\exp[-i/4v\chi]$

关于3.3.8.2节开头的内容：
指数部分的积分为
$$i\int dt\ d\pmb x\ \frac{\chi}{2}\theta(\partial_{\pmb x}^2-\partial_t^2)\theta\\=i\int (-id\tau)\ d\pmb x\ \frac\chi2\theta(\partial_{\pmb x}^2+\partial_\tau^2)\theta\\=\int d^3x\ \frac{\chi}{2}\theta(\partial_i^2)\theta$$

所以根据(2.2.14)可知
$$\mathcal G_\theta=\frac{1}{-\chi\partial_i^2}$$

其实关于$\pm i\frac{1}{4\pi\chi}\frac{1}{\sqrt{|\pmb x^2-t^2|}}$这个正负号，也只需要像3.3.8.1节那样，把$\frac{1}{4\pi\chi}\frac{1}{\sqrt{\pmb x^2-t^2+i0^+}}$写清楚就行。

关于(3.3.27)式之前那段话中的表达式：
其中所谓(3.3.9)式中被省略的东西，是指：
$$\partial_t\theta\frac{1}{V_0-\frac{1}{4m\rho_0}\partial_{\pmb x}^2}\partial_t\theta\simeq\partial_t\theta\frac{1}{V_0}\partial_t\theta$$

这个过程中丢失的东西是$\frac{1}{4m\rho_0}\partial_{\pmb x}^2$，从而，该项不能忽略就是指$\frac{1}{4m\rho_0}\partial_{\pmb x}^2\sim V_0$，故$l\sim(4mV_0\rho_0)^{-1/2}$

同样根据之前的推导可知
$$G_\theta(0,\pmb r)\propto r^{1+\epsilon/2-d}$$

所以这就说明，临界为$d_l=1+\epsilon/2$

## 问题 3.3.6.
1.

$$L_E=\frac\chi2((\partial_\tau\theta)^2+v^2(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta)^2)$$

从而
$$\langle\exp[i\theta(x,\tau)]\exp[-i\theta(0,0)]\rangle\\=\frac{\int\mathcal D\theta\ \exp[i\theta(x,\tau)]\exp[-i\theta(0,0)]\exp[-S_E]}{\int\mathcal D\theta\ \exp[-S_E]}$$

设
$$Z[f(x,\tau)]=\int\mathcal D\theta\ \exp[-S_E]\exp\left[\int d\tau\ dx\ f(x,\tau)\theta(x,\tau)\right]$$

于是
$$\langle\exp[i\theta(x_0,\tau_0)]\exp[-i\theta(0,0)]\rangle\\=\frac{Z[i\delta(x-x_0,\tau-\tau_0)-i\delta(x,\tau)]}{Z[0]}$$

而
$$\frac{Z[f(x,\tau)]}{Z[0]}=\exp\left[\frac12\int d\tau_1\ dx_1\ d\tau_2\ dx_2f(x_1,\tau_1)\mathcal G_\theta(x_1-x_2,\tau_1-\tau_2)f(x_2,\tau_2)\right]$$

其中
$$\mathcal G_\theta(x,\tau)=\langle\theta(x,\tau)\theta(0,0)\rangle=-\frac{\chi^{-1}}{\partial_\tau^2+v^2\partial_x^2}$$

从而$$\langle\exp[i\theta(x,\tau)]\exp[-i\theta(0,0)]\rangle\\=\exp\left[\frac12(-\mathcal G_\theta(0,0)+\mathcal G_\theta(x,\tau)+\mathcal G_\theta(x,\tau)-\mathcal G_\theta(0,0))\right]\\=\exp[\mathcal G_\theta(x,\tau)]\exp[-\mathcal G_\theta(0,0)]$$

考虑傅里叶，于是
$$\mathcal G_\theta(k,\omega)=\frac{\chi^{-1}}{\omega^2+v^2k^2}$$

从而（其中箭头是为了减掉$k=0$的项，因为最后还要和$\mathcal G_\theta(0,0)$做减法，所以这部分会抵消掉）
$$\mathcal G_\theta(x,\tau)=\frac1{2\pi L}\sum_{k}\int d\omega\ \frac{\chi^{-1}}{\omega^2+v^2k^2}\exp[ikx+i\omega\tau]\\=\frac{1}{2\pi L\chi}\sum_k\frac{1}{2iv|k|}\int d\omega\left(\frac{1}{\omega-iv|k|}-\frac{1}{\omega+iv|k|}\right)\exp[ikx+i\omega\tau]\\=\begin{cases}\frac{1}{vL\chi}\sum_k\frac{1}{2|k|}\exp[ikx-v|k|\tau],\tau>0\\\frac{1}{vL\chi}\sum_k\frac{1}{2|k|}\exp[ikx+v|k|\tau],\tau<0\end{cases}\\=\frac{1}{2vL\chi}\sum_k\frac{1}{|k|}\exp[ikx-v|k||\tau|]\\\to\frac{1}{4\pi v\chi}\sum_{n\geq1}\frac{1}{n}\exp[+2\pi inx/L-2\pi nv|\tau|/L]\\+\frac{1}{4\pi v\chi}\sum_{n\geq1}\frac1n\exp[-2\pi inx/L-2\pi nv|\tau|/L]\\=-\frac{1}{4\pi v\chi}\left(\log\left[1-\exp[-2\pi v|\tau|/L+2\pi ix/L]\right]\\+\log\left[1-\exp[-2\pi v|\tau|/L-2\pi ix/L]\right]\right)$$

从而
$$\exp[\mathcal G_\theta(x,\tau)]\exp[-\mathcal G_\theta(0,0)]=\left(\frac{4\pi^2l^2/L^2}{1-2\exp[-2\pi v|\tau|/L]\cos[2\pi x/L]+\exp[-4\pi v|\tau|/L]}\right)^{\frac{1}{4\pi v\chi}}$$
