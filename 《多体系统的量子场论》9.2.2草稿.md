# 《多体系统的量子场论》9.2.2草稿

## 问题 9.2.3.
(a)
首先已知
$$U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}=\chi\tau^3+\eta\tau^1,U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}=\chi\tau^3-\eta\tau^1$$

注意由于$\chi,\eta$都是实数，所以如上两个矩阵都是厄米矩阵，所以
$$U_{\pmb j\pmb i}=U_{\pmb i\pmb j}^\dagger=U_{\pmb i\pmb j}$$

所以
$$P_{\square}=U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}U_{\pmb i+\pmb x,\pmb i+\pmb x+\pmb y}U_{\pmb i+\pmb x+\pmb y,\pmb i+\pmb y}U_{\pmb i+\pmb y,\pmb i}\\=(\chi\tau^3+\eta\tau^1)(\chi\tau^3-\eta\tau^1)(\chi\tau^3+\eta\tau^1)(\chi\tau^3-\eta\tau^1)\\=(\chi^2\tau^0-\eta^2\tau^0-2i\chi\eta\tau^2)^2\\=[(\chi^2-\eta^2)^2-4\chi^2\eta^2]\tau^0-4i\chi\eta(\chi^2-\eta^2)\tau^2$$

接着说明所有回路的$P$值都不含$\tau^1,\tau^3$。注意，由于
$$(a\tau^1+b\tau^3)(c\tau^1+d\tau^3)=(ac+bd)\tau^0+i(bc-ad)\tau^2$$

$$(a\tau^1+b\tau^3)(c\tau^0+d\tau^2)=(ac-ibd)\tau^1+(bc+iad)\tau^3$$

$$(a\tau^0+b\tau^2)(c\tau^0+d\tau^2)=(ac+bd)\tau^0+(ad+bc)\tau^2$$

故可知对于任何偶长链，其$P$值一定是$\tau^0$和$\tau^2$的线性组合，而回路路长必须为偶数。
(b)
这里考虑设
$$G_{\pmb i}=(i\tau^3)^{i_x+i_y}$$

于是
$$U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}'=G_{\pmb i}U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}G_{\pmb i+\pmb x}^\dagger=(i\tau^3)^{i_x+i_y}(\chi\tau^3+\eta\tau^1)(-i\tau^3)^{i_x+i_y+1}\\=-i(\chi\tau^0+i(-)^{i_x+i_y+1}\eta\tau^2)=-i\chi\tau^0-(-)^{i_x+i_y}\eta\tau^2=c_0\exp[-i\theta_{\pmb i}\tau^2]$$

$$U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}'=G_{\pmb i}U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}G_{\pmb i+\pmb y}^\dagger=(i\tau^3)^{i_x+i_y}(\chi\tau^3-\eta\tau^1)(-i\tau^3)^{i_x+i_y+1}\\=-i(\chi\tau^0-i(-)^{i_x+i_y+1}\eta\tau^2)=-i\chi\tau^0+(-)^{i_x+i_y}\eta\tau^2=c_0\exp[i\theta_{\pmb i}\tau^2]$$

其中
$$c_0=-i\sqrt{\chi^2+\eta^2},\theta_{\pmb i}=\arctan(\eta/\chi)(-)^{i_x+i_y}$$

接着设$R=\frac{1}{\sqrt2}(\tau^0-i\tau^1)$，于是
$$R\tau^2R^\dagger=\frac12(\tau^0-i\tau^1)\tau^2(\tau^0+i\tau^1)=\frac12(\tau^2-\tau^2+\tau^3+\tau^3)=\tau^3$$

所以
$$RG_{\pmb i}U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}G_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}^\dagger R^\dagger=c_0\exp[-i\theta_{\pmb i}\tau^3]\\RG_{\pmb i}U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}G_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}^\dagger R^\dagger=c_0\exp[i\theta_{\pmb i}\tau^3]$$

(b)
注意$U_{\pmb i\pmb j}=c_{\pmb i\pmb j}\tau^0$，故
$$H=\sum_{\langle\pmb i\pmb j\rangle}-\frac38J_{\pmb i\pmb j}\left(\psi_{\pmb i}^\dagger U_{\pmb i\pmb j}\psi_{\pmb j}+h.c.\right)\\=\sum_{\langle\pmb i\pmb j\rangle}-\frac38J_{\pmb i\pmb j}\left(c_{\pmb i\pmb j}\psi_{\pmb i}^\dagger\psi_{\pmb j}+h.c.\right)$$

根据问题9.1.7.，我们已经知道该拟设下自旋子是有能隙的，从而基态无简并，而注意到$H$在对$\psi_{\pmb i}$执行全局$SU(2)$操作下不变，从而基态本身也满足此条件，从而$\langle\psi_{\pmb i}^\dagger\tau^l\psi_{\pmb i}\rangle$只能为零。

(c)
注意$U_{\pmb i\pmb j}$全都是可以随意和其他内容交换的，故
$$-i\chi_1W_{\pmb i}W_{\pmb i+\pmb x}^\dagger=-\chi(\tau^3+\tau^1)$$

于是
$$W_{\pmb i}W_{\pmb i+\pmb x}^\dagger=-i\frac{\chi}{\chi_1}(\tau^3+\tau^1)$$

所以我们知道$\chi_1=\sqrt2\chi$，于是
$$W_{\pmb i}W_{\pmb i+\pmb x}^\dagger=-i\frac{\tau^3+\tau^1}{\sqrt2}$$

$$W_{\pmb i}W_{\pmb i+\pmb y}^\dagger=-i(-)^{i_x}\frac{\tau^3-\tau^1}{\sqrt2}$$

考虑定义
$$T_x=i\frac{\tau^3+\tau^1}{\sqrt2},T_y=i\frac{\tau^3-\tau^1}{\sqrt2}$$

从而$T_x^2=-\tau^0,T_y^2=-\tau^0$，故
$$W_{\pmb i+\pmb x}=T_xW_{\pmb i},W_{\pmb i+\pmb y}=(-)^{i_x}T_yW_{\pmb i}$$

我们猜测
$$W_{\pmb i}=T_x^{i_x}T_y^{i_y}$$

考虑验证这个$W$配置：
$$-i\sqrt2\chi W_{\pmb i}W_{\pmb i+\pmb x}^\dagger=-i\sqrt2\chi T_{x}^{i_x}T_y^{i_y}(-T_y)^{i_y}(-T_x)^{i_x+1}=-i\sqrt2\chi(-T_x)\\=-\chi(\tau^3+\tau^1)$$

$$-i\sqrt2\chi(-)^{i_x}W_{\pmb i}W_{\pmb i+\pmb y}^\dagger=-i\sqrt2\chi(-)^{i_x}T_x^{i_x}T_y^{i_y}(-T_y)^{i_y+1}(-T_x)^{i_x}\\=i\sqrt2\chi T_x^{i_x}T_yT_x^{i_x}$$

注意
$$T_xT_yT_x=-i\frac{1}{2\sqrt2}(\tau^3+\tau^1)(\tau^3-\tau^1)(\tau^3+\tau^1)\\=-i\frac{1}{2\sqrt2}(-2i\tau^2)(\tau^3+\tau^1)=-\frac{1}{\sqrt2}(i\tau_1-i\tau_3)=T_y$$

故
$$-i\sqrt2\chi(-)^{i_x}W_{\pmb i}W_{\pmb i+\pmb y}^\dagger=-\chi(\tau^3-\tau^1)$$

$$i\chi_2(-)^{i_x}W_{\pmb i}W_{\pmb i+\pmb x+\pmb y}^\dagger=i\chi_2(-)^{i_x}T_x^{i_x}T_y^{i_y}(-T_y)^{i_y+1}(-T_x)^{i_x+1}\\=i\chi_2T_yT_x=-\frac12i\chi_2(\tau^3-\tau^1)(\tau^3+\tau^1)=-\frac12i\chi_2(2i\tau_2)=\chi_2\tau_2$$

$$i\chi_2(-)^{i_x}W_{\pmb i}W_{\pmb i+\pmb x-\pmb y}^\dagger=i\chi_2(-)^{i_x}T_x^{i_x}T_y^{i_y}(-T_y)^{i_y+1}(-T_x)^{i_x+1}\\=-i\chi_2T_yT_x=-\chi_2\tau_2$$

所以取$\chi_2=\eta$即可。
