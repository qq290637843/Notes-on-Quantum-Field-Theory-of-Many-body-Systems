# 《多体系统的量子场论》7.4.2草稿

## 问题 7.4.4.
根据(7.4.5)
$$[\rho_k,\rho_{k'}]=\frac{\nu}{2\pi}k\delta_{k+k',0}$$

以及
$$\rho_k=\int dx\ \frac{1}{\sqrt L}\exp[ikx]\rho(x)$$

从而
$$\rho(x)=\frac{1}{\sqrt L}\sum_k\exp[-ikx]\rho_k$$

于是
$$[\rho(x_1),\rho(x_2)]=\frac1L\sum_{k_1,k_2}\exp[-ik_1x_1-ik_2x_2] [\rho_{k_1},\rho_{k_2}]\\=\frac1L\sum_{k_1}\exp[-ik_1x_1+ik_1x_2]\frac{\nu}{2\pi}k_1\\=i\frac{\nu}{2\pi}\partial_{x_1}\delta(x_1-x_2)$$

而根据$\rho(x)=\frac1{2\pi}\partial_x\phi(x)$，得
$$[\rho(x_1),\partial_{x_2}\phi(x_2)]=i\nu\partial_{x_1}\delta(x_1-x_2)$$

故
$$[\rho(x_1),\phi(x_2)]=-i\nu\delta(x_1-x_2)$$

故
$$[\partial_{x_1}\phi(x_1),\phi(x_2)]=-2\pi i\nu\delta(x_1-x_2)$$

所以
$$[\phi(x_2+0^+),\phi(x_2)]-[\phi(x_2-0^+),\phi(x_2)]=-2\pi i\nu$$

而注意到$[A,B]=-[B,A]$，故
$$[\phi(x_1),\phi(x_2)]=-i\pi\nu{\rm sgn}(x_1-x_2)$$

现在注意由于$[\phi(x_1),\phi(x_2)]$的结果是c-数，故
$$\Psi(x_1)\Psi(x_2)=\exp[i\phi(x_1)/\nu]\exp[i\phi(x_2)/\nu]\\=\exp[i\phi(x_2)/\nu]\exp[i\phi(x_1)/\nu]\exp[i\pi\nu{\rm sgn}(x_1-x_2)/\nu^2]\\=\Psi(x_2)\Psi(x_1)\exp[i\pi{\rm sgn}(x_1-x_2)/\nu]=(-1)^{1/\nu}\Psi(x_2)\Psi(x_1)$$

## 问题 7.4.5.
1.
首先
$$H_b=2\pi v\sum_{k>0}\rho_k^\dagger\rho_k$$

而
$$[\rho_k,\rho_{k'}^\dagger]=\frac1{2\pi}k\delta_{k,k'}$$

所以考虑定义
$$a_k=\sqrt{\frac{2\pi}{k}}\rho_k,a_k^\dagger=\sqrt{\frac{2\pi}{k}}\rho_{-k}$$

于是
$$H_b=\sum_{k>0}vka_k^\dagger a_k,[a_k,a_{k'}^\dagger]=\delta_{k,k'}$$

所以$n$号能级的状态数就是$n$的整数划分方案数，故$n=1,2,3,4,5$的方案数分别为$1,2,3,5,7$，和问题7.4.2一致。
2.
首先我们需要处理$p\neq q$时$:c_p^\dagger c_q:$的定义。显然其应该定义如下：
$$:c_p^\dagger c_q:=\begin{cases}c_p^\dagger c_q,p>0\land q>0\\-c_qc_p^\dagger,p<0\land q<0\\c_p^\dagger c_q,p>0\land q<0\\c_p^\dagger c_q,p<0\land q>0\end{cases}$$

总之就是说，在排序的时候要按照“海平面下讨论空穴，海平面上讨论粒子”为依据执行正规排序。所以至少在两个产生湮灭一次项相乘的时候有$:AB:=AB-\langle AB\rangle$，而这意味着只要$p\neq q$，则正规序记号可以直接去掉。
首先
$$c(x)=\frac1{\sqrt L}\sum_k\exp[ikx]c_k\\c^\dagger(x)=\frac1{\sqrt L}\sum_k\exp[-ikx]c_k^\dagger$$

于是
$$\rho_f(x)=\frac1L\sum_{k_1,k_2}\exp[ik_1x-ik_2x]:c_{k_2}^\dagger c_{k_1}:$$

于是（我们为了和正文中量纲一致，这里采用另一个定义）
$$\rho_{f,k}=\frac{1}{\sqrt L}\int dx\ \exp[-ikx]\rho_f(x)\\=\frac1{L\sqrt L}\int dx\ \sum_{k_1,k_2}\exp[-ikx+ik_1x-ik_2x]:c_{k_2}^\dagger c_{k_1}:\\=\frac{1}{\sqrt L}\sum_{k_1,k_2}\delta_{k-k_1+k_2,0}:c_{k_2}^\dagger c_{k_1}:=\frac1{\sqrt L}\sum_{q}:c_{q}^\dagger c_{q+k}:$$

于是（注意正规积和积只差一个c-数）
$$[\rho_{f,k},\rho_{f,k'}]=\frac1L\sum_{q,q'}[:c_q^\dagger c_{q+k}:,:c_{q'}^\dagger c_{q'+k'}:]\\=\frac1L\sum_{q,q'}[c_q^\dagger c_{q+k},c_{q'}^\dagger c_{q'+k'}]\\=\frac{1}{L}\sum_{q,q'}\left(c_q^\dagger[c_{q+k},c_{q'}^\dagger c_{q'+k'}]+[c_q^\dagger,c_{q'}^\dagger c_{q'+k'}]c_{q+k}\right)\\=\frac1L\sum_{q,q'}\left(c_q^\dagger\{c_{q+k},c_{q'}^\dagger\}c_{q'+k'}-c_{q'}^\dagger\{c_q^\dagger,c_{q'+k'}\}c_{q+k}\right)\\=\frac1L\sum_{q}c_q^\dagger c_{q+k+k'}-\frac1L\sum_{q}c_{q-k'}^\dagger c_{q+k}\\=\frac1L\sum_q\left(:c_q^\dagger c_{q+k+k'}:-:c_{q-k'}^\dagger c_{q+k}:\right)\\+\frac1L\sum_q\left\langle c_q^\dagger c_{q+k+k'}-c_{q-k'}^\dagger c_{q+k}\right\rangle$$

其中第一项因为是正规积，而正规积无论输入是什么低能态（也就是说只有有限个空穴和有限个激发粒子的态），只会有有限个下标配置方式输出非零，这意味着第一项可以执行下标平移，平移后得零，从而只剩第二项。

$$[\rho_{f,k},\rho_{f,k'}]=\frac1L\sum_q\left\langle c_q^\dagger c_{q+k+k'}-c_{q-k'}^\dagger c_{q+k}\right\rangle\\=\frac1L\delta_{k+k',0}\sum_q\left\langle c_q^\dagger c_q-c_{q+k}^\dagger c_{q+k}\right\rangle\\=\frac1L\delta_{k+k',0}\sum_q(n_q-n_{q+k})\\=\frac{1}{L}\delta_{k+k',0}\frac{L}{2\pi}k=\frac{k}{2\pi}\delta_{k+k',0}$$

3.
本题依旧采取定义$\rho_{f,k}=\frac{1}{\sqrt L}\int dx\ \exp[-ikx]\rho_f(x)$。

$$2\pi v\sum_{k>0}\rho_{f,k}^\dagger\rho_{f,k}=\frac{2\pi v}{L}\sum_{k>0}\sum_{p,q}:c_{p+k}^\dagger c_p::c_{q-k}^\dagger c_q:\\=\frac{2\pi v}{L}\sum_{k>0}\sum_{p,q}(c_{p+k}^\dagger c_p-\langle c_{p+k}^\dagger c_p\rangle)(c_{q-k}^\dagger c_q-\langle c_{q-k}^\dagger c_q\rangle)\\=\frac{2\pi v}{L}\sum_{k>0}\sum_{p,q}\left(:c_{p+k}^\dagger c_pc_{q-k}^\dagger c_q:+\langle c_{p+k}^\dagger c_p\rangle:c_{q-k}^\dagger c_q:\\+\langle c_{q-k}^\dagger c_q\rangle:c_{p+k}^\dagger c_p:-\langle c_{p+k}^\dagger c_{q-k}^\dagger\rangle:c_pc_q:\\-\langle c_pc_q\rangle:c_{p+k}^\dagger c_{q-k}^\dagger:+\langle c_{p+k}^\dagger c_q\rangle:c_pc_{q-k}^\dagger:\\+\langle c_pc_{q-k}^\dagger\rangle:c_{p+k}^\dagger c_q:+\langle c_{p+k}^\dagger c_p\rangle\langle c_{q-k}^\dagger c_q\rangle\\-\langle c_{p+k}^\dagger c_{q-k}^\dagger\rangle\langle c_pc_q\rangle+\langle c_pc_{q-k}^\dagger\rangle\langle c_{p+k}^\dagger c_q\rangle\\-\langle c_{p+k}^\dagger c_p\rangle:c_{q-k}^\dagger c_q:-\langle c_{p+k}^\dagger c_p\rangle\langle c_{q-k}^\dagger c_q\rangle\\-\langle c_{q-k}^\dagger c_q\rangle:c_{p+k}^\dagger c_p:-\langle c_{q-k}^\dagger c_q\rangle\langle c_{p+k}^\dagger c_p\rangle\\+\langle c_{p+k}^\dagger c_p\rangle\langle c_{q-k}^\dagger c_q\rangle\right)\\=\frac{2\pi v}{L}\sum_{k>0}\sum_{p,q}\left(:c_{p+k}^\dagger c_pc_{q-k}^\dagger c_q:+\delta_{p+k,q}n_q:c_pc_{q-k}^\dagger:\\+\delta_{p,q-k}(1-n_p):c_{p+k}^\dagger c_q:+\delta_{p,q-k}n_q(1-n_p)\right)$$

第四项：
$$\delta_{p,q-k}n_q(1-n_p)=\delta_{p+k,q}n_{p+k}(1-n_p)=0$$

第三项：
$$\sum_{k>0}\sum_{p,q}\delta_{p,q-k}(1-n_p):c_{p+k}^\dagger c_q:\\=\sum_{k>0}\sum_q(1-n_{q-k}):c_q^\dagger c_q:$$

第二项：
$$\sum_{k>0}\sum_{p,q}\delta_{p+k,q}n_q:c_pc_{q-k}^\dagger:\\=-\sum_{k>0}\sum_pn_{p+k}:c_p^\dagger c_p:$$

所以后三项总和为（这里$n_0=1$，因为真空的时候，$0$处确实塞了一个电子）
$$\frac{2\pi v}{L}\sum_q\sum_{k>0}(1-n_{q-k}-n_{q+k}):c_q^\dagger c_q:\\=\frac{2\pi v}{L}\sum_q\frac{Lq}{2\pi}:c_q^\dagger c_q:-\frac{2\pi v}{L}\sum_{q>0}c_q^\dagger c_q\\=H_f-\frac{2\pi v}{L}\sum_{q>0}c_q^\dagger c_q$$

接着处理第一项（同上一小问，由于这里是处理一整个正规乘积，所以可以随意修改求和的枚举方式）：
设
$$T_k=\sum_{p,q}:c_{p+k}^\dagger c_pc_{q-k}^\dagger c_q:$$

于是
$$\sum_{k>0}T_k+\sum_{k<0}T_k+T_0=-\sum_{P_1+P_2=P_3+P_4}:c_{P_1}^\dagger c_{P_2}^\dagger c_{P_3}c_{P_4}:=0$$

而
$$\sum_{k<0}T_k=\sum_{k>0}\sum_{p,q}:c_{q-k}^\dagger c_qc_{p+k}^\dagger c_p:=\sum_{k>0}T_k$$

所以$$\sum_{k>0}T_k=-\frac12T_0$$

最后注意
$$T_0=\sum_{p,q}:c_p^\dagger c_pc_q^\dagger c_q:$$

现在注意（对于四个费米算符$ABCD$）：
$$:ABCD:=ABCD-\langle AB\rangle:CD:-\langle CD\rangle:AB:+\langle AC\rangle:BD:\\+\langle BD\rangle:AC:-\langle AD\rangle:BC:-\langle BC\rangle:AD:\\-\langle AB\rangle\langle CD\rangle+\langle AC\rangle\langle BD\rangle-\langle AD\rangle\langle BC\rangle$$

$$:AB::CD:=(AB-\langle AB\rangle)(CD-\langle CD\rangle)\\=ABCD-\langle CD\rangle AB-\langle AB\rangle CD+\langle AB\rangle\langle CD\rangle\\=ABCD-\langle CD\rangle:AB:-\langle CD\rangle\langle AB\rangle-\langle AB\rangle:CD:-\langle AB\rangle\langle CD\rangle\\+\langle AB\rangle\langle CD\rangle$$

所以
$$:ABCD:=:AB::CD:+\langle AC\rangle:BD:+\langle BD\rangle:AC:-\langle AD\rangle:BC:\\-\langle BC\rangle:AD:+\langle AC\rangle\langle BD\rangle-\langle AD\rangle\langle BC\rangle$$

故（设$N=\sum_p:c_p^\dagger c_p:,N_{ex}=\sum_{p>0}:c_p^\dagger c_p:$，于是显然$N=0$且$N_{ex}=\sum_{p\leq0}(1-c_p^\dagger c_p)=-\sum_{p\leq0}:c_p^\dagger c_p:$，因为总电子数不变，注意这里$N_{ex}$实际上是指被从海里抬出去的粒子总数）
$$T_0=\sum_{p,q}\left(:c_p^\dagger c_p::c_q^\dagger c_q:+\langle c_p^\dagger c_q^\dagger\rangle:c_pc_q:+\langle c_pc_q\rangle:c_p^\dagger c_q^\dagger:\\-\langle c_p^\dagger c_q\rangle:c_pc_q^\dagger:-\langle c_pc_q^\dagger\rangle:c_p^\dagger c_q:+\langle c_p^\dagger c_q^\dagger\rangle\langle c_pc_q\rangle-\langle c_p^\dagger c_q\rangle\langle c_pc_q^\dagger\rangle\right)\\=N^2+\sum_{p,q}\left(\delta_{p,q}n_p:c_q^\dagger c_p:-\delta_{p,q}(1-n_p):c_p^\dagger c_q:-\delta_{p,q}n_p(1-n_p)\right)\\=N^2+\sum_{p\leq0}:c_p^\dagger c_p:-\sum_{p>0}:c_p^\dagger c_p:=N^2-2N_{ex}$$

故
$$\sum_{k>0}T_k=-\frac12N^2+N_{ex}$$

综上可得
$$H_b=H_f-\frac{2\pi v}{L}N_{ex}-\frac{\pi v}{L}N^2+\frac{2\pi v}{L}N_{ex}=H_f$$
