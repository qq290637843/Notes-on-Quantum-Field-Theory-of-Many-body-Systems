# 《多体系统的量子场论》3.8.2草稿

## 问题3.8.1.
欧几里得作用量为：
$$S_E=\int_0^\beta d\tau \left[\frac12m\left(\frac{dx}{d\tau}\right)^2+\frac12m\omega_0^2x^2\right]+g\int_0^\beta d\tau\ x^3$$

而自由传播子为：
$$\mathcal G_0^\beta(\tau)=\langle T_{\tau}x(\tau)x(0)\rangle_0^\beta=\frac{1}{2m\omega_0}\langle T_\tau(a(\tau)+a^\dagger(\tau))(a(0)+a^\dagger(0))\rangle_0^\beta$$

注意$$a(\tau)=\exp[\tau\omega_0(a^\dagger a+1/2)]a\exp[-\tau\omega_0(a^\dagger a+1/2)]\\=\exp[-\omega_0\tau]a$$

$$a^\dagger(\tau)=\exp[\tau\omega_0(a^\dagger a+1/2)]a^\dagger\exp[-\tau\omega_0(a^\dagger a+1/2)]\\=\exp[\omega_0\tau]a^\dagger$$

所以当$\tau>0$时：
$$\mathcal G_0^\beta(\tau)=\frac{1}{2m\omega_0}(\exp[-\omega_0\tau]\langle aa\rangle_0^\beta+\exp[-\omega_0\tau]\langle aa^\dagger\rangle_0^\beta\\+\exp[\omega_0\tau]\langle a^\dagger a\rangle_0^\beta+\exp[\omega_0\tau]\langle a^\dagger a^\dagger\rangle_0^\beta)\\=\frac{1}{2m\omega_0}(\exp[-\omega_0\tau]n_B+\exp[-\omega_0\tau]+\exp[\omega_0\tau]n_B)$$

当$\tau<0$时：
$$\mathcal G_0^\beta(\tau)=\frac{1}{2m\omega_0}(\exp[-\omega_0\tau]\langle aa\rangle_0^\beta+\exp[\omega_0\tau]\langle aa^\dagger\rangle_0^\beta\\+\exp[-\omega_0\tau]\langle a^\dagger a\rangle_0^\beta+\exp[\omega_0\tau]\langle a^\dagger a^\dagger\rangle_0^\beta)\\=\frac{1}{2m\omega_0}(\exp[-\omega_0\tau]n_B+\exp[\omega_0\tau]+\exp[\omega_0\tau]n_B)$$

故
$$\mathcal G_0^\beta(\tau)=\frac{1}{2m\omega_0}((1+n_B)\exp[-\omega_0|\tau|]+n_B\exp[\omega_0|\tau|])\\=\frac{1}{2m\omega_0}\left(\frac{\exp[-\omega_0|\tau|]}{1-\exp[-\beta\omega_0]}+\frac{\exp[\omega_0|\tau|]}{\exp[\beta\omega_0]-1}\right)\\=\frac1{2m\omega_0}\frac{\exp[(\beta-|\tau|)\omega_0]-\exp[-\omega_0|\tau|]+\exp[\omega_0|\tau|]-\exp[(|\tau|-\beta)\omega_0]}{\exp[\beta\omega_0]+\exp[-\beta\omega_0]-2}\\=\frac{1}{2m\omega_0}\frac{\sinh[\omega_0|\tau|]+\sinh[\omega_0(\beta-|\tau|)]}{\cosh[\beta\omega_0]-1}\\=\frac{1}{2m\omega_0}\frac{\cosh[\omega_0(\beta/2-|\tau|)]}{\sinh[\beta\omega_0/2]}$$

根据连通图定理可知：
$$\delta F=-\frac1\beta\frac1{2!}\left\langle\left(g\int_0^\beta d\tau\ x^3\right)^2\right\rangle_{0c}^\beta+O(g^3)\\=-\frac{g^2}{2\beta}\int_0^\beta d\tau_1\int_0^\beta d\tau_2\ \langle T_\tau x(\tau_1)^3x(\tau_2)^3\rangle_{0c}^\beta+O(g^3)\\=-\frac{g^2}{2}\int_0^\beta d\tau\ \langle T_\tau x(\tau)^3x(0)^3\rangle_{0c}^\beta+O(g^3)$$

分别算图的贡献

$$\delta F_A=-\frac{3!3!}{2!3!}g^2\int_0^\beta d\tau\ \mathcal G_0^\beta(\tau)^3\\=-3g^2\int_0^\beta d\tau\ \frac{\cosh[\omega_0(\beta/2-\tau)]^3}{(2m\omega_0\sinh[\beta\omega_0/2])^3}\\=-g^2\frac{5+\cosh[\beta\omega_0]}{8m^3\omega_0^4\sinh[\beta\omega_0/2]^2}$$

$$\delta F_B=-\frac{3!3!}{2\times2\times2}g^2\int_0^\beta d\tau\ \mathcal G_0^\beta(\tau)\mathcal G_0^\beta(0)^2\\=-\frac{9}{2}g^2\int_0^\beta d\tau\frac{\cosh[\omega_0(\beta/2-\tau)]\cosh[\omega_0\beta/2]^2}{(2m\omega_0\sinh[\beta\omega_0/2])^3}\\=-g^2\frac{9\cosh[\beta\omega_0/2]^2}{8m^3\omega_0^4\sinh[\beta\omega_0/2]^2}$$

故
$$\delta F=-g^2\frac{19+11\cosh[\beta\omega_0]}{8m^3\omega_0^4(\cosh[\beta\omega_0]-1)}$$

接着处理第二部分，这里用瞬子处理。

欧几里得作用量为
$$S_E=\int d\tau\ \left[\frac12m\left(\frac{dx}{d\tau}\right)^2+\frac12m\omega_0^2x^2+gx^3\right]$$

于是欧几里得运动方程为
$$m\frac{d^2x}{d\tau^2}=m\omega_0^2x+3gx^2$$

在反向势能场中的折返点为：
$$x_B=-\frac{m\omega_0^2}{2g}$$

于是回弹作用量为
$$S_B=\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}d\tau\ 2V(x)\\=2\int_{x_B}^0dx\frac{2V(x)}{\sqrt{2V(x)/m}}=2\sqrt{2m}\int_{x_B}^0dx\ \sqrt{V(x)}\\=\frac{2m^3\omega_0^5}{15g^2}$$

故
$$\Gamma\propto\exp\left[-\frac{2m^3\omega_0^5}{15g^2}\right]$$
