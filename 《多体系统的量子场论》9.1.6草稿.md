## 问题 9.1.6.
$$H_{\rm mean}=\sum_{\langle\pmb i\pmb j\rangle}\left[-\frac{J_{\pmb i\pmb j}}{2}(\chi_{\pmb j\pmb i}f_{\pmb i\alpha}^\dagger f_{\pmb j\alpha}+h.c.)+\frac{J_{\pmb i\pmb j}}{2}|\chi_{\pmb i\pmb j}|^2\right]\\=\sum_{\pmb i}\frac{J_1}{2}\chi_1^2+\sum_{\pmb i}\frac{J_1}{2}\chi_1^2+\sum_{\pmb i}\frac{J_2}{2}\chi_2^2+\sum_{\pmb i}\frac{J_2}{2}\chi_2^2+H_{\rm ex}$$

故
$$\langle H_{\rm mean}\rangle=-N\int_{\rm BZ}\frac{d^2\pmb k}{(2\pi)^2}\sqrt{J_1^2\chi_1^2(\sin^2[k_x]+\sin^2[k_y])+J_2^2\chi_2^2(\cos[k_x+k_y]+\cos[k_x-k_y])^2}\\+NJ_1\chi_1^2+NJ_2\chi_2^2\\=-N\int_{\rm BZ}\frac{d^2\pmb k}{(2\pi)^2}\sqrt{J_1^2\chi_1^2(\sin^2[k_x]+\sin^2[k_y])+4J_2^2\chi_2^2\cos^2[k_x]\cos^2[k_y]}+NJ_1\chi_1^2+NJ_2\chi_2^2$$

求导得极值方程为
$$2=J_1\int_{\rm BZ}\frac{d^2\pmb k}{(2\pi)^2}\frac{(\sin^2[k_x]+\sin^2[k_y])}{E_{\pmb k}}\\1=2J_2\int_{\rm BZ}\frac{d^2\pmb k}{(2\pi)^2}\frac{\cos^2[k_x]\cos^2[k_y]}{E_{\pmb k}}$$
