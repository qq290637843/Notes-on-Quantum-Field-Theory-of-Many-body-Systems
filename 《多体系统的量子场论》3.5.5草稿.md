# 《多体系统的量子场论》3.5.5草稿

注意，本节中提到紧致钟模型的涡。即使紧致了，也就是说$\theta$和$\theta+2\pi$被视为同一值，由于书中钟模型的构建并不是基于$\varphi$构建起来的，所以并没有$\varphi=0$的地方让$\theta$无值可取，从而直观上看，涡的存在必须允许$\theta$有非连续位，也就是奇点。但是实际上，这并不是问题，因为书中存在着截断$l$，这个截断$l$本身就提供了$\pmb x$空间的亏格，所以涡的奇点就是存在于截断$l$之内，也就是说，在这个意义上，$\theta$不需要承受这种不连续。

## 问题3.5.2.
$$\theta_l=\theta_\lambda+\delta_\theta$$

从而
$$S=\int d^2\pmb x\ \left(\frac{\kappa_l}{2}(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta_\lambda)^2+V_l(\theta_\lambda)+\frac{\kappa_l}{2}(\partial_{\pmb x}\delta\theta)^2\right)\\+\int d^2\pmb x\ \left(V_l'(\theta_\lambda)\delta\theta+\frac12V_l''(\theta_\lambda)(\delta\theta)^2\right)$$

故把$\delta\theta$积分掉得到
$$S=\int d^2\pmb x\ \left(\frac{\kappa_l}{2}(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta_\lambda)^2+V_l(\theta_\lambda)+\frac12V_l''(\theta_\lambda)K(\pmb0)\right)\\-\int d^2\pmb xd^2\pmb y\ \frac12V_l'(\theta_\lambda(\pmb x))K(\pmb x-\pmb y)V_l'(\theta_\lambda(\pmb y))$$

而
$$-\int d^2\pmb xd^2\pmb y\ \frac12V_l'(\theta_\lambda(\pmb x))K(\pmb x-\pmb y)V_l'(\theta_\lambda(\pmb y))\\=\int d^2\pmb xd^2\pmb y\ \frac14[V_l'(\theta_\lambda(\pmb x))-V_l'(\theta_\lambda(\pmb y))]^2K(\pmb x-\pmb y)\\-\int d^2\pmb x\ \frac12[V_l'(\theta_\lambda(\pmb x))]^2\bar K\\=\int d^2\pmb xd^2\pmb y\ \frac18[V_l''(\theta_\lambda(\pmb x))]^2(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta_\lambda(\pmb x))^2(\pmb x-\pmb y)^2K(\pmb x-\pmb y)\\-\int d^2\pmb x\ \frac12[V_l'(\theta_\lambda(\pmb x))]^2\bar K$$

故
$$V_\lambda=V_l+\frac12V_l''K(\pmb0)-\frac12V_l'^2\bar K$$

而我们默认$V_l$小，所以$V_l'^2$项扔掉，故
$$V_\lambda=V_l+\frac12V_l''K(\pmb0)$$

而
$$K(\pmb0)=\int_{2\pi/\lambda<|\pmb k|<2\pi/l}\frac{d^2\pmb k}{(2\pi)^2}\frac{1}{\kappa_l|\pmb k|^2}=\frac{1}{2\pi\kappa_l}\log[\lambda/l]$$

故$$\frac{dV_\lambda}{db}=\frac{1}{4\pi\kappa}V_\lambda''$$

考虑傅里叶：
$$V(\theta)=\sum_nv_n\exp[in\theta]$$

故
$$\frac{dv_n}{db}=-\frac{n^2}{4\pi\kappa}v_n$$

故
$$V_\lambda(\theta_\lambda)=\sum_nv_n\exp[in\theta]\exp\left[-\frac{n^2}{4\pi\kappa}b\right]$$

也就是说，高频部分会被抹平

## 问题3.5.3.
作用量为
$$S=\int d^2x\ \left(\frac\kappa2(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta)^2-g\cos\theta\right)$$

注意这里的磁场加入，直接破坏了涡的存在可能

①相关微扰：
本节不考虑$\kappa$的变化，于是$h=\frac{1}{2\kappa\pi}<2$，故随着$\lambda$增大，$gl^2$增长，而$gl^2\sim1$的尺度为：
$$\xi=l(g_ll^2)^{-4\pi\kappa/(8\pi\kappa-1)}$$

在达到该尺度时，系统处于强耦合有序相，自旋锁死在$x$方向，从而$\langle\cos\theta\rangle\sim1$

于是可知配分函数为
$$Z\simeq\exp(-S(\theta=0))=\exp\left(\int d^2\pmb x\ g_\xi\right)=\exp(\mathcal Vg_\xi)$$

注意在重整化流线上，$Z$不变，从而在$l$尺度
$$S_x=\frac{1}{\mathcal V}\frac{\partial\log Z}{\partial g_l}=\frac{\partial g_\xi}{\partial g_l}$$

注意$$g_\xi=g_l(\xi/l)^{-h}=g_l(g_ll^2)^{h/(2-h)}=g_l^{2/(2-h)}l^{2h/(2-h)}$$

故$S_x\sim g_l^{h/(2-h)}l^{2h(2-h)}$

②无关微扰：
由于是无关微扰，所以可以直接用标准的微扰论：
$$\langle\cos\theta(\pmb x)\rangle_g=\frac{\int\mathcal D\theta\ \exp[-S_0]\exp\left[\int d^2\pmb y\ g\cos\theta(\pmb y)\right]\cos\theta(\pmb x)}{\int\mathcal D\theta\ \exp[-S_0]\exp\left[\int d^2\pmb y\ g\cos\theta(\pmb y)\right]}\\=\frac{\langle\cos\theta(\pmb x)\rangle_0+g\int d^2\pmb y\ \langle\cos\theta(\pmb x)\cos\theta(\pmb y)\rangle_0}{1+g\int d^2\pmb y\ \langle\cos\theta(\pmb y)\rangle_0}\\=g\int d^2\pmb y\langle\cos\theta(\pmb x)\cos\theta(\pmb y)\rangle_0\\=g\int d^2\pmb y\left(\frac14\langle\exp[+i\theta(\pmb x)+i\theta(\pmb y)]\rangle_0+\frac14\langle\exp[+i\theta(\pmb x)-i\theta(\pmb y)]\rangle_0\\\frac14\langle\exp[-i\theta(\pmb x)+i\theta(\pmb y)]\rangle_0+\frac14\langle\exp[-i\theta(\pmb x)-i\theta(\pmb y)]\rangle_0\right)\\=g\int d^2\pmb y\ \left(\frac12\exp[-\mathcal G_\theta(\pmb0)-\mathcal G_\theta(\pmb y-\pmb x)]\\+\frac12\exp[-\mathcal G_\theta(\pmb0)+\mathcal G_\theta(\pmb y-\pmb x)]\right)\\=\frac g2\int d^2\pmb y\ \left(\frac{|\pmb y-\pmb x|l}{L^2}\right)^{2h}+\frac g2\int d^2\pmb y\ \left(\frac{l}{|\pmb y-\pmb x|}\right)^{2h}\\=\frac{g}{2}l^{2h}\frac{2\phi}{2+2h}\mathcal V^{1-h}+\pi gl^{2h}\frac{l^{2-2h}}{2h-2}\\=\frac{\pi}{2h-2}gl^2$$
