# 《多体系统的量子场论》9.10.2草稿

## 问题 9.10.1.
(a)
(9.6.1)是
$$u_{\pmb x}=\chi\tau^1-\eta\tau^2,u_{\pmb y}=\chi\tau^1+\eta\tau^2\\u_{\pmb x+\pmb y}=\gamma\tau^1,u_{-\pmb x+\pmb y}=\gamma\tau^1\\a_0^1\neq0,a_0^{2,3}=0$$

取$\gamma=0$，从而在动量空间的哈密顿量为
$$H(\pmb k)=\sum_{\pmb m}(\exp[i\pmb k\cdot\pmb m]u_{\pmb m}+h.c.)+a_0\\=2\cos(k_x)(\chi\tau^1-\eta\tau^2)+2\cos(k_y)(\chi\tau^1+\eta\tau^2)+a_0^1\tau^1\\=[2\chi(\cos k_x+\cos k_y)+a_0^1]\tau^1+2\eta(-\cos k_x+\cos k_y)\tau^2$$

所以无能隙位要满足
$$2\chi(\cos k_x+\cos k_y)+a_0^1=0,\cos k_x=\cos k_y$$

注意后者意味着零点必须在$k_y=k_x$或$k_y=-k_x$两条对角线上。

从而前者变成
$$\cos k_x=-\frac{a_0^1}{4\chi}$$

从而如果$|a_0^1|<4|\chi|$，那么布里渊区内有四个狄拉克点，分别在$(\pm k_0,\pm k_0)$，其中$\cos k_0=-a_0^1/4\chi$

如果$a_0^1=4\chi$，于是$k_x=k_y=\pm\pi$，于是原本四个狄拉克点在$(\pi,\pi)$处汇聚并湮灭

如果$a_0^1-4\chi$，于是$k_x=k_y=0$，于是原本四个狄拉克点在$(0,0)$处汇聚并湮灭

如果$|a_0^1|>4|\chi|$，那么能隙完全打开

现在注意能量密度为负能带全部填满，也就是
$$E_0=-\int\frac{d^2k}{(2\pi)^2}\sqrt{[2\chi(\cos k_x+\cos k_y)+a_0^1]^2+[2\eta(-\cos k_x+\cos k_y)]^2}$$

考虑以$a_0^1=-4\chi$为例说明奇异性。设$a_0^1=-4\chi-\delta$，于是
$$2\chi(\cos k_x+\cos k_y)+a_0^1=-\delta-\chi(k_x^2+k_y^2)+o(k^2)\\2\eta(-\cos k_x+\cos k_y)=\eta(k_y^2-k_x^2)+o(k^2)$$

从而在$\pmb k=0$附近
$$E(\pmb k)\simeq\sqrt{(\delta+\chi|\pmb k|^2)^2+\eta^2(k_x^2-k_y^2)^2}$$

从而
$$\frac{\partial^2E(\pmb k)}{\partial\delta^2}\bigg|_{\delta=0}\sim\frac{1}{k^2}$$

于是
$$\frac{\partial^2E_0}{\partial\delta^2}\sim-\int\frac{kdk}{k^2}\sim\log\Lambda$$

(b)
当$a_0^1=-4\chi$，于是
$$E(\pmb k)\simeq\sqrt{\chi^2(k_x^2+k_y^2)^2+\eta^2(k_x^2-k_y^2)^2}$$

## 问题 9.10.2.
(a)
$Z2A003n$即$Z2A(\tau_+^0)(\tau_+^0)(\tau^3)(\tau_-^0)$

故
$$G_x(\pmb i)=\tau^0,G_y(\pmb i)=\tau^0\\G_{P_x}(\pmb i)=\tau^0,G_{P_y}(\pmb i)=\tau^0\\G_{P_{xy}}(\pmb i)=i\tau^3,G_{T}(\pmb i)=(-)^{i_x+i_y}\tau^0$$

(b)
从$G_x(\pmb i)=\tau^0,G_y(\pmb i)=\tau^0$可知拟设具有平移不变性，故$H(\pmb k)$是在标准第一布里渊区定义的
从$G_{P_x}(\pmb i)=\tau^0,G_{P_y}(\pmb i)=\tau^0$可知哈密顿量需要满足
$$H(-k_x,k_y)=H(k_x,k_y)=H(k_x,-k_y)$$

故$\epsilon^0,\epsilon^1,\epsilon^2,\epsilon^3$都是关于$k_x$和$k_y$的偶函数，从而只能是$\cos(n_xk_x)\cos(n_yk_y)$这样的项的和。

从$G_T(\pmb i)=(-)^{i_x+i_y}\tau^0$可知
$$H(k_x+\pi,k_y+\pi)=-H(k_x,k_y)$$

所以$n_x+n_y$必须为奇数

从$G_{P_{xy}}(\pmb i)=i\tau^3$可知
$$\tau^3H(k_x,k_y)\tau^3=H(k_y,k_x)$$

故
$$\epsilon^0(k_x,k_y)=\epsilon^0(k_y,k_x),\epsilon^3(k_x,k_y)=\epsilon^3(k_y,k_x),\epsilon^2(k_x,k_y)=-\epsilon^2(k_y,k_x),\epsilon^1(k_x,k_y)=-\epsilon^1(k_y,k_x)$$

综上可知
$$\epsilon^0(\pmb k)=\sum_{n_x+n_y奇}f_{n_xn_y}^0(\cos(n_xk_x)\cos(n_yk_y)+\cos(n_xk_y)\cos(n_yk_x))$$

$$\epsilon^1(\pmb k)=\sum_{n_x+n_y奇}f_{n_xn_y}^1(\cos(n_xk_x)\cos(n_yk_y)+\cos(n_xk_y)\cos(n_yk_x))$$

$$\epsilon^2(\pmb k)=\sum_{n_x+n_y奇}f_{n_xn_y}^2(\cos(n_xk_x)\cos(n_yk_y)-\cos(n_xk_y)\cos(n_yk_x))$$

$$\epsilon^3(\pmb k)=\sum_{n_x+n_y奇}f_{n_xn_y}^3(\cos(n_xk_x)\cos(n_yk_y)-\cos(n_xk_y)\cos(n_yk_x))$$

(c)
根据$T$变换知道
$$\epsilon^\mu(\pi/2,\pi/2)=-\epsilon^\mu(-\pi/2,-\pi/2),\epsilon^\mu(\pi/2,-\pi/2)=-\epsilon^\mu(-\pi/2,\pi/2)$$

根据$P_x,P_y$知道
$$\epsilon^\mu(\pi/2,\pi/2)=\epsilon^\mu(\pi/2,-\pi/2)=\epsilon^\mu(-\pi/2,-\pi/2)=\epsilon^\mu(-\pi/2,\pi/2)$$

所以都是零。
