关于(3.3.8)：

$$\partial\varphi=\partial(\sqrt{\rho_0+\delta\rho}\exp[i\theta])=\\i\sqrt{\rho_0+\delta\rho}\exp[i\theta]\partial\theta+\frac12(\rho_0+\delta\rho)^{-1/2}\exp[i\theta]\partial\delta\rho$$

$$S=\int d^d\pmb x\ dt\ \left[\frac i2\left(i(\rho_0+\delta\rho)\partial_t\theta+\frac12\partial_t\delta\rho\\+i(\rho_0+\delta\rho)\partial_t\theta-\frac12\partial_t\delta\rho\right)\\-\frac{1}{2m}\left((\rho_0+\delta\rho)\partial_{\pmb x}\theta\partial_{\pmb x}\theta+\frac14(\rho_0+\delta\rho)^{-1}\partial_{\pmb x}\delta\rho\partial_{\pmb x}\delta\rho\right)\\+\mu(\rho_0+\delta\rho)-\frac{V_0}{2}(\rho_0+\delta\rho)^2\right]\\=\int d^d\pmb x\ dt\ \left[-(\rho_0+\delta\rho)\partial_t\theta-\frac1{2m}\rho_0(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta)^2-\frac{1}{8m\rho_0}(\partial_{\pmb x}\delta\rho)^2\\+\mu\rho_0+\mu\delta\rho-\frac{V_0}{2}\rho_0^2-V_0\rho_0\delta\rho-\frac{V_0}{2}\delta\rho^2\right]\\=\int d^d\pmb x\ dt\ \left[-(\rho_0+\delta\rho)\partial_t\theta-\frac{\rho_0(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta)^2}{2m}-\frac{(\partial_{\pmb x}\delta\rho)^2}{8m\rho_0}-\frac{V_0}{2}\delta\rho^2+\frac\mu2\rho_0\right]$$

取实部并保留一次项得：

## 问题 3.3.3
(a)
根据(3.3.8)式，$\delta\rho$的运动方程为：
$$-\partial_t\theta+\frac{\partial_{\pmb x}^2\delta\rho}{4m\rho_0}-V_0\delta\rho=0$$

故$$\delta\rho=\frac{\partial_t\theta}{\frac{\partial_{\pmb x}^2}{4m\rho_0}-V_0}\simeq-\frac{\partial_t\theta}{V_0}$$

于是（注意在长波低能近似下，$\partial_{\pmb x}$和$\partial_t$都是一阶小量。
$$S=\int d^d\pmb x\ dt\ \left[\frac{(\partial_t\theta)^2}{2V_0}-\frac{\rho_0(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta)^2}{2m}\right]$$

(b)
$$\partial_t\rho+\partial_{\pmb x}\cdot\pmb j=-\frac{\partial_t^2\theta}{V_0}+\frac{\rho_0}{m}\partial_{\pmb x}^2\theta$$

而$\theta$的运动方程就是
$$-\frac{\partial_t^2\theta}{V_0}+\frac{\rho_0\partial_{\pmb x}^2\theta}{m}=0$$

(c)
注意对易关系为$[\partial_t\theta(\pmb x,t),\theta(\pmb0,0)]=-iV_0\delta(t)\delta^{(d)}(\pmb x)$

于是$$\partial_t(T\theta(\pmb x,t)\theta(\pmb0,0))=\partial_t(\Theta(t)\theta(\pmb x,t)\theta(\pmb0,0)+\Theta(-t)\theta(\pmb0,0)\theta(\pmb x,t))\\=\delta(t)\theta(\pmb x,t)\theta(\pmb0,0)+\Theta(t)\partial_t(\pmb x,t)\theta(\pmb0,0)\\-\delta(t)\theta(\pmb0,0)\theta(\pmb x,t)+\Theta(-t)\theta(\pmb0,0)\partial_t(\pmb x,t)\\=\Theta(t)\partial_t(\pmb x,t)\theta(\pmb0,0)+\Theta(-t)\theta(\pmb0,0)\partial_t(\pmb x,t)$$

$$\partial_t^2(T\theta(\pmb x,t)\theta(\pmb0,0))=\delta(t)\partial_t(\pmb x,t)\theta(\pmb0,0)+\Theta(t)\partial_t^2(\pmb x,t)\theta(\pmb0,0)\\-\delta(t)\theta(\pmb0,0)\partial_t(\pmb x,t)+\Theta(-t)\theta(\pmb0,0)\partial_t^2(\pmb x,t)$$

故
$$\left(\frac{\partial_t^2}{V_0}-\frac{\rho_0\partial_{\pmb x}^2}{m}\right)\langle\theta(\pmb x,t)\theta(\pmb0,0)\rangle=-i\delta(t)\delta^{(d)}(\pmb x)$$

而$$\langle\rho(\pmb x,t)\rho(\pmb0,0)\rangle=-\frac{1}{V_0^2}\partial_t^2\langle\theta(\pmb x,t)\theta(\pmb0,0)\rangle$$

故$$\left(\frac{\partial_t^2}{V_0}-\frac{\rho_0\partial_{\pmb x}^2}{m}\right)\langle\rho(\pmb x,t)\rho(\pmb0,0)\rangle=\frac{i}{V_0^2}\partial_t^2\delta(t)\delta^{(d)}(\pmb x)$$

故
$$\left(-\frac{\omega^2}{V_0}+\frac{\rho_0k^2}{m}-i0^+\right)iG_{\pmb k,\omega}=-\frac{i\omega^2}{V_0^2}$$

从而

$$iG_{\pmb k,\omega}=\frac{i\omega^2}{V_0\omega^2-V_0^2\rho_0k^2/m+i0^+}$$
