# 《多体系统的量子场论》7.3.2草稿

## 问题 7.3.1.
$$\mathcal L=-\frac12\frac{m}{2\pi}a_\mu\partial_\lambda a_\nu\epsilon^{\mu\lambda\nu}+(j_1^\mu+j_2^\mu)a_\mu$$

从而对$a_\mu$执行高斯积分后得
$$\mathcal L=\frac12j^\mu\frac{1}{\frac m{2\pi}\partial_\lambda\epsilon^{\mu\lambda\nu}}j^\nu$$

从而
$$\mathcal L=\frac12j_1^\mu\frac{1}{\frac m{2\pi}\partial_\lambda\epsilon^{\mu\lambda\nu}}j_1^\nu+\frac12j_2^\mu\frac{1}{\frac m{2\pi}\partial_\lambda\epsilon^{\mu\lambda\nu}}j_2^\nu\\+j_1^\mu\frac{1}{\frac m{2\pi}\partial_\lambda\epsilon^{\mu\lambda\nu}}j_2^\nu$$

设
$$\frac m{2\pi}\partial_\lambda\epsilon^{\mu\lambda\nu}f_\nu=j_2^\mu$$

固定$j_2$所对应粒子在$(0,0)$处，故
$$j_2^0=l_2\delta(\pmb x),j_2^i=0$$

于是
$$\frac{m}{2\pi}\pmb\nabla\times\pmb f=l_2\delta(\pmb x),f_0=0$$

于是
$$\oint \pmb f\cdot d\pmb l=\int(\pmb\nabla\times\pmb f)d^2\pmb x=\frac{2\pi}{m}\int l_2\delta(\pmb x)d^2\pmb x=\frac{2\pi l_2}{m}$$

注意$j_1$粒子绕转一圈的相互作用累积相位为
$$\int d^3\pmb x\ j_1^\mu f_\mu=\int d^3x\ l_1\dot{\pmb x}_1(t)\cdot\pmb f(\pmb x)\delta(\pmb x-\pmb x_1(t))\\=\int dt\ l_1\dot{\pmb x}_1(t)\cdot\pmb f(\pmb x_1(t))=l_1\oint\pmb f\cdot d\pmb x_1=\frac{2\pi l_1l_2}{m}$$

## 问题 7.3.2.
考虑对
$$\mathcal L=-\frac{m}{4\pi}\epsilon^{\mu\nu\lambda}a_\mu\partial_\nu a_\lambda+\frac{1}{2g_1}\pmb e^2-\frac{1}{2g_2}b^2$$求变分。
1.处理$a_0$：
$$\frac{\partial\mathcal L}{\partial a_0}=\partial_\mu\frac{\partial\mathcal L}{\partial(\partial_\mu a_0)}$$

注意
$$\pmb e^2=(\partial_0a_i-\partial_ia_0)(\partial_0a_i-\partial_ia_0)\\=\dot{\pmb a}\cdot\dot{\pmb a}-2\dot{\pmb a}\cdot\pmb\nabla a_0+\pmb\nabla a_0\cdot\pmb\nabla a_0$$

故
$$\pmb\nabla\cdot\frac{\partial\pmb e^2}{\partial(\pmb\nabla a_0)}=-2\pmb\nabla\cdot\pmb e$$

接着
$$\epsilon^{\mu\nu\lambda}a_\mu\partial_\nu a_\lambda\\=a_0(\pmb\nabla\times\pmb a)+a_1(\partial_2a_0-\partial_0a_2)+a_2(\partial_0a_1-\partial_1a_0)$$

故
$$\frac{\partial\epsilon^{\mu\nu\lambda}a_\mu\partial_\nu a_\lambda}{\partial a_0}=\pmb b,\pmb\nabla\cdot\frac{\partial\epsilon^{\mu\nu\lambda}a_\mu\partial_\nu a_\lambda}{\partial(\pmb\nabla a_0)}=-\pmb b$$

故方程为
$$-\frac{m}{4\pi}\pmb b=-\frac1{g_1}\pmb\nabla\cdot\pmb e+\frac{m}{4\pi}\pmb b$$

即
$$\pmb\nabla\cdot\pmb e=\frac{g_1m}{2\pi}\pmb b$$

2.处理$\pmb a$：
注意
$$b^2=(\partial_1a_2-\partial_2a_1)(\partial_1a_2-\partial_2a_1)\\=(\partial_1a_2)^2+(\partial_2a_1)^2-2\partial_1a_2\partial_2a_1$$

故
$$\pmb\nabla\cdot\frac{\partial b^2}{\partial(\pmb\nabla a_1)}=-2\partial_2b,\pmb\nabla\cdot\frac{\partial b^2}{\partial(\pmb\nabla a_2)}=2\partial_1b$$

$$\partial_0\frac{\partial\pmb e^2}{\partial(\partial_0\pmb a)}=2\dot{\pmb e}\\\frac{\partial\epsilon^{\mu\nu\lambda}a_\mu\partial_\nu a_\lambda}{\partial a_1}=-e_2,\frac{\partial\epsilon^{\mu\nu\lambda}a_\mu\partial_\nu a_\lambda}{\partial a_2}=e_1\\\partial_\xi\frac{\partial\epsilon^{\mu\nu\lambda}a_\mu\partial_\nu a_\lambda}{\partial(\partial_\xi a_1)}=e_2,\partial_\xi\frac{\partial\epsilon^{\mu\nu\lambda}a_\mu\partial_\nu a_\lambda}{\partial(\partial_\xi a_2)}=-e_1$$

从而方程为
$$\frac{m}{4\pi}e_2=\frac{1}{g_2}\partial_2b+\frac{1}{g_1}\dot e_1-\frac{m}{4\pi}e_2\\-\frac{m}{4\pi}e_1=-\frac{1}{g_2}\partial_1b+\frac{1}{g_1}\dot e_2+\frac{m}{4\pi}e_1$$

即
$$\frac{m}{2\pi}\pmb e=\frac{1}{g_1}\hat{\pmb z}\times\dot{\pmb e}+\frac{1}{g_2}\pmb\nabla b$$

即
$$\frac{1}{g_1}\dot{\pmb e}=\frac1{g_2}\hat{\pmb z}\times\pmb\nabla b-\frac{m}{2\pi}\hat{\pmb z}\times\pmb e$$

3.注意根据$\pmb e$和$b$的定义还有
$$\dot b-\pmb\nabla\times\pmb e=0$$

方程整理到一起为
$$\begin{cases}\pmb\nabla\cdot\pmb e=\frac{g_1m}{2\pi}b\\\dot b=\pmb\nabla\times\pmb e\\\dot{\pmb e}=\frac{g_1}{g_2}\hat{\pmb z}\times\pmb\nabla b-\frac{g_1m}{2\pi}\hat{\pmb z}\times\pmb e\end{cases}$$

从而
$$\ddot b=\pmb\nabla\times\left[\frac{g_1}{g_2}\hat{\pmb z}\times\pmb\nabla b-\frac{g_1m}{2\pi}\hat{\pmb z}\times\pmb e\right]\\=\frac{g_1}{g_2}\pmb\nabla^2b-\left(\frac{g_1m}{2\pi}\right)^2b$$

故色散关系为
$$\omega^2=\frac{g_1}{g_2}|\pmb k|^2+\left(\frac{g_1m}{2\pi}\right)^2$$

故能隙为$$\frac{g_1m}{2\pi}$$

现在来推$g_1$和$g_2$。
题干中说能隙数量级为$\frac{e^2}{m^2l_B}$，而准空穴尺寸数量级为$l_B$，故
$$\frac{g_1m}{2\pi}\sim\frac{e^2}{m^2l_B}$$

即
$$g_1\sim\frac{2\pi e^2}{m^3l_B}$$

而准空穴尺寸即静态准空穴的$b$衰减长度。注意此时第三个方程就是
$$\pmb e=\frac{2\pi}{mg_2}\pmb\nabla b$$

所以
$$\frac{g_1m}{2\pi}b=\frac{2\pi}{mg_2}\pmb\nabla^2b$$

故
$$\pmb\nabla^2b=\frac{g_1g_2m^2}{4\pi^2}b$$

所以
$$\xi=\frac{2\pi}{m\sqrt{g_1g_2}}$$

故
$$\frac{2\pi}{m\sqrt{g_1g_2}}\sim l_B$$

故
$$g_2\sim\frac{2\pi m}{e^2l_B}$$
