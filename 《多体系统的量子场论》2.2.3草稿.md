# 《多体系统的量子场论》2.2.3草稿

有效作用量$$L_{\rm eff}=\frac{1}{2g}(\dot{\mathcal E}^2-\omega_{CL}^2\mathcal E^2)+\frac{e^2}{2m}\mathcal E\left(\frac{d^2}{dt^2}+\omega_0^2\right)^{-1}\mathcal E$$

中，最后那个部分：
$$\left(\frac{d^2}{dt^2}+\omega_0^2\right)^{-1}=\omega_0^{-2}\left(1+\frac{1}{\omega_0^2}\frac{d^2}{dt^2}\right)^{-1}=\omega_0^{-2}\left(1-\frac{1}{\omega_0^2}\frac{d^2}{dt^2}+...\right)$$

由于$\omega_0\gg\omega_{CL}$，所以$\frac{1}{\omega_0^{2n}}\frac{d^{2n}}{dt^{2n}}\mathcal E$可以忽略不记，故最后的近似是把括号中的$\frac{d^2}{dt^2}$丢掉。

## 问题 2.2.3.
$$Z=\int\mathcal Dx\ \exp\left[i\int dt\ \left(\frac12m\dot x^2-\frac12m\omega^2x^2\right)\right]\\\times\int\mathcal DX\ \exp\left[i\int dt\ \left(\frac12M\dot X^2-\frac12M\Omega^2X^2-gxX\right)\right]$$

设
$$Z[x]=\int \mathcal DX\ \exp\left[i\int dt\ \left(\frac12M\dot X^2-\frac12M\Omega^2X^2-gxX\right)\right]$$

借助于
$$\int\prod_{k=1}^{N-1}dx_k\ \exp\left(-\frac12\pmb x^TA\pmb x+\pmb J^T\pmb x\right)=\sqrt{\frac{(2\pi)^{N-1}}{\det A}}\exp\left(\frac12\pmb J^TA^{-1}\pmb J\right)$$

$$\frac12M\dot X^2-\frac12M\Omega^2X^2\to-\frac12MX\left(\frac{d^2}{dt^2}+\Omega^2\right)X$$

于是得
$$\frac{Z[x]}{Z[0]}=\exp\left(i\int dt\ \frac12g^2M^{-1}x\left(\frac{d^2}{dt^2}+\Omega^2\right)^{-1}x\right)$$

故
$$Z=Z[0]\int\mathcal Dx\ \exp\left[i\int dt\ \left(-\frac12mx\left(\frac{d^2}{dt^2}+\omega^2\right)x\\+\frac12g^2M^{-1}x\left(\frac{d^2}{dt^2}+\Omega^2\right)^{-1}x\right)\right]$$

故$$L_0=-\frac12mx\left(\frac{d^2}{dt^2}+\omega^2\right)x\\L_I=\frac12g^2M^{-1}x\left(\frac{d^2}{dt^2}+\Omega^2\right)^{-1}x\\=\frac12g^2M^{-1}x\Omega^{-2}\left(1-\frac{d^2}{\Omega^2dt^2}+\frac{d^4}{\Omega^4dt^4}+...\right)x\\\simeq\frac12g^2M^{-1}\Omega^{-2}x^2-\frac12g^2M^{-1}\Omega^{-4}x\frac{d^2}{dt^2}x$$

从而$$m^*\simeq m+g^2M^{-1}\Omega^{-4}\\m^*(\omega^*)^2\simeq m\omega^2-g^2M^{-1}\Omega^{-2}$$
