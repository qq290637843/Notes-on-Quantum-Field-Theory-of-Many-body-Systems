# 《多体系统的量子场论》4.2.3草稿

## 问题4.2.5.
根据(4.2.3)
$$iG(t,k)=\Theta(t)\Theta(\xi_k)\exp[-it\xi_k]-\Theta(-t)\Theta(-\xi_k)\exp[-it\xi_k]$$

于是
$$iG(t,x)=\int\frac{dk}{2\pi}iG(t,k)\exp[ikx]\\=\Theta(t)\int\frac{dk}{2\pi}\Theta(\xi_k)\exp[ikx-it\xi_k]\\-\Theta(-t)\int\frac{dk}{2\pi}\Theta(-\xi_k)\exp[ikx-it\xi_k]$$

当$t$趋于无穷，可知$\xi_k$必须约等于零才有贡献，故考虑在费米点附近将能谱线性化：
在$+k_F$附近，设$k=k_F+q,\xi_k\simeq v_Fq$
在$-k_F$附近，设$k=-k_F+q,\xi_k\simeq -v_Fq$

将积分为$t>0$和$t<0$两种情况：
当$t>0$：
于是
$$iG(t,x)=\int_{|k|>k_F}\frac{dk}{2\pi}\exp[ikx-i\xi_kt]\\\simeq\int_0^\infty\frac{dq}{2\pi}\exp[ik_Fx+iqx-iv_Fqt]\\+\int_{-\infty}^0\frac{dq}{2\pi}\exp[-ik_Fx+iqx+iv_Fqt]\\=\exp[ik_Fx]\int_0^\infty\frac{dq}{2\pi}\exp[(ix-iv_Ft-0^+)q]\\+\exp[-ik_Fx]\int_0^\infty\frac{dq}{2\pi}\exp[(-ix-iv_Ft-0^+)q]\\=\frac1{2\pi}\left(\frac{\exp[ik_Fx]}{iv_Ft-ix+0^+}+\frac{\exp[-ik_Fx]}{iv_Ft+ix+0^+}\right)$$

当$t<0$：
于是
$$iG(t,x)=-\int_{|k|<k_F}\frac{dk}{2\pi}\exp[ikx-i\xi_kt]\\\simeq-\int_0^\infty\frac{dq}{2\pi}\exp[-ik_Fx+iqx+iv_Fqt]\\-\int_{-\infty}^0\frac{dq}{2\pi}\exp[ik_Fx+iqx-iv_Fqt]\\=-\exp[-ik_Fx]\int_0^\infty\frac{dq}{2\pi}\exp[(ix+iv_Ft-0^+)q]\\-\exp[ik_Fx]\int_0^\infty\frac{dq}{2\pi}\exp[(-ix+iv_Ft-0^+)q]\\=\frac1{2\pi}\left(\frac{\exp[ik_Fx]}{iv_Ft-ix-0^+}+\frac{\exp[-ik_Fx]}{iv_Ft+ix-0^+}\right)$$

故
$$G(t,x)=\frac{1}{2\pi}\left(\frac{\exp[ik_Fx]}{x-v_Ft+i{\rm sgn}(t)0^+}-\frac{\exp[-ik_Fx]}{x+v_Ft-i{\rm sgn}(t)0^+}\right)$$

当$t/x\to0$，有
$$G(t,x)\to\frac1{2\pi}\left(\frac{\exp[ik_Fx]}{x}-\frac{\exp[-ik_Fx]}{x}\right)=\frac{i}{\pi}\frac{\sin[k_Fx]}{x}$$

当$t/x\to\infty$，有
$$G(t,x)\to\frac1{2\pi}\left(-\frac{\exp[ik_Fx]}{v_Ft}-\frac{\exp[-ik_Fx]}{v_Ft}\right)=-\frac{1}{\pi v_Ft}$$

## 问题4.2.6.
$$iG_I(t)=\langle Tc_L(t,0)c_R^\dagger(t,0)c_R(0,0)c_L^\dagger(0,0)\rangle\\+\langle Tc_L(t,a)c_R^\dagger(t,a)c_R(0,0)c_L^\dagger(0,0)\rangle\\+\langle Tc_L(t,a)c_R^\dagger(t,a)c_R(0,a)c_L^\dagger(0,a)\rangle\\+\langle Tc_L(t,0)c_R^\dagger(t,0)c_R(0,a)c_L^\dagger(0,a)\rangle$$

于是当$t>0$，有
$$iG_I(t)=iG(t,0)(-i)G(-t,0)+iG(t,a)(-i)G(-t,-a)\\+iG(t,0)(-i)G(-t,0)+iG(t,-a)(-i)G(-t,a)\\=2G(t,0)G(-t,0)+G(t,a)G(-t,-a)+G(t,-a)G(-t,a)$$

而
$$G(t,x)G(-t,-x)=\frac1{4\pi^2}\left(\frac{\exp[ik_Fx]}{x-v_Ft+i0^+}-\frac{\exp[-ik_Fx]}{x+v_Ft-i0^+}\right)\left(\frac{\exp[-ik_Fx]}{-x+v_Ft-i0^+}-\frac{\exp[ik_Fx]}{-x-v_Ft+i0^+}\right)\\=\frac{1}{4\pi^2}\left(-\frac{1}{(x-v_Ft+i0^+)^2}-\frac{1}{(x+v_Ft-i0^+)^2}+\frac{2\cos[2k_Fx]}{x^2-(v_Ft-i0^+)^2}\right)$$

故
$$iG_I(t)=-\frac{2}{\pi^2(-v_Ft+i0^+)^2}+\frac{1}{2\pi^2}\left(-\frac{1}{(a-v_Ft+i0^+)^2}-\frac{1}{(a+v_Ft-i0^+)^2}+\frac{2\cos[2k_Fa]}{a^2-(v_Ft-i0^+)^2}\right)$$

故$${\rm Re}G_I(t)={\rm Im}(iG_I(t))=-\frac{2}{\pi}\delta'(-v_Ft)+\frac{1}{2\pi}\left(-\delta'(a-v_Ft)-\delta'(-a-v_Ft)-2\cos[2k_Fa]\delta(a^2-v_F^2t^2)\right)\\=-\frac2{\pi v_F^2}\delta'(-t)+\frac1{2\pi v_F^2}\left(-\delta'(a/v_F-t)-\delta'(-a/v_F-t)\\-\frac{\cos[2k_Fa]v_F}{a}\delta(t-a/v_F)-\frac{\cos[2k_Fa]v_F}{a}\delta(t+a/v_F)\right)\\=-\frac{2}{\pi v_F^2}\delta'(-t)-\frac{1}{2\pi v_F^2}\delta'(a/v_F-t)-\frac{\cos[2k_Fa]}{2\pi av_F}\delta(a/v_F-t)$$

所以$$D_I(t)=-\Theta(t)\frac{4}{\pi v_F^2}\delta'(-t)-\frac{1}{\pi v_F^2}\delta'(a/v_F-t)-\frac{\cos[2k_Fa]}{2\pi av_F}\delta(a/v_F-t)$$

所以$$D_I(\omega)=-\frac{2i\omega}{\pi v_F^2}-\frac{i\omega}{\pi v_F^2}\exp[i\omega a/v_F]-\frac{\cos[2k_F a]}{\pi av_F}\exp[i\omega a/v_F]$$

于是
$$\langle j_T\rangle(t)=2\Gamma^2{\rm Im}D_I(V)=-\frac{4V\Gamma^2}{\pi v_F^2}-\frac{2V\Gamma^2\sin[Va/v_F]}{\pi v_F^2}-\frac{2\Gamma^2\cos[2k_Fa]\sin[Va/v_F]}{\pi av_F}$$

## 问题4.2.7.
$$\int d\omega\ A_+(\omega,\pmb k)=\\\sum_{n,m}\frac{\exp[-\beta\epsilon_n]}{Z}\langle\psi_n|c_{\pmb k}|\psi_m\rangle\langle\psi_m|c_{\pmb k}^\dagger|\psi_n\rangle\int d\omega\ \delta(\omega-(\epsilon_m-\epsilon_n))\\=\sum_n\frac{\exp[-\beta\epsilon_n]}{Z}\langle\psi_n|c_{\pmb k}c_{\pmb k}^\dagger|\psi_n\rangle$$

$$\int d\omega\ A_-(\omega,\pmb k)=\\\sum_{n,m}\frac{\exp[-\beta\epsilon_n]}{Z}\langle\psi_n|c_{\pmb k}^\dagger|\psi_m\rangle\langle\psi_m|c_{\pmb k}|\psi_n\rangle\int d\omega\ \delta(\omega-(\epsilon_m-\epsilon_n))\\=\sum_n\frac{\exp[-\beta\epsilon_n]}{Z}\langle\psi_n|c_{\pmb k}^\dagger c_{\pmb k}|\psi_n\rangle$$

## 问题4.2.8.
1.
注意隧穿哈密顿量只有同动量才有匹配。所以
$$\langle j_T\rangle(t)=\frac{1}{2\pi}A^2S\int d\nu\ d^2\pmb k\ (A_{L-}(\nu,\pmb k)A_{R+}(\nu-V,\pmb k)\\-A_{L+}(\nu,\pmb k)A_{R-}(\nu-V,\pmb k))\\=\frac1{2\pi}A^2S\int d\nu\ d^2\pmb k\ (n_F(\xi_{\pmb k})\delta(\nu-\xi_{\pmb k})(1-n_F(\xi_{\pmb k}))\delta(\nu-V-\xi_{\pmb k})\\-(1-n_F(\xi_{\pmb k}))\delta(\nu-\xi_{\pmb k})n_F(\xi_{\pmb k})\delta(\nu-V-\xi_{\pmb k}))=0$$

2.
$$\langle j_T\rangle(t)=\frac1{2\pi^3}A^2S\int d\nu\ d^2\pmb k\ (1-n_F(\nu-V))n_F(\nu)\frac{\Gamma^2}{((\nu-\xi_{\pmb k})^2+\Gamma^2)((\nu-V-\xi_{\pmb k})^2+\Gamma^2)}\\-\frac{1}{2\pi^3}A^2S\int d\nu\ d^2\pmb k\ n_F(\nu-V)(1-n_F(\nu))\frac{\Gamma^2}{((\nu-\xi_{\pmb k})^2+\Gamma^2)((\nu-V-\xi_{\pmb k})^2+\Gamma^2)}\\=\frac{m}{\pi^2}A^2S\int d\nu\ [n_F(\nu)-n_F(\nu-V)]\int_0^\infty d\epsilon\ \frac{\Gamma^2}{((\nu-\epsilon+\mu)^2+\Gamma^2)((\nu-V-\epsilon+\mu)^2+\Gamma^2)}$$

注意由于$T\ll E_F$，故只有$\nu$和$\nu-V$能把$0$框住的范围附近，积分的左侧部分才有贡献，而这就意味着，因为$\Gamma\ll E_F$，当$\epsilon<0$，积分右侧部分基本就没有贡献了，所以可以把积分右侧部分的下界取到无穷，故得：

$$\langle j_T\rangle(t)=\frac{2m\Gamma A^2S}{\pi(V^2+4\Gamma^2)}\int d\nu\ [n_F(\nu)-n_F(\nu-V)]=-\frac{2m\Gamma VA^2S}{\pi(V^2+4\Gamma^2)}$$

3.
注意，上一问的结果跟温度无关，所以只要温度远小于费米能，那么电导和温度无关。
而电导为$$\frac{mA^2S}{2\pi\Gamma}$$

从而单位面积电导为$$\frac{mA^2}{2\pi\Gamma}$$

4.
$$\langle j_T\rangle(t)=\frac1{2\pi^3}A^2S\int d\nu\ d^2\pmb k\ (1-n_F(\nu-V))n_F(\nu)\times\\\frac{\Gamma^2}{((\nu-(\pmb k+\pmb Q/2)^2/2m+\mu)^2+\Gamma^2)((\nu-V-(\pmb k-\pmb Q/2)^2/2m+\mu)^2+\Gamma^2)}\\-\frac{1}{2\pi^3}A^2S\int d\nu\ d^2\pmb k\ n_F(\nu-V)(1-n_F(\nu))\times\\\frac{\Gamma^2}{((\nu-(\pmb k+\pmb Q/2)^2/2m+\mu)^2+\Gamma^2)((\nu-V-(\pmb k-\pmb Q/2)^2/2m+\mu)^2+\Gamma^2)}\\=\frac{1}{2\pi^3}A^2S\int d\nu\ [n_F(\nu)-n_F(\nu-V)]\times\\\int d^2\pmb k\ \frac{\Gamma^2}{((\nu-(\pmb k+\pmb Q/2)^2/2m+\mu)^2+\Gamma^2)((\nu-V-(\pmb k-\pmb Q/2)^2/2m+\mu)^2+\Gamma^2)}$$

而
$$\int d^2\pmb k\ \frac{\Gamma^2}{((\nu-(\pmb k+\pmb Q/2)^2/2m+\mu)^2+\Gamma^2)((\nu-V-(\pmb k-\pmb Q/2)^2/2m+\mu)^2+\Gamma^2)}\\=\int_0^{2\pi}d\theta\ \int kdk\ \frac{\Gamma}{(\nu-k^2/2m-kQ\cos\theta/2m-Q^2/8m+\mu)^2+\Gamma^2}\\\times\frac{\Gamma}{(\nu-V-k^2/2m+kQ\cos\theta/2m-Q^2/8m+\mu)^2+\Gamma^2}$$

当$Q$接近零，那么（注意积分左边部分限定了$\pmb k$在费米面附近）
$$\xi_{L\pmb k}-\xi_{R\pmb k}=(\pmb k+\pmb Q/2)^2/2m-(\pmb k-\pmb Q/2)^2/2m=\pmb k\cdot\pmb Q/m\simeq Qk_F\cos\theta/m$$

所以可以近似认为上述积分就是再错开$Qk_F\cos\theta/m$的卷积，换言之
$$\langle j_T\rangle_Q\simeq\langle j_T\rangle_0\int_0^{2\pi}\frac{d\theta}{2\pi}\frac{V^2+4\Gamma^2}{(V+Qk_F\cos\theta/m)^2+4\Gamma^2}$$

从而
$$\sigma_Q\simeq\sigma_0\int_0^{2\pi}\frac{d\theta}{2\pi}\frac{4\Gamma^2}{(Qk_F\cos\theta/m)^2+4\Gamma^2}=\sigma_0\frac{2\Gamma}{\sqrt{Q^2v_F^2+4\Gamma^2}}=\frac{\sigma_0}{\sqrt{1+\frac{Q^2v_F^2}{4\Gamma^2}}}$$

当$Q$接近$2k_F$，两个费米面会发生一次相切，形成一个导电率的峰。现在考虑这么计算：
$$\int d^2\pmb k\ \frac{\Gamma^2}{((\nu-(\pmb k+\pmb Q/2)^2/2m+\mu)^2+\Gamma^2)((\nu-V-(\pmb k-\pmb Q/2)^2/2m+\mu)^2+\Gamma^2)}\\=\int dk_x\ dk_y\ \frac{\Gamma}{((\nu-k_x^2/2m-k_y^2/2m-k_xQ/2m-Q^2/8m+\mu)+\Gamma^2)}\times\\\frac{\Gamma}{((\nu-V-k_x^2/2m-k_y^2/2m+k_xQ/2m-Q^2/8m+\mu)+\Gamma^2)}$$

注意由于$Q$接近$2k_F$，故$k_x\simeq0$的区域才有贡献，所以实际上可以把$k_x^2/2m$抛掉，从而上式等于
$$\int dk_y\ \frac{2\Gamma\pi}{((2\nu-V-k_y^2/m+2\mu-Q^2/4m)^2+4\Gamma^2)Q/2m}\\=\frac{\sqrt 2m^{3/2}\pi^2}{Q}\frac{\sqrt{\sqrt{C^2+4\Gamma^2}+C}}{\sqrt{C^2+4\Gamma^2}}$$

其中$$C=2\nu-V+2\mu-Q^2/4m$$

故
$$\langle j_T\rangle(t)=\frac{m^{3/2}}{\sqrt2\pi Q}A^2S\int d\nu\ [n_F(\nu)-n_F(\nu-V)]\frac{\sqrt{\sqrt{C^2+4\Gamma^2}+C}}{\sqrt{C^2+4\Gamma^2}}$$
