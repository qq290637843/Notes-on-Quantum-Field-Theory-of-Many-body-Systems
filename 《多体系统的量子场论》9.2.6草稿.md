# 《多体系统的量子场论》9.2.6草稿

## 问题 9.2.6.
$$u_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}=i\eta\tau^0-\chi(\tau^3-\tau^1),u_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}=i\eta\tau^0-\chi(\tau^3+\tau^1),a_0^l=0$$

以及
$$G_x=\tau^0,G_y=\tau^0,G_T=(-)^{\pmb i}\tau^0\\G_{P_x}=i(-)^{i_x}\frac{\tau^1+\tau^3}{\sqrt2},G_{P_y}=i(-)^{i_y}\frac{\tau^1-\tau^3}{\sqrt2},G_{P_{xy}}=i\tau^3$$
现在开始校验：
1.$x$平移：显然
2.$y$平移：显然
3.时间反演：
$$G_{T,\pmb i}^\dagger u_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}G_{T,\pmb i+\pmb x}=-u_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x},G_{T,\pmb i}^\dagger u_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}G_{T,\pmb i+\pmb y}=-u_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}$$

4.$x$方向空间反演：
$$G_{P_x,\pmb i}^\dagger u_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}G_{P_x,\pmb i+\pmb x}\\=-i(-)^{i_x}\frac{\tau^1+\tau^3}{\sqrt2}[i\eta\tau^0-\chi(\tau^3-\tau^1)]i(-)^{i_x+1}\frac{\tau^1+\tau^3}{\sqrt2}\\=-\frac12(\tau^1+\tau^3) [i\eta\tau^0-\chi(\tau^3-\tau^1)] (\tau^1+\tau^3)\\=-\frac12[i\eta(\tau^1+\tau^3)+2i\chi\tau^2] (\tau^1+\tau^3)\\=-i\eta\tau^0-\chi(\tau^3-\tau^1)$$

$$G_{P_x,\pmb i}^\dagger u_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}G_{P_x,\pmb i+\pmb y}\\=-i(-)^{i_x}\frac{\tau^1+\tau^3}{\sqrt2}[i\eta\tau^0-\chi(\tau^3+\tau^1)]i(-)^{i_x}\frac{\tau_1+\tau_3}{\sqrt2}\\=\frac12(\tau^1+\tau^3)[i\eta\tau^0-\chi(\tau^3+\tau^1)] (\tau^1+\tau^3)\\=\frac12[i\eta(\tau^1+\tau^3)-2\chi\tau^0] (\tau^1+\tau^3)\\=i\eta\tau^0-\chi(\tau^3+\tau^1)$$

5.$y$方向空间反演：
$$G_{P_y,\pmb i}^\dagger u_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}G_{P_y,\pmb i+\pmb x}\\=-i(-)^{i_y}\frac{\tau^1-\tau^3}{\sqrt2}[i\eta\tau^0-\chi(\tau^3-\tau^1)]i(-)^{i_y}\frac{\tau^1-\tau^3}{\sqrt2}\\=\frac12(\tau^1-\tau^3) [i\eta\tau^0-\chi(\tau^3-\tau^1)] (\tau^1-\tau^3)\\=\frac12[i\eta(\tau^1-\tau^3)+2\chi\tau^0] (\tau^1-\tau^3)\\=i\eta\tau^0-\chi(\tau^3-\tau^1)$$

$$G_{P_y,\pmb i}^\dagger u_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}G_{P_y,\pmb i+\pmb y}\\=-i(-)^{i_y}\frac{\tau^1-\tau^3}{\sqrt2}[i\eta\tau^0-\chi(\tau^3+\tau^1)]i(-)^{i_y+1}\frac{\tau_1-\tau_3}{\sqrt2}\\=-\frac12(\tau^1-\tau^3)[i\eta\tau^0-\chi(\tau^3+\tau^1)] (\tau^1-\tau^3)\\=-\frac12[i\eta(\tau^1-\tau^3)+2i\chi\tau^2] (\tau^1-\tau^3)\\=-i\eta\tau^0-\chi(\tau^3+\tau^1)$$

6.对角线方向空间反演：
$$G_{P_{xy},\pmb i}^\dagger u_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}G_{P_{xy},\pmb i+\pmb x}\\=-i\tau^3[i\eta\tau^0-\chi(\tau^3-\tau^1)]i\tau^3\\=i\eta\tau^0-\chi(\tau^3+\tau^1)$$

$$G_{P_{xy},\pmb i}^\dagger u_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}G_{P_{xy},\pmb i+\pmb y}\\=-i\tau^3[i\eta\tau^0-\chi(\tau^3+\tau^1)]i\tau^3\\=i\eta\tau^0-\chi(\tau^3-\tau^1)$$

## 问题9.2.7.
$$U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}=-i\chi_1\tau^0,U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}=-i\chi_1(-)^{i_x}\tau^0\\U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x+\pmb y}=i\chi_2(-)^{i_x}\tau^0,U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x-\pmb y}=i\chi_2(-)^{i_x}\tau^0$$

$$u_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}=-\chi(\tau^3+\tau^1),u_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}=-\chi(\tau^3-\tau^1)\\u_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x+\pmb y}=\eta\tau^2,u_{\pmb i,\pmb i-\pmb x+\pmb y}=-\eta\tau^2$$

1.$x$方向平移：显然
2.$y$方向平移：显然
3.旋转九十度：
旋转后为
$$v_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}=-\chi(\tau^3-\tau^1),v_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}=-\chi(\tau^3+\tau^1)\\v_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x+\pmb y}=-\eta\tau^2,v_{\pmb i,\pmb i-\pmb x+\pmb y}=\eta\tau^2$$

注意显然需要的$G$的任务是$G\tau^3G^\dagger=\tau^3,G\tau^1G^\dagger=-\tau^1,G\tau^2G^\dagger=-\tau^2$，所以取$G=i\tau^3$即可
4.$TP_x$：
$$v_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}=\chi(\tau^3+\tau^1),v_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}=\chi(\tau^3-\tau^1)\\v_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x+\pmb y}=-\eta\tau^2,v_{\pmb i,\pmb i-\pmb x+\pmb y}=\eta\tau^2$$

显然取$G=i\tau^2$即可
5.$TP_y$：
$$v_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}=\chi(\tau^3+\tau^1),v_{\pmb i,\pmb i-\pmb y}=\chi(\tau^3+\tau^1)\\v_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x+\pmb y}=-\eta\tau^2,v_{\pmb i,\pmb i-\pmb x+\pmb y}=\eta\tau^2$$

显然取$G=i\tau^2$即可
6.$TP_{xy}$：
$$v_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}=\chi(\tau^3-\tau^1),v_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}=\chi(\tau^3+\tau^1)\\v_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x+\pmb y}=-\eta\tau^2,v_{\pmb i,\pmb i-\pmb x+\pmb y}=\eta\tau^2$$

显然取$G=i\tau^1$即可
