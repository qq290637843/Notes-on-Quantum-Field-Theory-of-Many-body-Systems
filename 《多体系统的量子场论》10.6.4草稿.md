# 《多体系统的量子场论》10.6.4草稿

## 问题 10.6.2.
首先坐标连续化为：
二维：$\sum_{\pmb i}\to\frac{1}{l^2}\int d^2x$，三维：$\sum_{\pmb i}\to\frac{1}{l^3}\int d^3x$

规范场连续化为：
$$a_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb\mu}=la_\mu(\pmb x),a_0(\pmb i)=a_0(\pmb x),a_0(\pmb i+\pmb\mu)-a_0(\pmb i)\simeq l\partial_\mu a_0(\pmb x)$$

于是二维情况为
$$L=\frac{1}{4J}\sum_{\pmb i,\pmb\mu=\pmb x,\pmb y}[\dot a_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb\mu}+a_0(\pmb i)-a_0(\pmb i+\pmb\mu)]^2+g\sum_{\pmb i}\cos(\Phi_{\pmb i})\\\to\frac1{4J}\sum_{\pmb i}\sum_{\mu=x,y}[le_\mu(\pmb i)]^2+g\sum_{\pmb i}\cos(l^2b(\pmb i))\\\simeq\frac{1}{4J}\sum_{\pmb i}\sum_{\mu=x,y}[le_\mu(\pmb i)]^2+g\sum_{\pmb i}1-g\sum_{\pmb i}\frac{l^4b^2(\pmb i)}{2}\\\to\frac{1}{4Jl^2}\int d^2x\ (l^2\pmb e^2)+\frac{g}{l^2}\int d^2x-\frac{g}{2l^2}\int d^2x\ (l^4b^2)\\=\int d^2x\ \left(\frac{1}{4J}\pmb e^2-\frac{gl^2}{2}b^2+\frac{g}{l^2}\right)$$

三维情况为
$$L=\frac{1}{4J}\sum_{\pmb i,\pmb\mu=\pmb x,\pmb y,\pmb z}[\dot a_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb\mu}+a_0(\pmb i)-a_0(\pmb i+\pmb\mu)]^2+g\sum_p\cos(\Phi_p)\\\to\frac{1}{4J}\sum_{\pmb i}\sum_{\mu=x,y}[le_\mu(\pmb i)]^2+g\sum_{p}\cos(l^2\pmb b(\pmb i))\\\simeq\frac{1}{4J}\sum_{\pmb i}\sum_{\mu=x,y}[le_\mu(\pmb i)]^2+3g\sum_{\pmb i}1-g\sum_{\pmb i}\frac{l^4\pmb b^2(\pmb i)}{2}\\\to\frac{1}{4Jl^3}\int d^3x\ (l^2\pmb e^2)+\frac{3g}{l^3}\int d^3x-\frac{g}{2l^3}\int d^3x\ (l^4\pmb b^2)\\=\int d^3x\ \left(\frac{1}{4Jl}\pmb e^2-\frac{gl}{2}\pmb b^2+\frac{3g}{l^3}\right)$$
