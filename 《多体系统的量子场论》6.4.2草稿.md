# 《多体系统的量子场论》6.4.2草稿

## 问题 6.4.2.
(a)
$$Z=\int\mathcal Da\mathcal Da_0\ \exp\left[i\int dt\left\{\frac{1}{4J}\left[\sum_{i=1}^4(\dot a_{i,i+1}+a_0(i)-a_0(i+1))^2\\+(\dot a_{24}+a_0(2)-a_0(4))^2\right]+g(\cos\Phi_1+\cos\Phi_2)\right\}\right]$$

其中
$$\Phi_1=a_{12}+a_{24}+a_{41},\Phi_2=a_{23}+a_{34}+a_{42}$$

(b)
考虑
$$\frac{\partial L}{\partial\dot a_{ij}}=\frac{1}{2J}e_{ij}=\frac{1}{2J}(\dot a_{ij}+a_0(i)-a_0(j))$$

故
$$Z=\int\mathcal Da\mathcal Da_0\mathcal De_{ij}\ \exp\left[i\int dt\left\{\sum_{i=1}^4\frac{e_{i,i+1}}{2J}(\dot a_{i,i+1}+a_0(i)-a_0(i+1))\\+\frac{e_{24}}{2J}(\dot a_{24}+a_0(2)-a_0(4))-\frac{1}{4J}\sum_{i=1}^4e_{i,i+1}^2-\frac{1}{4J}e_{24}^2\\+g(\cos\Phi_1+\cos\Phi_2)\right\}\right]\\=\int\mathcal Da\mathcal De_{ij}\ \exp\left[i\int dt\left\{\sum_{i=1}^4\frac{e_{i,i+1}}{2J}\dot a_{i,i+1}+\frac{e_{24}}{2J}\dot a_{24}\\-\frac{1}{4J}\sum_{i=1}^4e_{i,i+1}^2-\frac1{4J}e_{24}^2+g(\cos\Phi_1+\cos\Phi_2)\right\}\right]\\\prod_{t}\left(\delta(e_{12}+e_{14})\delta(e_{32}+e_{34})\delta(e_{21}+e_{24}+e_{23})\delta(e_{41}+e_{42}+e_{43})\right)$$

从而
$$H=\frac{1}{4J}\sum_{i=1}^4e_{i,i+1}^4+\frac1{4J}e_{24}^2-g(\cos\Phi_1+\cos\Phi_2)\\ [a_{ij},(2J)^{-1}e_{ij}]=i\hbar$$

且态空间满足
$$\sum_{j}e_{ij}|{\rm phy}\rangle=0$$

由于$a_{ij}$以$2\pi$为周期，故$e_{ij}/2J$量子化为整数$n_{ij}$，所以当$g=0$，能级为$J\sum_{\langle ij\rangle}n_{ij}^2$，且$\sum_{j}n_{ij}=0$，故$n_{12}=n_{41}=N_1,n_{43}=n_{32}=N_2,n_{42}=N_1+N_2$，从而能级为$J(3N_1^2+3N_2^2+2N_1N_2)$
所以基态能为$0$，第一激发态能为$3J$

## 问题 6.4.3.
(a)
从(6.4.2)得到要证命题的过程，和从(6.4.10)得到(6.4.11)之前那个表达式的过程完全一样。不过注意，题干中$g$应该在分子上，而不是分母，并且应该是$+i$而不是$-i$，这个错误在前文中到处都是。
(b)
显然，这就是上边缘的上边缘，自然等于零，所以就满足了高斯定律的约束。
于是
$$\sum_{\pmb i,\pmb\mu=\pmb x,\pmb y}\dot a_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb\mu}^2=\sum_{\langle\pmb p_1,\pmb p_2\rangle}(\dot\phi_{\pmb p_1}-\dot\phi_{\pmb p_2})^2$$

$$\Phi_{\pmb p}=4\phi_{\pmb p}-\sum_{\pmb\mu=\pm\pmb x,\pm\pmb y}\phi_{\pmb p+\pmb\mu}=:-\Delta\phi_{\pmb p}$$

从而
$$Z=\int\mathcal D\phi_{\pmb p}\ \exp\left[i\int dt\left\{\frac1{4J}\sum_{\langle\pmb p_1,\pmb p_2\rangle}(\dot\phi_{\pmb p_1}-\dot\phi_{\pmb p_2})^2-\frac{g}{2}\sum_{\pmb p}(\Delta\phi_{\pmb p})^2\right\}\right]$$

(c)
拉格朗日量为
$$L=\frac{1}{4J}\sum_{\pmb p,\pmb\mu=\pmb x,\pmb y}(\dot\phi_{\pmb p}-\dot\phi_{\pmb p+\pmb\mu})^2-\frac g{2}\sum_{\pmb p}(\Delta\phi_{\pmb p})^2$$

考虑格点上的分部求和，改为
$$L=-\frac{1}{4J}\sum_{\pmb p}\dot\phi_{\pmb p}\Delta\dot\phi_{\pmb p}-\frac{g}{2}\sum_{\pmb p}\phi_{\pmb p}\Delta^2\phi_{\pmb p}$$

从而运动方程为：
$$-\frac{1}{2J}\Delta\ddot\phi_{\pmb p}=-g\Delta^2\phi_{\pmb p}$$

考虑傅里叶变换：
$$\phi_{\pmb p}(t)=\int\frac{d\omega}{2\pi}\sum_{\pmb k}\tilde\phi_{\pmb k}(\omega)\exp[il\pmb k\cdot\pmb p-i\omega t]$$

故
$$-\Delta\phi_{\pmb p}=(4-\exp[ik_xl]-\exp[-ik_xl]-\exp[ik_yl]-\exp[-ik_yl])\phi_{\pmb p}$$

这就是说
$$-\Delta\to4-2\cos(k_xl)-2\cos(k_yl)=:\lambda(\pmb k)$$

故运动方程为
$$-\omega^2\frac{1}{2J}\lambda(\pmb k)\tilde\phi_{\pmb k}(\omega)=-g\lambda(\pmb k)^2\tilde\phi_{\pmb k}(\omega)$$

从而
$$\omega^2=2Jg(4-2\cos(k_xl)-2\cos(k_yl))$$

在长波近似下
$$\omega\simeq\sqrt{2Jgl^2}|\pmb k|$$
