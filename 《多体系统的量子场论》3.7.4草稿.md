# 《多体系统的量子场论》3.7.4草稿

关于$\langle j_T\rangle_0$：
根据正文定义，$j_T=-i\Gamma(\varphi_L\varphi_R^\dagger\exp[-ia]-\varphi_R\varphi_L^\dagger\exp[+ia])$。根据定义，$\langle j_T\rangle_0$是两个超流体没有相互作用项的电流。注意由于两个超流体都有着自发对称性破缺，所以$\langle\varphi_L\rangle=\sqrt{\rho_L}\exp[i\theta_L],\langle\varphi_R\rangle=\sqrt{\rho_R}\exp[i\theta_R]$，故
$$\langle j_T\rangle_0=-i\Gamma\sqrt{\rho_L\rho_R}(\exp[i\theta_L-i\theta_R-ia]-\exp[i\theta_R-i\theta_L+ia])\\=2\Gamma\sqrt{\rho_L\rho_R}\sin(\theta-a)$$

注释25的第一个表达式，请参看问题3.3.6.的解答过程，可知$+i\theta$和$-i\theta$的前后不影响结果

注意书中$\langle j_T\rangle$的最终表达式少写了一个负号

## 问题 3.7.3.
根据问题3.3.6.3.可知，在温度$T$，有
$$\langle\varphi(t)\varphi^\dagger(0)\rangle=\exp\left[-i\frac{1}{4v\chi}\right]\left(\frac{\pi\tau_0T}{\sinh(\pi T|t|)}\right)^{\frac{1}{2\pi v\chi}}\\=\exp[-i\eta\pi/2]\left(\frac{\sinh(\pi T|t|)}{\pi T\tau_0}\right)^{-\eta}$$

故$$\langle[I(t),I^\dagger(t')]\rangle\\=\langle\varphi_L(t)\varphi_L^\dagger(t')\rangle\langle\varphi_R^\dagger(t)\varphi_R(t')\rangle-\langle\varphi_L^\dagger(t')\varphi_L(t)\rangle\langle\varphi_R(t')\varphi_R^\dagger(t)\rangle\\=-2i\sin\left(\frac{(\eta_L+\eta_R)\pi}{2}\right)\left(\frac{\sinh(\pi T|t-t'|)}{\pi T\tau_0}\right)^{-\eta}$$

从而
$$\langle j_T\rangle(t)=-4\sin\left(\frac{(\eta_L+\eta_R)\pi}{2}\right)\left(\pi T\tau_0\right)^{\eta_L+\eta_R}\times\\\Gamma^2\int^tdt'\ \sin[V(t-t')]\sinh^{-\eta_L-\eta_R}(\pi T|t-t'|)\\=-4\sin\left(\frac{(\eta_L+\eta_R)\pi}{2}\right)(\pi T\tau_0)^{\eta_L+\eta_R}\times\\\Gamma^2\int_0^{+\infty}dt\ \sin[Vt]\sinh^{-\eta_L-\eta_R}(\pi Tt)$$

故在$V=0$处，
$$d\langle j_T\rangle/dV=-4\sin\left(\frac{(\eta_L+\eta_R)\pi}{2}\right)(\pi T\tau_0)^{\eta_L+\eta_R}\times\\\Gamma^2\int_0^\infty dt\ t\sinh^{-\eta_L-\eta_R}(\pi Tt)$$

设$$t=u/T$$

于是积分部分变为
$$\int_0^\infty dt\ t\sinh^{-\eta_L-\eta_R}(\pi Tt)\\=T^{-2}\int_0^\infty du\ u\sinh^{-\eta_L-\eta_R}[\pi u]$$

这就说明了$$d\langle j_T\rangle/dV\propto T^{\eta_L+\eta_R-2}$$
