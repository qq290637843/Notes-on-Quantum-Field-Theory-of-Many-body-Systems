# 《多体系统的量子场论》4.1.4草稿

## 问题 4.1.5.
$$\hat t_{lk}\hat t_{il}\hat t_{lj}=c_l^\dagger c_kc_i^\dagger c_lc_l^\dagger c_j=c_l^\dagger c_jc_kc_i^\dagger c_lc_l^\dagger=-c_l^\dagger c_jc_i^\dagger c_kc_lc_l^\dagger\\=c_l^\dagger c_jc_i^\dagger c_l c_kc_l^\dagger=-c_l^\dagger c_jc_i^\dagger c_lc_l^\dagger c_k=-\hat t_{li}\hat t_{il}\hat t_{lk}$$

## 问题 4.1.6.
(a)
$$\hat W_{lk}\hat W_{il}\hat W_{lj}=\hat t_{lu_1}...\hat t_{u_ak}\hat t_{iv_1}...\hat t_{v_bl}\hat t_{lw_1}...\hat t_{w_cj}\\=\hat t_{iv_1}...\hat t_{v_{b-1}v_b}\hat t_{lu_1}\hat t_{v_bl}\hat t_{lw_1}\hat t_{u_1u_2}...\hat t_{u_ak}\hat t_{w_1w_2}...\hat t_{w_cj}\\=-\hat t_{iv_1}...\hat t_{v_{b-1}v_b}\hat t_{lw_1}\hat t_{v_bl}\hat t_{lu_1}\hat t_{u_1u_2}...\hat t_{u_ak}\hat t_{w_1w_2}...\hat t_{w_cj}\\=-\hat t_{lw_1}...\hat t_{w_cj}\hat t_{iv_1}...\hat t_{v_bl}\hat t_{lu_1}...\hat t_{u_ak}=-\hat W_{lj}\hat W_{il}\hat W_{lk}$$

(b)
如果交换，那么
$$\hat W_{lk}\hat W_{il}\hat W_{lj}=C_l^+C_k^-C_i^+C_l^-C_l^+C_j^-\\=C_l^+C_j^-C_i^+C_l^-C_l^+C_k^-=\hat W_{lj}\hat W_{il}\hat W_{lk}$$

会造成归零。

如果反交换，那么
$$\hat W_{lk}\hat W_{il}\hat W_{lj}=C_l^+C_k^-C_i^+C_l^-C_l^+C_j^-\\=(-1)^7C_l^+C_j^-C_i^+C_l^-C_l^+C_k^-=-\hat W_{lj}\hat W_{il}\hat W_{lk}$$

故满足(4.1.9)
