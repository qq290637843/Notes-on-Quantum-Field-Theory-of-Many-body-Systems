## 问题 5.4.2.
$$iG(\pmb x,t)\delta_{\alpha\beta}=\left\langle Tc_\alpha(\pmb x,t)c_\beta^\dagger(0,0)\right\rangle_0+\left\langle Tc_\alpha(\pmb x,t)c_\beta^\dagger(0,0)(-i)\int dt'\ H_I(t')\right\rangle_{0c}$$

从而
$$iG_{\pmb k,\omega}=iG_{0,\pmb k,\omega}+\int dt\ \exp[i\omega t]\left\langle Tc_{\alpha\pmb k}(t)c_{\alpha\pmb k}^\dagger(0)(-i)\int dt'\ H_I(t')\right\rangle_{0c}\\=iG_{0,\pmb k,\omega}+\int dt\ dt'\ \exp[i\omega t]\sum_{\pmb p,\pmb p',\pmb q,\beta,\lambda}\frac{1}{2\mathcal V}(-iV_{\pmb q})\times\\\langle Tc_{\alpha\pmb k}(t)c_{\alpha\pmb k}^\dagger(0)c_{\beta\pmb p}^\dagger(t')c_{\beta,\pmb p-\pmb q}(t')c_{\lambda\pmb p'}^\dagger(t')c_{\lambda,\pmb p'+\pmb q}(t')\rangle_{0c}\\=iG_{0,\pmb k,\omega}-2\int dt\ dt'\ \exp[i\omega t]\sum_{\pmb p',\lambda}\frac{1}{2\mathcal V}(-iV_{\pmb 0})iG_{0,\pmb k}(t,t')(-i)G_{0,\pmb k}(t',0)(-i)G_{0,\pmb p'}(0^-)\\-2\int dt\ dt'\ \exp[i\omega t]\sum_{\pmb q}\frac{1}{2\mathcal V}(-iV_{\pmb q})iG_{0,\pmb k}(t,t')iG_{0,\pmb k-\pmb q}(0^+)(-i)G_{0,\pmb k}(t',0)\\=iG_{0,\pmb k,\omega}-(iG_{0,\pmb k,\omega})^2(-iV_{\pmb 0})\frac{1}{\mathcal V}\sum_{\pmb p',\lambda}iG_{0,\pmb p'}(0^-)\\+(iG_{0,\pmb k,\omega})^2\frac{1}{\mathcal V}\sum_{\pmb q}(-iV_{\pmb q})iG_{0,\pmb k-\pmb q}(0^+)\\=iG_{0,\pmb k,\omega}-(iG_{0,\pmb k,\omega})^2(-iV_{\pmb 0})\frac1{\mathcal V}\sum_{\pmb k',\alpha'}\int\frac{d\nu}{2\pi}iG_{0,\pmb k',\nu}\exp[i\nu0^+]\\+(iG_{0,\pmb k,\omega})^2\frac1{\mathcal V}\sum_{\pmb q}(-iV_{\pmb q})\int\frac{d\nu}{2\pi}iG_{0,\pmb k-\pmb q,\nu}\exp[-i\nu0^+]$$

## 问题 5.4.3.
方便的一点是，五张图的对称因子都是$1$。以下书写中两个外腿带来的$(iG_{0,\pmb k,\omega})^2$均不写，换言之，写的是$-i\Sigma_{\pmb k,\omega}$的修正项
(a)
$$-(-iV_0)\frac{1}{\mathcal V^2}\sum_{\pmb q_1,\pmb q_2,\alpha}\int\frac{d\nu_1d\nu_2}{(2\pi)^2}(-iV_{\pmb q_2})iG_{\pmb q_1,\nu_1}iG_{\pmb q_1+\pmb q_2,\nu_2}iG_{\pmb q_1,\nu_1}\exp[-i\nu_20^+]$$

(b)
$$(-iV_0)^2\frac{1}{\mathcal V^2}\sum_{\pmb q_1,\pmb q_2,\alpha,\beta}\int\frac{d\nu_1d\nu_2}{(2\pi)^2}(iG_{\pmb q_1,\nu_1})^2iG_{\pmb q_2,\nu_2}\exp[i\nu_20^+]$$

(c)
$$\frac{1}{\mathcal V^2}\sum_{\pmb q_1,\pmb q_2}\int\frac{d\nu_1d\nu_2}{(2\pi)^2}(-iV_{\pmb q_1})(-iV_{\pmb q_2})iG_{\pmb k-\pmb q_1,\omega-\nu_1}iG_{\pmb k-\pmb q_1-\pmb q_2,\omega-\nu_1-\nu_2}iG_{\pmb k-\pmb q_2,\omega-\nu_2}$$

(d)
$$-\frac{1}{\mathcal V^2}\sum_{\pmb q_1,\pmb q_2}\int\frac{d\nu_1d\nu_2}{(2\pi)^2}(-iV_{\pmb q_1})^2iG_{\pmb k-\pmb q_1,\omega-\nu_1}iG_{\pmb q_2,\nu_2}iG_{\pmb q_2-\pmb q_1,\nu_2-\nu_1}$$

(e)
$$\frac{1}{\mathcal V^2}\sum_{\pmb q_1,\pmb q_2}\int\frac{d\nu_1d\nu_2}{(2\pi)^2}(-iV_{\pmb q_1})(-iV_{\pmb q_2})iG_{\pmb k-\pmb q_1,\omega-\nu_1}iG_{\pmb k-\pmb q_1-\pmb q_2,\omega-\nu_1-\nu_2}iG_{\pmb k-\pmb q_1,\omega-\nu_1}\exp[-i(\omega-\nu_1-\nu_2)0^+]$$
