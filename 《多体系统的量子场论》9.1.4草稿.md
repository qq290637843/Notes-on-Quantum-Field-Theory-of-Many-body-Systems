## 问题 9.1.2.
1.
$$\tilde\chi_{\pmb i\pmb j}=\exp[i\theta_{\pmb i}]\chi_{\pmb i\pmb j}\exp[-i\theta_{\pmb j}]$$

$$H_{\rm mean}^{(\chi)}=\sum_{\langle\pmb i\pmb j\rangle}-\frac{J_{\pmb i\pmb j}}{2}\left[(f_{\pmb i\alpha}^\dagger f_{\pmb j\alpha}\chi_{\pmb j\pmb i}+h.c.)-|\chi_{\pmb i\pmb j}|^2\right]+...$$

$$H_{\rm mean}^{(\tilde\chi)}=\sum_{\langle\pmb i\pmb j\rangle}-\frac{J_{\pmb i\pmb j}}{2}\left[(f_{\pmb i\alpha}^\dagger f_{\pmb j\alpha}\chi_{\pmb j\pmb i}\exp[i\theta_{\pmb j}-i\theta_{\pmb i}]+h.c.)-|\bar\chi_{\pmb i\pmb j}|^2\right]+...$$

考虑
$$U=\exp\left[i\sum_{\pmb i}\theta_{\pmb i}n_{\pmb i}\right]$$

于是有
$$U^\dagger f_{\pmb i\alpha}U=\sum_{u=0}^\infty\frac{1}{u!}(-i\theta_{\pmb i})^u[n_{\pmb i},...[n_{\pmb i},f_{\pmb i\alpha}]]=f_{\pmb i\alpha}\exp[i\theta_{\pmb i}]$$

$$U^\dagger f_{\pmb i\alpha}^\dagger U=\sum_{u=0}^\infty\frac{1}{u!}(-i\theta_{\pmb i})^u[n_{\pmb i},...[n_{\pmb i},f_{\pmb i\alpha}^\dagger]]=f_{\pmb i\alpha}^\dagger\exp[-i\theta_{\pmb i}]$$

故
$$H_{\rm mean}^{(\tilde\chi)}=U^\dagger H_{\rm mean}^{(\chi)}U$$

所以
$$|\Psi_{\rm mean}^{(\tilde\chi)}\rangle=U^\dagger|\Psi^{(\chi)}_{\rm mean}\rangle=\exp\left[-i\sum_{\pmb i}\theta_{\pmb i}n_{\pmb i}\right]|\Psi_{\rm mean}^{(\chi)}\rangle$$

从而
$$\Psi_{\rm spin}^{(\tilde\chi)}(\{\alpha_{\pmb i}\})=\langle0|\prod_{\pmb i}f_{\pmb i\alpha_{\pmb i}}\exp\left[-i\sum_{\pmb i}\theta_{\pmb i}n_{\pmb i}\right]|\Psi_{\rm mean}^{(\chi)}\rangle\\=\exp\left[-i\sum_{\pmb i}\theta_{\pmb i}\right]\Psi_{\rm spin}^{(\chi)}(\{\alpha_{\pmb i}\})$$

注意，这里的旋转，认为只考虑晶格位置的旋转，而不考虑自旋本身的旋转，两个旋转是解耦的。
首先注意原拟设为
$$\chi_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}=i\chi_1,\chi_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}=i\chi_1(-1)^{i_x}$$

旋转九十度得
$$\tilde\chi_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}=i\chi_1(-1)^{i_y+1},\tilde\chi_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}=i\chi_1$$

所以我们需要构造$\theta_{\pmb i}$，使得$$\exp[i\theta_{\pmb i}-i\theta_{\pmb i+\pmb x}]=(-1)^{i_y+1},\exp[i\theta_{\pmb i}-i\theta_{\pmb i+\pmb y}]=(-1)^{i_x}$$

注意绕一个方格走一圈，两套$\chi$的商乘起来等于$1$，所以这个构造很容易。

## 问题 9.1.3.
注意有条件：
$$\langle\Psi_{\rm mean}|n_{\pmb i}|\Psi_{\rm mean}\rangle=1,\langle\Psi_{\rm mean}|f_{\pmb i\alpha}^\dagger f_{\pmb j\beta}|\Psi_{\rm mean}\rangle=\frac12\delta_{\alpha\beta}\chi_{\pmb i\pmb j},\\\langle\Psi_{\rm mean}|f_{\pmb i\alpha}^\dagger f_{\pmb i\beta}|\Psi_{\rm mean}\rangle=\frac12\delta_{\alpha\beta},\langle\Psi_{\rm mean}|ff|\Psi_{\rm mean}\rangle=0$$

故

$$\langle\Psi_{\rm mean}|\pmb S_{\pmb i}\cdot\pmb S_{\pmb j}|\Psi_{\rm mean}\rangle\\=\frac14\langle\Psi_{\rm mean}|f_{\pmb i\alpha}^\dagger f_{\pmb i\beta}f_{\pmb j\gamma}^\dagger f_{\pmb j\delta}|\Psi_{\rm mean}\rangle(2\delta_{\alpha\delta}\delta_{\beta\gamma}-\delta_{\alpha\beta}\delta_{\gamma\delta})\\=\frac12\langle\Psi_{\rm mean}|f_{\pmb i\alpha}^\dagger f_{\pmb i\beta}f_{\pmb j\beta}^\dagger f_{\pmb j\alpha}|\Psi_{\rm mean}\rangle\\-\frac14\langle\Psi_{\rm mean}|n_{\pmb i}n_{\pmb j}|\Psi_{\rm mean}\rangle\\=-\frac12\langle\Psi_{\rm mean}|f_{\pmb i\alpha}^\dagger f_{\pmb j\alpha}f_{\pmb j\beta}^\dagger f_{\pmb i\beta}|\Psi_{\rm mean}\rangle+\frac12\langle\Psi_{\rm mean}|n_{\pmb i}|\Psi_{\rm mean}\rangle\\-\frac14\langle\Psi_{\rm mean}|n_{\pmb i}n_{\pmb j}|\Psi_{\rm mean}\rangle\\=-\frac12\langle f_{\pmb i\alpha}^\dagger f_{\pmb j\alpha}\rangle\langle f_{\pmb j\beta}^\dagger f_{\pmb i\beta}\rangle-\frac12\langle f_{\pmb i\alpha}^\dagger f_{\pmb i\beta}\rangle\langle f_{\pmb j\alpha} f_{\pmb j\beta}^\dagger\rangle+\frac12\\-\frac14\langle n_{\pmb i}\rangle\langle n_{\pmb j}\rangle+\frac14\langle f_{\pmb i\alpha}^\dagger f_{\pmb j\beta}\rangle\langle f_{\pmb i\alpha}f_{\pmb j\beta}^\dagger\rangle\\=-\frac18\delta_{\alpha\alpha}\chi_{\pmb i\pmb j}\delta_{\beta\beta}\chi_{\pmb j\pmb i}-\frac12(\frac12\delta_{\alpha\beta})(\delta_{\alpha\beta}-\frac12\delta_{\alpha\beta})+\frac12\\-\frac14-\frac14(\frac12\delta_{\alpha\beta}\chi_{\pmb i\pmb j})(-\frac12\delta_{\alpha\beta}\chi_{\pmb j\pmb i})\\=-\frac12|\chi_{\pmb i\pmb j}|^2-\frac14+\frac12-\frac14+\frac18|\chi_{\pmb i\pmb j}|^2\\=-\frac{3}{8}|\chi_{\pmb i\pmb j}|^2$$

注意$\pi$-通量的配置，可知上式等于
$$-\frac38\chi_1^2$$

所以
$$\langle H\rangle_\pi=-\frac34J_1N\chi_1^2$$

而
$$\langle H\rangle_{\rm dimer}=-\frac3{16}J_1N$$

注意
$$\chi_1=\frac12\int\frac{d^2\pmb k}{(2\pi)^2}\sqrt{\sin(k_x)^2+\sin(k_y)^2}\simeq0.48$$

故$$\langle H\rangle_\pi>\langle H\rangle_{\rm dimer}$$
