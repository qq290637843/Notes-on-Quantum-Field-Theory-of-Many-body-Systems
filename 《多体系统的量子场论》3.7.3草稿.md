# 《多体系统的量子场论》3.7.3草稿

## 问题 3.7.2.
1.
$$L=i\frac12(\varphi^*(\partial_t+iA_0)\varphi-\varphi(\partial_t-iA_0)\varphi^*)\\-\frac1{2m}|(\partial_{\pmb x}+i\pmb A)\varphi|^2+\mu|\varphi|^2-\frac{V_0}2|\varphi|^4$$

取
$$\varphi(\pmb x,t)=\exp[i\theta]\sqrt{\rho_0+\delta\rho}$$

$$\partial\varphi=\partial(\sqrt{\rho_0+\delta\rho}\exp[i\theta])=\\i\sqrt{\rho_0+\delta\rho}\exp[i\theta]\partial\theta+\frac12(\rho_0+\delta\rho)^{-1/2}\exp[i\theta]\partial\delta\rho\\=i\varphi\partial\theta+\frac12\frac{\varphi}{|\varphi|^2}\partial\delta\rho$$

于是
$$(\partial_t+iA_0)\varphi=i\varphi(\partial_t\theta+A_0)+\frac12\frac{\varphi}{|\varphi|^2}\partial_t\delta\rho$$

以及
$$(\partial_{\pmb x}+i\pmb A)\varphi=i\varphi(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta+\pmb A)+\frac12\frac{\varphi}{|\varphi|^2}\partial_{\pmb x}\delta\rho$$

故
$$L=-(\rho_0+\delta\rho)(\partial_t\theta+A_0)-\frac1{2m}(\rho_0+\delta\rho)(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta+\pmb A)^2\\-\frac1{8m}\frac{(\partial_{\pmb x}\delta\rho)^2}{\rho_0+\delta\rho}+\mu(\rho_0+\delta\rho)-\frac{V_0}2(\rho_0+\delta\rho)^2\\\simeq-(\rho_0+\delta\rho)(\partial_t\theta+A_0)-\frac1{2m}(\rho_0+\delta\rho)(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta+\pmb A)^2\\-\frac{1}{8m}\frac{(\partial_{\pmb x}\delta\rho)^2}{\rho_0}+\mu(\rho_0+\delta\rho)-\frac{V_0}2\rho_0-V_0\rho_0\delta\rho-\frac{V_0}2\delta\rho^2$$

把$\delta\rho$积掉并去掉高频项、常数项以及全导数项得
$$L=\frac{1}{2V_0}\left((\partial_t\theta+A_0)+\frac{1}{2m}(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta+\pmb A)^2\right)^2-\frac{\rho_0}{2m}(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta+\pmb A)^2\\=\frac{1}{2V_0}(\partial_t\theta+A_0)^2-\frac{\rho_0}{2m}(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta+\pmb A)^2\\+\frac{1}{2V_0m}(\partial_t\theta+A_0)(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta+\pmb A)^2+\frac{1}{8V_0m^2}(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta+\pmb A)^4$$

这里的结果和(3.7.10)不同，是因为(3.7.10)是把$A$作为一阶小量。

注意本题取$A_0=0$，且$\pmb A$为常数，并只考虑保留到$\partial\theta$的二次且去掉常数项以及全导数项得
$$L=\frac{1}{2V_0}(\partial_t\theta)^2-\frac{\rho_0}{2m}(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta)^2+\frac{1}{V_0m}\partial_t\theta\partial_{\pmb x}\theta\pmb A\\+\frac{1}{4V_0m^2}(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta)^2\pmb A^2+\frac{1}{2V_0m^2}(\pmb A\partial_{\pmb x}\theta)^2$$

于是运动方程为
$$\frac{1}{V_0}\partial_t^2\theta-\frac{\rho_0}{m}\partial_{\pmb x}^2\theta+\frac{2}{V_0m}\pmb A\partial_t\partial_{\pmb x}\theta+\frac{1}{2V_0m^2}\pmb A^2\partial_{\pmb x}^2\theta\\+\frac{1}{V_0m^2}A_iA_j\partial_i\partial_j\theta=0$$

从而色散关系为
$$\frac1{V_0}\omega^2-\frac{\rho_0}{m}\pmb k^2-\frac{2}{V_0m}\omega\pmb A\pmb k+\frac{1}{2V_0m^2}\pmb A^2\pmb k^2+\frac{1}{V_0m^2}(\pmb A\pmb k)^2=0$$

即
$$\left(\omega-\frac{\pmb A\pmb k}{m}\right)^2=\left(\frac{V_0\rho_0}{m}-\frac{\pmb A^2}{2m^2}\right)\pmb k^2$$

2.
$$L=i\frac12(\varphi^*(\partial_t+iA_0)\varphi-\varphi(\partial_t-iA_0)\varphi^*)\\-\frac1{2m}|(\partial_{\pmb x}+i\pmb A)\varphi|^2+\mu|\varphi|^2-\frac{V_0}2|\varphi|^4+\frac{c}{2}|(\partial_t+iA_0)\varphi|^2$$

取
$$\varphi(\pmb x,t)=\exp[i\theta]\sqrt{\rho_0+\delta\rho}$$

$$\partial\varphi=\partial(\sqrt{\rho_0+\delta\rho}\exp[i\theta])=\\i\sqrt{\rho_0+\delta\rho}\exp[i\theta]\partial\theta+\frac12(\rho_0+\delta\rho)^{-1/2}\exp[i\theta]\partial\delta\rho\\=i\varphi\partial\theta+\frac12\frac{\varphi}{|\varphi|^2}\partial\delta\rho$$

于是
$$(\partial_t+iA_0)\varphi=i\varphi(\partial_t\theta+A_0)+\frac12\frac{\varphi}{|\varphi|^2}\partial_t\delta\rho$$

以及
$$(\partial_{\pmb x}+i\pmb A)\varphi=i\varphi(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta+\pmb A)+\frac12\frac{\varphi}{|\varphi|^2}\partial_{\pmb x}\delta\rho$$

故
$$L=-(\rho_0+\delta\rho)(\partial_t\theta+A_0)-\frac1{2m}(\rho_0+\delta\rho)(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta+\pmb A)^2\\-\frac1{8m}\frac{(\partial_{\pmb x}\delta\rho)^2}{\rho_0+\delta\rho}+\mu(\rho_0+\delta\rho)-\frac{V_0}2(\rho_0+\delta\rho)^2\\+\frac c2(\rho_0+\delta\rho)(\partial_t\theta+A_0)^2+\frac{c}{8}\frac{(\partial_t\delta\rho)^2}{\rho_0+\delta\rho}\\\simeq-(\rho_0+\delta\rho)(\partial_t\theta+A_0)-\frac1{2m}(\rho_0+\delta\rho)(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta+\pmb A)^2\\-\frac{1}{8m}\frac{(\partial_{\pmb x}\delta\rho)^2}{\rho_0}+\mu(\rho_0+\delta\rho)-\frac{V_0}2\rho_0-V_0\rho_0\delta\rho-\frac{V_0}2\delta\rho^2\\+\frac c2(\rho_0+\delta\rho)(\partial_t\theta+A_0)^2+\frac c8\frac{(\partial_t\delta\rho)^2}{\rho_0}$$

把$\delta\rho$积掉并去掉高频项、常数项以及全导数项得
$$L=\frac{1}{2V_0}\left((\partial_t\theta+A_0)+\frac{1}{2m}(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta+\pmb A)^2-\frac c2(\partial_t\theta+A_0)^2\right)^2\\-\frac{\rho_0}{2m}(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta+\pmb A)^2+\frac{c\rho_0}{2}(\partial_t\theta+A_0)^2$$

注意本题取$A_0=0$，且$\pmb A$为常数，并只考虑保留到$\partial\theta$的二次且去掉常数项以及全导数项得
$$L=\frac{1}{2V_0}(\partial_t\theta)^2-\frac{\rho_0}{2m}(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta)^2+\frac{c\rho_0}{2}(\partial_t\theta)^2\\+\frac{1}{V_0m}\partial_t\theta\partial_{\pmb x}\theta\pmb A-\frac{c}{4V_0m}\pmb A^2(\partial_t\theta)^2\\+\frac{1}{4V_0m^2}(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta)^2\pmb A^2+\frac{1}{2V_0m^2}(\pmb A\partial_{\pmb x}\theta)^2$$

于是运动方程为
$$\frac{1}{V_0}\partial_t^2\theta-\frac{\rho_0}{m}\partial_{\pmb x}^2\theta+\frac{2}{V_0m}\pmb A\partial_t\partial_{\pmb x}\theta+\frac{1}{2V_0m^2}\pmb A^2\partial_{\pmb x}^2\theta\\+\frac{1}{V_0m^2}A_iA_j\partial_i\partial_j\theta+c\rho_0\partial_t^2\theta-\frac{c}{2V_0m}\pmb A^2\partial_t^2\theta=0$$

从而色散关系为
$$\frac1{V_0}\omega^2-\frac{\rho_0}{m}\pmb k^2-\frac{2}{V_0m}\omega\pmb A\pmb k+\frac{1}{2V_0m^2}\pmb A^2\pmb k^2+\frac{1}{V_0m^2}(\pmb A\pmb k)^2\\+c\rho_0\omega^2-\frac{c}{2V_0m}\pmb A^2\omega^2=0$$

即
$$\left(cV_0\rho_0-\frac{c\pmb A^2}{2m}\right)\omega^2+\left(\omega-\frac{\pmb A\pmb k}{m}\right)^2=\left(\frac{V_0\rho_0}{m}-\frac{\pmb A^2}{2m^2}\right)\pmb k^2$$

3.
$$L=\frac{1}{2V_0}\left((\partial_t\theta+A_0)+\frac{1}{2m}(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta+\pmb A)^2-\frac c2(\partial_t\theta+A_0)^2\right)^2\\-\frac{\rho_0}{2m}(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta+\pmb A)^2+\frac{c\rho_0}{2}(\partial_t\theta+A_0)^2$$

由于是求伦敦方程，故$A_\mu$是一阶小量且$\partial\theta$是一阶小量，于是只保留到二阶（这时没有什么可去掉的全导数项和常数项，因为求电流关于$A_\mu$的表达式并不能默认$A_\mu$是常场），得
$$L=\left(\frac{1}{2V_0}+\frac{c\rho_0}{2}\right)(\partial_t\theta+A_0)^2-\frac{\rho_0}{2m}(\partial_{\pmb x}\theta+\pmb A)^2$$

所以电流为：
$$\pmb J=-\frac{\partial L}{\partial\pmb A}=\frac{\rho_0}{m}(\pmb A+\partial_{\pmb x}\theta)$$

于是零温下的电流为
$$\frac{\rho_0}{m}\pmb A$$

有限温的电流为
$$\frac{\rho_0}{m}\pmb A+\int\frac{d^d\pmb k}{(2\pi)^d}n_B(\epsilon_{\pmb A}(\pmb k))\partial_{\pmb A}\epsilon_{\pmb A}(\pmb k)$$

现在解决这个求导：
$$\left(cV_0\rho_0-\frac{c\pmb A^2}{2m}\right)\omega^2+\left(\omega-\frac{\pmb A\pmb k}{m}\right)^2=\left(\frac{V_0\rho_0}{m}-\frac{\pmb A^2}{2m^2}\right)\pmb k^2$$

$$\epsilon_{\pmb A}(\pmb k)=\frac{\frac{\pmb A\pmb k}{m}+\sqrt{\frac{(\pmb A\pmb k)^2}{m^2}+\left(cV_0\rho_0-\frac{c\pmb A^2}{2m}+1\right)\left(\frac{V_0\rho_0\pmb k^2}{m}-\frac{\pmb A^2\pmb k^2}{2m^2}-\frac{(\pmb A\pmb k)^2}{m^2}\right)}}{cV_0\rho_0-\frac{c\pmb A^2}{2m}+1}\\\simeq\sqrt{\frac{V_0\rho_0+cV_0^2\rho_0^2}{m}}|\pmb k|+\frac{\pmb A\pmb k}{m+mcV_0\rho_0}\\=:\frac{u}{\sqrt{\gamma}}|\pmb k|+\gamma\frac{\pmb A}{m}\pmb k$$

$$u:=\sqrt{\frac{V_0\rho_0}{m}},\gamma:=\frac{1}{1+cV_0\rho_0}$$

所以$$\partial_{\pmb A}\epsilon_{\pmb A}(\pmb k)\simeq\frac{\pmb k}{m+mcV_0\rho_0}$$

$$n_B(\epsilon_{\pmb A}(\pmb k))\simeq n_B(u|\pmb k|/\sqrt\gamma)+n_B'(u|\pmb k|/\sqrt\gamma)\gamma\frac{\pmb A}{m}\pmb k$$

故有限温的电流为
$$\frac{\rho_0}{m}\pmb A+\int\frac{d^d\pmb k}{(2\pi)^d}\frac{\pmb k}{m}\gamma\left(n_B\left(\frac{u|\pmb k|}{\sqrt\gamma}\right)+n_B'\left(\frac{u|\pmb k|}{\sqrt\gamma}\right)\gamma\frac{\pmb A\pmb k}{m}\right)$$
