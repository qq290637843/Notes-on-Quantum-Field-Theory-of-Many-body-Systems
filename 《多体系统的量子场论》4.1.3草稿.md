# 《多体系统的量子场论》4.1.3草稿

$$\{\lambda^\dagger(k_1),\lambda(k_2)\}=\frac1N\sum_{j_1,j_2,k_1,k_2}\{\lambda_{j_1},\lambda_{j_2}\}\exp[ij_1k_1-ij_2k_2]\\=\frac1N\sum_{j,k_1,k_2}\exp[ijk_1-ijk_2]=\delta_{k_1,k_2}$$

## 问题 4.1.4.
$$\{\lambda_{1,i},\lambda_{1,j}\}=\left\{\frac{c_i+c_i^\dagger}{\sqrt2},\frac{c_j+c_j^\dagger}{\sqrt2}\right\}=\delta_{ij}$$

$$\{\lambda_{2,i},\lambda_{2,j}\}=\left\{\frac{c_i-c_i^\dagger}{i\sqrt2},\frac{c_j-c_j^\dagger}{i\sqrt2}\right\}=\delta_{ij}$$

$$\{\lambda_{1,i},\lambda_{2,j}\}=\left\{\frac{c_i+c_i^\dagger}{\sqrt2},\frac{c_j-c_j^\dagger}{i\sqrt2}\right\}=0$$

$$c_i=\frac{\lambda_{1,i}+i\lambda_{2,i}}{\sqrt2},c_i^\dagger=\frac{\lambda_{1,i}-i\lambda_{2,i}}{\sqrt2}$$

$$H=\frac12\sum_i(t\lambda_{1,i}\lambda_{1,i+1}+t\lambda_{2,i}\lambda_{2,i+1}-it\lambda_{2,i}\lambda_{1,i+1}+it\lambda_{1,i}\lambda_{2,i+1}\\+t\lambda_{1,i+1}\lambda_{1,i}+t\lambda_{2,i+1}\lambda_{2,i}+it\lambda_{1,i+1}\lambda_{2,i}-it\lambda_{2,i+1}\lambda_{1,i}\\+\eta\lambda_{1,i}\lambda_{1,i+1}-\eta\lambda_{2,i}\lambda_{2,i+1}-i\eta\lambda_{1,i}\lambda_{2,i+1}-i\eta\lambda_{2,i}\lambda_{1,i+1}\\+\eta\lambda_{1,i+1}\lambda_{1,i}-\eta\lambda_{2,i+1}\lambda_{2,i}+i\eta\lambda_{2,i+1}\lambda_{1,i}+i\eta\lambda_{1,i+1}\lambda_{2,i})\\=\sum_i(i(t-\eta)\lambda_{1,i}\lambda_{2,i+1}-i(t+\eta)\lambda_{2,i}\lambda_{1,i+1})$$

取
$$\lambda_{u,j}=\frac{1}{\sqrt N}\sum_k\lambda_{u,k}\exp[ijk]$$

注意
$$\lambda_{u,-k}=\lambda_{u,k}^\dagger$$

于是
$$\sum_j\lambda_{u,j}\lambda_{v,j+1}=\frac1N\sum_{j,k_1,k_2}\lambda_{u,k_1}\lambda_{v,k_2}\exp[ijk_1+ijk_2+ik_2]\\=\sum_k\lambda_{u,k}\lambda_{v,-k}\exp[-ik]$$

于是
$$H=i\sum_k((t-\eta)\lambda_{1,k}\lambda_{2,-k}-(t+\eta)\lambda_{2,k}\lambda_{1,-k})\exp[-ik]\\=i\sum_k((t-\eta)\lambda_{1,k}\lambda_{2,-k}\exp[-ik]+(t+\eta)\lambda_{1,k}\lambda_{2,-k}\exp[ik])\\=\sum_k(2it\cos k-2\eta\sin k)\lambda_{1,k}\lambda_{2,-k}\\=\sum_{k>0}(2\eta\sin k-2it\cos k)\lambda_{2,k}^\dagger\lambda_{1,k}\\+\sum_{k>0}(2\eta\sin k+2it\cos k)\lambda_{1,k}^\dagger\lambda_{2,k}\\=\sum_{k>0}\left[\begin{matrix}\lambda_{1,k}^\dagger&\lambda_{2,k}^\dagger\end{matrix}\right]\left[\begin{matrix}0&2\eta\sin k+2it\cos k\\2\eta\sin k-2it\cos k&0\end{matrix}\right]\left[\begin{matrix}\lambda_{1,k}\\\lambda_{2,k}\end{matrix}\right]$$

故
$$E_k=2\sqrt{\eta^2\sin^2k+t^2\cos^2k}$$
