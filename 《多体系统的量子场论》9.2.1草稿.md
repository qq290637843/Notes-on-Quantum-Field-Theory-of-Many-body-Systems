## 问题 9.2.2.
1.
拟设为
$$\chi_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}=i\chi_1,\chi_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}=i\chi_1(-)^{i_x},a_0(\pmb i)=0$$

而
$$U_{\pmb i\pmb j}=-i{\rm Im}\chi_{\pmb i\pmb j}\sigma_0+{\rm Re}\eta_{\pmb i\pmb j}\sigma_1-{\rm Im}\eta_{\pmb i\pmb j}\sigma_2+{\rm Re}\chi_{\pmb i\pmb j}\sigma_3$$

所以
$$U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}=-i\chi_1\tau^0,U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}=-i\chi_1(-)^{i_x}\tau^0$$

2.
$$\chi_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}=\chi,\chi_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}=\chi,\eta_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}=\chi,\eta_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}=-\chi,a_0^l=0$$

所以
$$U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}=\chi\tau^1+\chi\tau^3,U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}=-\chi\tau^1+\chi\tau^3$$

3.
注意$U_{\pmb i\pmb j}^\pi$为单位阵，所以$U_{\pmb i\pmb j}^\pi$和任何东西都交换。所以
$$U_{\pmb i\pmb j}'=G_{\pmb i}U_{\pmb i\pmb j}G_{\pmb j}^\dagger=U_{\pmb i\pmb j}G_{\pmb i}G_{\pmb j}^\dagger$$

这时设$G_{\pmb i}=(i\tau^1)^{i_x}(i\tau^3)^{i_y}$，于是
$$U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}'=-i\chi_1(i\tau^1)^{i_x}(i\tau^3)^{i_y}(-i\tau^3)^{i_y}(-i\tau^1)^{i_x+1}\\=-i\chi_1(i\tau^1)^{i_x}(-i\tau^1)^{i_x+1}=-\chi_1\tau^1$$

$$U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}'=-i\chi_1(-)^{i_x}(i\tau^1)^{i_x}(i\tau^3)^{i_y}(-i\tau^3)^{i_y+1}(-i\tau^1)^{i_x}\\=-\chi_1(-)^{i_x}(\tau^1)^{i_x}\tau^3(\tau^1)^{i_x}=-\chi_1(-)^{i_x}(-)^{i_x}\tau^3(\tau^1)^{i_x}(\tau^1)^{i_x}=-\chi_1\tau^3$$

注意设
$$R=\cos[\theta/2]\tau^0-i\sin[\theta/2]\tau^2$$

于是
$$R\tau^1R^\dagger=(\cos[\theta/2]-i\sin[\theta/2]\tau^2)\tau^1(\cos[\theta/2]+i\sin[\theta/2]\tau^2)\\=\cos^2[\theta/2]\tau^1-\sin^2[\theta/2]\tau^1-2\sin[\theta/2]\cos[\theta/2]\tau^3\\=\cos[\theta]\tau^1-\sin[\theta]\tau^3$$

$$R\tau^3R^\dagger=(\cos[\theta/2]-i\sin[\theta/2]\tau^2)\tau^3(\cos[\theta/2]+i\sin[\theta/2]\tau^2)\\=\cos^2[\theta/2]\tau^3-\sin^2[\theta/2]\tau^3+2\sin[\theta/2]\cos[\theta/2]\tau^1\\=\cos[\theta]\tau^3+\sin[\theta]\tau^1$$

所以当$\theta=3\pi/4$，则
$$\tilde U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}=RU_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb x}'R^\dagger=-\chi_1R\tau^1R^\dagger=-\chi_1\cos[\theta]\tau^1+\chi_1\sin[\theta]\tau^3\\=\chi_1\tau^1/\sqrt2+\chi_1\tau^3/\sqrt2$$

$$\tilde U_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}=RU_{\pmb i,\pmb i+\pmb y}'R^\dagger=-\chi_1R\tau^3R^\dagger=-\chi_1\cos[\theta]\tau^3-\chi_1\sin[\theta]\tau^1\\=-\chi_1\tau^1/\sqrt2+\chi_1\tau^3/\sqrt2$$

所以
$$\chi_1=\sqrt2\chi,W_{\pmb i\pmb j}=(\cos[3\pi/8]\tau^0-i\sin[3\pi/8]\tau^2)(i\tau^1)^{i_x}(i\tau^3)^{i_y}$$
